Question 1Grade 7 • RP
A discount store is offering 35% off on all items. What fraction, in simplest form, represents this discount?
A. 7/10
B. 3/5
C. 7/20
D. 35/100
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
35% = 35/100
A percentage represents a part out of 100. So, 35% can be written as the fraction 35/100.
2
GCD(35, 100) = 5 35 ÷ 5 = 7 100 ÷ 5 = 20
To simplify the fraction, find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and the denominator. Both 35 and 100 are divisible by 5. Divide both by 5.
3
Simplified fraction = 7/20
The fraction 7/20 cannot be simplified further, as 7 and 20 have no common divisors other than 1.

Answer: 7/20 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 7 • RP
In a basketball game, a player made 16 out of 25 free throws. What percentage of free throws did the player make?
A. 25%
B. 16%
C. 64%
D. 0.64%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fraction = 16/25
To find the percentage, first express the number of successful free throws as a fraction of the total free throws attempted.
2
16 ÷ 25 = 0.64
Divide the numerator (part) by the denominator (whole) to convert the fraction to a decimal.
3
0.64 × 100 = 64
To convert a decimal to a percentage, multiply the decimal by 100.
4
The player made 64% of their free throws.
The final answer is 64%.

Answer: 64% (Option C)

Question 8Grade 7 • RP

A. 88.2 people
B. 180 people
C. 18 people
D. 196 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Part = 126, Percent = 70%, Whole = ?
We are given a part of the whole (126 people) and the percentage this part represents (70%). We need to find the total capacity, which is the whole.
2
70% = 0.70
Convert the percentage to a decimal by dividing by 100 before using it in calculations.
3
126 = 0.70 × Whole
The relationship is Part = Percent × Whole. Substitute the known values into this equation.
4
Whole = 126 ÷ 0.70
To isolate 'Whole', divide both sides of the equation by 0.70.
5
126 ÷ 0.70 = 180 Total capacity = 180 people
Perform the division to find the total capacity of the theater.

Answer: 180 people (Option B)

Question 9Grade 7 • RP
A small town's population grew by 15% last year. If the current population is 9,430, what was the population before the growth? (Round to the nearest whole number if necessary)
A. 7900
B. 8200
C. 8000
D. 10844.5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original population + 15% growth = 100% + 15% = 115% of original population
If the population grew by 15%, the current population represents 100% (original) plus an additional 15%, totaling 115% of the population before growth.
2
115% = 1.15
Convert the total percentage to a decimal by dividing by 100.
3
Current Population = (1 + Growth Rate) × Original Population 9430 = 1.15 × Original Population
The current population is 1.15 times the original population. Set up an equation to find the original population.
4
Original Population = 9430 ÷ 1.15
To find the original population, divide the current population by the decimal equivalent of (100% + growth rate).
5
9430 ÷ 1.15 = 8200
Perform the division. The population before growth was 8200.

Answer: 8200 (Option B)
