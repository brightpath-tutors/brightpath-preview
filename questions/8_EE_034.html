Question 1Grade 8 • EE
Sophia works at a cafe and earns $12 per hour. If she works 5 hours, how much money will she earn? Evaluate the expression $12h$ when \(h=5\).
A. $7
B. $17
C. $125
D. $60
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
$12 \times 5$
The expression given is $12h$, where \(h\) represents the number of hours worked. We substitute \(h=5\) into the expression.
2
$12 \times 5 = 60$
Multiplying 12 by 5 gives the total earnings.

Answer: $60 (Option D)

Question 8Grade 8 • EE
A city's population increases by 500 people each year. If the current population is 15,000, what will be the population in 4 years? Evaluate the expression $15000 + 500y$ when \(y=4\).
A. 15,500 people
B. 17,000 people
C. 6,000,000 people
D. 15,004 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
$15000 + 500 \times 4$
The expression is $15000 + 500y$. Substitute \(y=4\) into the expression.
2
$15000 + 2000$
According to the order of operations, multiplication should be performed before addition. $500 \times 4 = 2000$.
3
$15000 + 2000 = 17000$
Add 15000 and 2000 to find the total population.

Answer: 17,000 people (Option B)
