Question 1Grade 4 • NF
Sophia is making a special drink for her friends. She has 6 pitchers, and each pitcher holds 1/2 gallon of the drink. What is the total amount of drink Sophia has?
A. 3 gallons
B. 6 gallons
C. 6 1/2 gallons
D. 12 gallons
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Whole number = 6 pitchers, Fraction = 1/2 gallon
Identify the given quantities: the number of pitchers (whole number) and the capacity of each pitcher (fraction).
2
Operation: Multiplication
To find the total amount, we need to multiply the number of pitchers by the amount in each pitcher.
3
Calculation: 6 * 1/2
Set up the multiplication expression.
4
(6 * 1) / 2 = 6 / 2 = 3
Multiply the whole number by the numerator and keep the denominator. Then simplify the fraction.

Answer: 3 gallons (Option A)
