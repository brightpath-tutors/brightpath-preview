A. 300 bacteria
B. 400 bacteria
C. 800 bacteria
D. 150 bacteria
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial amount (a) = 50 Growth factor (b) = 2 (since it doubles)
The problem states the colony starts with 50 bacteria, which is our initial value. It doubles every hour, meaning the growth factor is 2.
2
Number of hours (x) = 3
We need to find the number of bacteria after 3 hours, so the exponent will be 3.
3
y = a b^x y = 50 2^3
The general form of an exponential function is y = a * b^x, where 'a' is the initial value, 'b' is the growth factor, and 'x' is the number of time periods. Substitute the values we identified.
4
2^3 = 2 2 2 = 8
According to the order of operations, exponents are calculated before multiplication.
5
y = 50 * 8 y = 400
Multiply the initial number of bacteria by the calculated growth over 3 hours.
6
400 bacteria
The colony will have 400 bacteria after 3 hours.

Answer: 400 bacteria (Option B)

Question 2Grade 8 • F
An investment grows by 5% each year. If you invest $200, how much will you have after 2 years?
A. $220.50
B. $210.00
C. $220.00
D. $225.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial amount (a) = $200 Growth rate (r) = 5% = 0.05
The initial investment is $200. The growth rate is 5%, which needs to be converted to a decimal by dividing by 100.
2
Growth factor (b) = 1 + r = 1 + 0.05 = 1.05
Since the investment grows, the growth factor is 1 plus the growth rate as a decimal.
3
Number of years (x) = 2
We are looking for the amount after 2 years.
4
y = a b^x y = 200 (1.05)^2
Substitute the initial amount, growth factor, and number of years into the exponential growth formula.
5
(1.05)^2 = 1.05 * 1.05 = 1.1025
Square the growth factor before multiplying.
6
y = 200 * 1.1025 y = 220.5
Multiply the result by the initial investment to find the total amount.
7
$220.50
The amount after 2 years will be $220.50. Money is usually expressed with two decimal places.

Answer: $220.50 (Option A)
