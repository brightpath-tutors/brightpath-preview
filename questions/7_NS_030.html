Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A shopper's bank account started with a balance of $80. After making a purchase, the balance became -$20. On a number line where 0 represents a zero balance, where would you mark the final balance?
A. At the point -20
B. At the point 80
C. At the point -80
D. At the point 20
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The problem states the final balance is -$20.
This is the specific value to be represented on the number line.
2
On a number line, negative values are located to the left of zero. So, -20 is located 20 units to the left of 0.
A negative balance means the account is overdrawn, which is represented by a negative integer.

Answer: At the point -20. (Option A)

Question 2Grade 7 • NS

A. 7
B. -7
C. -3
D. 3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The player's initial score is -5.
This is the starting point on the number line.
2
The score improves by 2 strokes, which means adding 2 to the current score.
An improvement means moving in the positive direction on the number line.
3
-5 + 2 = -3.
On a number line, starting at -5 and moving 2 units to the right (positive direction) results in -3.

BC years are negative when using a number line where 0 is the start of AD.

3
3 years before the second event means -5 - 3 = -8.
Moving 'before' in time means moving in the negative direction on the number line.

-8 corresponds to 8 BC.
A negative integer indicates a BC year.

Digs down 7 feet, so -7.

Question 10Grade 7 • NS
A student is tracking their progress in a video game by points, where 0 is the starting score. They lose 30 points, then gain 15 points, and finally lose 45 points. What is their final score? How far is their final score from a score of 10 points?
A. Final score: -60 points, Distance: 70 units
B. Final score: -45 points, Distance: 55 units
C. Final score: -60 points, Distance: 50 units
D. Final score: -70 points, Distance: 80 units
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Starting score is 0.
This is the origin on the number line.
2
0 - 30 = -30.
Losing points means moving in the negative direction.
3
-30 + 15 = -15.
Gaining points means moving in the positive direction.
4
-15 - 45 = -60.
Another loss means further movement in the negative direction.
5
Distance = |10 - (-60)| = |10 + 60| = |70| = 70.
Distance on a number line is found by taking the absolute value of the difference between the two points.

Answer: Final score: -60 points, Distance: 70 units (Option A)
