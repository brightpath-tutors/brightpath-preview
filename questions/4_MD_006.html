Question 10Grade 4 • MD
An online store processed 36 orders in 1/4 of a day. If it processes orders at a constant rate, how many orders will it process in 3/4 of a day?
A. 72 orders
B. 48 orders
C. 9 orders
D. 108 orders
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Orders in 1/4 day = 36 orders
Identify the number of orders processed in the given fraction of a day.
2
Orders per 1/4 day = 36
This tells us the rate for each 1/4 day segment.
3
Total time = 3/4 day
Identify the total time for which we need to calculate orders.
4
Number of 1/4 day segments in 3/4 day = (3/4) / (1/4) = 3
Determine how many 1/4 day segments are in 3/4 of a day.
5
Total orders = Orders per 1/4 day * Number of segments
Multiply the orders per segment by the number of segments to find the total orders.
6
Total orders = 36 * 3 = 108 orders
Perform the multiplication to get the final count of orders.

Answer: 108 orders (Option D)
