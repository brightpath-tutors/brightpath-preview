Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A store offers a discount of 25%. What is this discount expressed as a fraction in simplest form?
A. 1/4
B. 1/5
C. 2/5
D. 1/2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
25% = 25/100
The term 'percent' means 'per hundred'. So, 25% is equivalent to 25 out of 100.
2
GCD(25, 100) = 25 25 ÷ 25 = 1 100 ÷ 25 = 4 So, 25/100 = 1/4
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their GCD, which is 25, to get the fraction in simplest form.

Answer: 1/4 (Option A)
