Question 2Grade 9 • A-REI
A basketball player has scored 30 points in x games. If he plays 2 more games and scores 10 points in those two games, his average points per game will be 8. The equation for this is (30 + 10) / (x + 2) = 8. How many games (x) had he played initially?
A. 4.75
B. 3
C. 7
D. -1.8
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
(30 + 10) / (x + 2) = 8
Identify the given rational equation.
2
40 / (x + 2) = 8
Simplify the numerator of the fraction.
3
(x + 2) (40 / (x + 2)) = 8 (x + 2)
Multiply both sides by the LCD, which is (x + 2), to eliminate the fraction.
4
40 = 8(x + 2)
Simplify the equation.
5
40 = 8x + 16
Distribute the 8 on the right side of the equation.
6
40 - 16 = 8x => 24 = 8x
Subtract 16 from both sides and simplify to isolate the term with x.
7
x = 3. Check: x + 2 ≠ 0. Since x = 3, 3 + 2 = 5 ≠ 0, so it is a valid solution.
Solve for x and verify that the solution does not make any denominator in the original equation equal to zero.

D. 10
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
120/x - 120/(x+10) = 1
Identify the given rational equation.
2
LCD = x(x+10)
Determine the least common denominator (LCD) of the fractions.
3
x(x+10) (120/x) - x(x+10) (120/(x+10)) = 1 * x(x+10)
Multiply every term in the equation by the LCD to eliminate the denominators.
4
120(x+10) - 120x = x(x+10)
Simplify the equation after multiplying by the LCD.
5
120x + 1200 - 120x = x^2 + 10x
Distribute terms on both sides of the equation.
6
1200 = x^2 + 10x
Combine like terms on the left side.
7
x^2 + 10x - 1200 = 0
Rearrange the equation into standard quadratic form (ax^2 + bx + c = 0).
8
(x + 40)(x - 30) = 0 => x = -40 or x = 30. Speed cannot be negative, so x = 30. Check: x ≠ 0 and x+10 ≠ 0. Since x = 30, it is a valid solution.
Factor the quadratic equation and solve for x. Discard any negative solutions as speed cannot be negative, and verify no denominators become zero.

Identify the given rational equation.
2

3
LCD = 3(x + 5)

4
3(x + 5) ((x - 5) / (x + 5)) = 3(x + 5) (1/3)

3(x - 5) = x + 5

6
3x - 15 = x + 5
Distribute the 3 on the left side.
7
3x - x = 5 + 15 => 2x = 20
Gather x terms on one side and constant terms on the other, then simplify.
8
x = 10. Check: x + 5 ≠ 0. Since x = 10, 10 + 5 = 15 ≠ 0, so it is a valid solution.

Identify the given rational equation.

100 = 5(t^2 + 3t + 2) => 20 = t^2 + 3t + 2
Combine like terms on the left side, then divide both sides by 5.
7
t^2 + 3t - 18 = 0
Rearrange the equation into standard quadratic form (at^2 + bt + c = 0).
8
(t + 6)(t - 3) = 0 => t = -6 or t = 3. Time cannot be negative, so t = 3. Check: t+1 ≠ 0 and t+2 ≠ 0. Since t = 3, it is a valid solution.

Question 10Grade 9 • A-REI
A computer can download a file at a speed of x MB/s. Another computer, older, downloads the same file at (x - 2) MB/s. If the newer computer takes 10 seconds less to download a 300 MB file than the older computer, the equation is 300/(x-2) - 300/x = 10. Find the download speed x of the newer computer in MB/s.
A. 1 - sqrt(61)
B. -1 + sqrt(61)
C. 1 + sqrt(31)
D. 1 + sqrt(61)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
300/(x-2) - 300/x = 10
Identify the given rational equation.
2
LCD = x(x-2)
Determine the least common denominator (LCD) of the fractions.
3
x(x-2) (300/(x-2)) - x(x-2) (300/x) = 10 * x(x-2)
Multiply every term in the equation by the LCD to eliminate the denominators.
4
300x - 300(x-2) = 10x(x-2)
Simplify the equation after multiplying by the LCD.
5
300x - 300x + 600 = 10x^2 - 20x
Distribute terms on both sides of the equation.
6
600 = 10x^2 - 20x
Combine like terms on the left side.
7
x^2 - 2x - 60 = 0
Divide by 10 and rearrange the equation into standard quadratic form (ax^2 + bx + c = 0).
8
x = [2 ± sqrt((-2)^2 - 4 1 (-60))] / (2 * 1) = [2 ± sqrt(4 + 240)] / 2 = [2 ± sqrt(244)] / 2 = 1 ± sqrt(61). Speed must be positive, and x-2 > 0 implies x > 2. So x = 1 + sqrt(61). Check: x ≠ 0 and x-2 ≠ 0. Valid.
Use the quadratic formula to solve for x. Discard the negative solution as speed cannot be negative, and ensure the older computer's speed (x-2) is also positive. Verify no denominators become zero.

Answer: 1 + sqrt(61) (Option D)
