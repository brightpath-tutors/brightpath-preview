Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A store sells small candles for $0.75 each and large candles for $1.50 each. Write an equation in standard form (Ax + By = C, where A, B, and C are integers and A ≥ 0) to represent a total purchase of $24.00 for x small candles and y large candles.
A. 3x + 6y = 96
B. 0.75x + 1.50y = 24.00
C. 75x + 150y = 2400
D. x + 2y = 32
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x = number of small candles Let y = number of large candles Cost of small candles = $0.75x Cost of large candles = $1.50y Total cost = $24.00 Initial equation: 0.75x + 1.50y = 24.00
We define the variables and set up the initial equation based on the given costs and total amount.
2
Multiply the entire equation by 100 to clear the decimals: 100 (0.75x + 1.50y) = 100 24.00 75x + 150y = 2400
To have integer coefficients in standard form, we multiply by the smallest power of 10 that will eliminate all decimal places. In this case, 100 works for both 0.75 and 1.50.
3
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 75, 150, and 2400. GCD(75, 150, 2400) = 75 Divide the entire equation by 75: (75x / 75) + (150y / 75) = (2400 / 75) x + 2y = 32
Divide all terms by their greatest common divisor to get the simplest integer coefficients. The GCD of 75, 150, and 2400 is 75.
4
The equation is x + 2y = 32. A = 1, B = 2, C = 32. A, B, and C are all integers, and A (1) is greater than or equal to 0.
The final equation satisfies all the conditions for standard form (Ax + By = C, where A, B, C are integers and A ≥ 0).

Answer: x + 2y = 32 (Option D)

Question 8Grade 8 • EE
A researcher spends x hours conducting interviews and y hours analyzing data. The time spent analyzing data (y) is 0.75 times the time spent conducting interviews (x), plus an additional 5 hours. Write an equation in standard form (Ax + By = C, where A, B, and C are integers and A ≥ 0).
A. 4x - 3y = 20
B. 3x - 4y = 20
C. 3x - 4y = -20
D. y = 0.75x + 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x = hours conducting interviews Let y = hours analyzing data The time spent analyzing data (y) is 0.75 times the time spent conducting interviews (x), plus an additional 5 hours. Initial equation: y = 0.75x + 5
We define the variables and translate the verbal description directly into a mathematical equation.
2
0.75 can be written as 3/4. So, y = (3/4)x + 5
Converting the decimal to a fraction can simplify the process of eliminating fractions later.
3
Multiply the entire equation by 4 to clear the fraction: 4 y = 4 ((3/4)x + 5) 4y = 3x + 20
To have integer coefficients, we multiply by the least common multiple of the denominators (in this case, 4).
4
Subtract 3x from both sides: 4y - 3x = 20 Rearrange terms to match Ax + By = C format: -3x + 4y = 20
Move the x term to the left side of the equation to start forming the standard form.
5
Since A (-3) is negative, multiply the entire equation by -1: -1 (-3x + 4y) = -1 20 3x - 4y = -20
By convention for standard form, the coefficient A (of the x-term) should be non-negative. If it's negative, multiply the entire equation by -1.
6
The equation is 3x - 4y = -20. A = 3, B = -4, C = -20. A, B, and C are all integers, and A (3) is greater than or equal to 0.
The final equation satisfies all the conditions for standard form (Ax + By = C, where A, B, C are integers and A ≥ 0).

Answer: 3x - 4y = -20 (Option C)
