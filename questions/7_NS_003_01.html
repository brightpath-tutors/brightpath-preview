Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Sarah checks her bank account and sees a balance of -$25. On a number line where $0 is at the center and each tick mark represents $5, where should she locate -$25?
A. At the fifth tick mark to the right of $0.
B. At the fourth tick mark to the left of $0.
C. At the fifth tick mark to the left of $0.
D. At the second tick mark to the left of $0.
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Balance = -$25
Identify the given bank balance. The negative sign indicates a value less than zero.
2
Direction = left of 0
Negative numbers are located to the left of 0 on a number line.
3
Tick mark value = $5
Identify the value each tick mark represents.
4
Number of tick marks = |-25| / 5 = 25 / 5 = 5
To find the number of tick marks, divide the absolute value of the balance by the value of each tick mark.
5
Location = 5th tick mark to the left of $0
Combine the direction and the number of tick marks to determine the exact location.

Answer: At the fifth tick mark to the left of $0. (Option C)
