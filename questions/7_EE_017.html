Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A baker uses 3 batches of dough. Each batch requires 2² cups of flour. How many cups of flour are needed in total?
A. 9 cups
B. 12 cups
C. 18 cups
D. 6 cups
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2² = 2 * 2 = 4 cups
The exponent indicates repeated multiplication of the base number.
2
4 cups/batch * 3 batches = 12 cups
Multiply the flour needed per batch by the total number of batches.

Answer: 12 cups (Option B)

Question 2Grade 7 • EE
A scientist observes a bacterial culture that doubles in size every hour. If she starts with 5 bacteria, how many bacteria will there be after 3 hours?
A. 40 bacteria
B. 60 bacteria
C. 30 bacteria
D. 15 bacteria
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2³ = 2 2 2 = 8
The base (2) is the doubling factor, and the exponent (3) is the number of hours.
2
5 bacteria * 8 = 40 bacteria
Multiply the starting amount by the total growth factor.

Answer: 40 bacteria (Option A)

E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2² = 2 * 2 = 4 miles
Evaluate the exponential expression for Monday's distance.
2

Question 8Grade 7 • EE

E. None of the above

Evaluate the exponential expression for large books.
2
16 books * 2 units/book = 32 units

3³ = 3 3 3 = 27 small books

E. None of the above

1
2³ = 2 2 2 = 8 mph
Evaluate the exponential expression for the first car's speed.
2
8 mph * 3 hours = 24 miles
Multiply speed by time to find the distance.
3
3² = 3 * 3 = 9 mph
Evaluate the exponential expression for the second car's speed.

Question 10Grade 7 • EE
An artist creates a sculpture using building blocks. The base is 3² blocks wide and 2³ blocks long. If each block is 1 cubic inch, what is the area of the base of the sculpture in square inches?
A. 18 square inches
B. 17 square inches
C. 72 square inches
D. 81 square inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
3² = 3 * 3 = 9 blocks
Evaluate the exponential expression for the width.
2
2³ = 2 2 2 = 8 blocks
Evaluate the exponential expression for the length.
3
Area = width length = 9 blocks 8 blocks = 72 square inches
Multiply the width by the length to find the area of the rectangular base. Since each block is 1 cubic inch, the area in blocks is also the area in square inches.

Answer: 72 square inches (Option C)
