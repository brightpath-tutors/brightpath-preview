D. 1600 bacteria
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students

1
200 * 2^3
The expression given is 200 * 2^h, and we are told that h = 3. So we replace 'h' with '3'.
2
2^3 = 2 2 2 = 8
According to the order of operations, exponents are evaluated before multiplication.
3

Multiplying 200 by 8 gives 1600.
4
Total bacteria = 1600 bacteria

E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
5 12 + 20 7

2

According to the order of operations, multiplication is performed before addition.
