Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A new online store projects that its daily sales will triple every month for the first 2 months. If the store's sales started at $10 per day, what will be its daily sales after 2 months?
A. $30
B. $90
C. $50
D. $60
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial sales = $10. Growth factor = 3 (triples). Number of months = 2.
The problem describes exponential growth. The initial value is $10, the base for the exponent is 3 (tripling), and the exponent is 2 (for 2 months).
2
Sales = Initial sales × (Growth factor)^Number of months = $10 × 3^2
To find the sales after 2 months, we multiply the initial sales by the growth factor raised to the power of the number of months.
3
3^2 = 3 × 3 = 9
The exponent 2 means we multiply the base (3) by itself 2 times.
4
$10 × 9 = $90
Multiply the result of the exponentiation by the initial sales amount.

Answer: $90 (Option B)

Question 7Grade 7 • EE

A. 343 square feet
B. 147 square feet
C. 21 square feet
D. 49 square feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Side length = 7 feet. Height = 7 feet. Number of sections = 7.
Each section is a square with side length 7 feet, and there are 7 such sections.
2
Area of one section = side × side = 7 × 7 = 49 square feet.
The area of a square is side length squared.
3
Total area = Area of one section × Number of sections = 49 × 7
Since there are 7 sections, we multiply the area of one section by 7.
4
Total area = 7 × 7 × 7 = 7^3
The total area can be seen as multiplying 7 by itself three times (7 feet length of a section, 7 feet high, 7 sections). This is 7 cubed.
5
7^3 = 7 × 7 × 7 = 49 × 7 = 343
Multiply 7 by itself three times.

Answer: 343 square feet (Option A)
