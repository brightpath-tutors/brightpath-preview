Question 1Grade 7 • RP
A shirt costs $35.00. If there is a 20% discount, how much is the discount in dollars?
A. $7.00
B. $3.50
C. $28.00
D. $700.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
20% = 0.20
To calculate a percentage of a number, first convert the percentage into its decimal equivalent by dividing by 100.
2
$35.00 × 0.20 = $7.00
Multiply the original price by the decimal form of the discount percentage to find the amount of the discount.

Answer: $7.00 (Option A)

Question 8Grade 7 • RP

D. 158.5 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
100% - 8.5% = 91.5%

2

Multiply the total movie runtime by the decimal form of the percentage that is not credits.
