Question 1Grade 7 • RP
A pack of 8 identical pens costs $12.40. What is the unit price of one pen?
A. $1.45
B. $99.20
C. $1.55
D. $0.65
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cost = $12.40 Total items = 8 pens
To find the unit price, we need to determine the cost per single item.
2
$12.40 ÷ 8 = $1.55
Dividing the total cost by the number of pens gives the cost of one pen, which is the unit price.

Answer: $1.55 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 7 • RP
A runner completed 15 kilometers in 90 minutes. What was the runner's average speed in kilometers per minute, in simplest form?
A. 1350 km/minute
B. 6 km/minute
C. 1/5 km/minute
D. 1/6 km/minute
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total distance = 15 km Total time = 90 minutes
To find the average speed (unit rate), we need to determine the distance covered per unit of time.
2
15 km ÷ 90 minutes = 15/90 km/minute
This gives the rate as a fraction that needs to be simplified.
3
15/90 = (15 ÷ 15) / (90 ÷ 15) = 1/6
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor, which is 15.

Answer: 1/6 km/minute (Option D)

Question 4Grade 7 • RP
A car traveled 360 kilometers in 4 hours and 30 minutes. What was its average speed in kilometers per hour?
A. 90 km/hour
B. 83.72 km/hour
C. 0.0125 hours/km
D. 80 km/hour
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
30 minutes ÷ 60 minutes/hour = 0.5 hours
To work with a consistent unit of time (hours), minutes must be converted to a decimal part of an hour.
2
4 hours + 0.5 hours = 4.5 hours
Add the whole hours and the converted decimal hours to get the total travel time.
3
360 km ÷ 4.5 hours = 80 km/hour
Dividing the total distance by the total time gives the average speed in kilometers per hour.

Answer: 80 km/hour (Option D)

E. None of the above

Monthly earning = $3400 Days in month = 30
To find the daily earning (unit rate), we divide the monthly earning by the number of days.

Question 10Grade 7 • RP
A download transferred 1500 megabytes of data in 5 minutes. What was the average download speed in megabytes per second?
A. 5 MB/second
B. 7500 MB·minutes
C. 300 MB/minute
D. 25 MB/second
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total data = 1500 MB Total time = 5 minutes
The desired unit rate is megabytes per second, so the time needs to be in seconds.
2
5 minutes × 60 seconds/minute = 300 seconds
There are 60 seconds in 1 minute, so multiply the number of minutes by 60.
3
1500 MB ÷ 300 seconds = 5 MB/second
This calculation gives the average download speed in the requested unit of megabytes per second.

Answer: 5 MB/second (Option A)
