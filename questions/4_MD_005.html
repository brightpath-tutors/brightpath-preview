Question 1Grade 4 • MD
A math class started at 8:30 AM and lasted for 55 minutes. What time did the math class end?
A. 9:35 AM
B. 8:85 AM
C. 9:05 AM
D. 9:25 AM
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Start time = 8:30 AM, Duration = 55 minutes
Identify the start time and the duration of the math class.
2
8:30 AM + 55 minutes
Add the duration to the start time.
3
Minutes: 30 + 55 = 85 minutes
Add the minutes part of the time.
4
Convert 85 minutes = 1 hour and 25 minutes (since 60 minutes = 1 hour)
Convert the total minutes into hours and remaining minutes.
5
Hours: 8 hours + 1 hour (from converted minutes) = 9 hours
Add the converted hour to the initial hour count.
6
Resulting time = 9 hours and 25 minutes
Combine the total hours and remaining minutes.
7
End time = 9:25 AM
State the final end time, including AM/PM.

Answer: 9:25 AM (Option D)
