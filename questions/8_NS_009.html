A. -$12
B. -$2
C. $12
D. $2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
-$7
Owing money is represented by a negative value.
2
-$5
Borrowing more money increases the debt, so it's another negative value.
3
-7 + (-5)
To find the total debt, we add the two debt amounts.
4
|-7| + |-5| = 7 + 5 = 12 -7 + (-5) = -12

5

The total debt is $12, represented as a negative value.
