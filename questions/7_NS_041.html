Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Sarah had $15.75. She spent $8.50 on a book. How much money does she have left?
A. $7.55
B. $7.20
C. $7.25
D. $24.25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
15.75 - 8.50
To find out how much money Sarah has left, subtract the amount she spent from her initial amount.
2
15.75 - 8.50 ------- 7.25
Align the decimal points and subtract column by column, starting from the rightmost digit. In the hundredths place, 5 - 0 = 5. In the tenths place, 7 - 5 = 2. In the ones place, 5 - 8 requires borrowing from the tens place, making it 15 - 8 = 7. In the tens place, 1 (after borrowing) - 0 = 0 (effectively 1-1 after 8 became 0).

Answer: $7.25 (Option C)

7.5 - 3.2
To find the net change, subtract the yards lost from the yards gained.

7.5 - 3.2 ----- 4.3

Question 3Grade 7 • NS
A recipe calls for 3/4 cup of sugar. If you only have 1/4 cup, how much more sugar do you need? (in simplest form)
A. 2/8 cup
B. 1/2 cup
C. 1 cup
D. 1/4 cup
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
3/4 - 1/4
To find out how much more sugar is needed, subtract the amount you have from the amount the recipe calls for.
2
(3 - 1) / 4 = 2/4
Since the fractions have the same denominator, subtract the numerators and keep the denominator.
3
2/4 = 1/2
Simplify the resulting fraction by dividing both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor, which is 2.

Answer: 1/2 cup (Option B)

Question 4Grade 7 • NS
A submarine is at a depth of -125.5 meters relative to sea level. If it descends another 30.7 meters, what is its new depth?
A. 156.2 meters
B. -94.8 meters
C. -156.2 meters
D. 94.8 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
-125.5 - 30.7
Descending further from a negative depth means we subtract an additional amount, or equivalently, add a negative value to the current depth.
2
-125.5 + (-30.7)
Rewrite the subtraction as adding a negative number. This means we add the absolute values and keep the negative sign.
3
125.5 + 30.7 = 156.2
Add the absolute values of the two numbers.
4
-156.2
Since both numbers were effectively negative (or one negative and subtracting another positive), the result remains negative.

Answer: -156.2 meters (Option C)

Question 5Grade 7 • NS
A student had a score of 87.25 on their first math test. On the second test, their score was 79.5. What is the difference between their two scores?
A. 7.25
B. 7.75
C. 8.75
D. 166.75
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
87.25 - 79.5
To find the difference between the scores, subtract the smaller score from the larger score.
2
87.25 - 79.50 ------- 7.75
Align the decimal points. Add a zero to 79.5 to make it 79.50 for easier subtraction. Subtract column by column, borrowing when necessary. For the hundredths: 5 - 0 = 5. For the tenths: 2 - 5 requires borrowing from the ones place, making it 12 - 5 = 7. For the ones: 6 (after borrowing) - 9 requires borrowing from the tens place, making it 16 - 9 = 7. For the tens: 7 (after borrowing) - 7 = 0.

Answer: 7.75 (Option B)

Question 6Grade 7 • NS
The temperature dropped from 4.8°C to -2.3°C. What is the total change in temperature?
A. -2.5°C
B. 2.5°C
C. -7.1°C
D. 7.1°C
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
4.8 - (-2.3)
To find the total change in temperature, subtract the final temperature from the initial temperature.
2
4.8 + 2.3
Subtracting a negative number is equivalent to adding its positive counterpart.
3
4.8 + 2.3 ----- 7.1
Add the numbers. Align the decimal points and add column by column. In the tenths place, 8 + 3 = 11, so write down 1 and carry over 1. In the ones place, 4 + 2 + 1 (carried over) = 7.

Answer: 7.1°C (Option D)

Question 7Grade 7 • NS
A piece of wood is 5 1/2 feet long. A worker cuts off 1 3/4 feet. How much wood is left? (in simplest form)
A. 3 3/4 feet
B. 4 1/4 feet
C. 3 1/4 feet
D. 4 3/4 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
5 1/2 - 1 3/4
To find out how much wood is left, subtract the length cut off from the initial length.
2
Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions: 5 1/2 = (5 * 2 + 1) / 2 = 11/2 1 3/4 = (1 * 4 + 3) / 4 = 7/4
Convert both mixed numbers to improper fractions to make subtraction easier.
3
Find a common denominator: 11/2 = 22/4
The common denominator for 2 and 4 is 4. Convert 11/2 to an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 4.
4
22/4 - 7/4 = (22 - 7) / 4 = 15/4
Now that both fractions have the same denominator, subtract the numerators and keep the common denominator.
5
15/4 = 3 3/4
Convert the improper fraction back to a mixed number. 15 divided by 4 is 3 with a remainder of 3, so it's 3 and 3/4.

Answer: 3 3/4 feet (Option A)

Question 8Grade 7 • NS
A train's scheduled arrival time is 2:15 PM. Due to delays, it arrives 1 2/3 hours later than scheduled. What is the actual arrival time? (Express your answer in simplest form using hours and minutes)
A. 3:35 PM
B. 4:15 PM
C. 3:55 PM
D. 3:45 PM
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Convert 1 2/3 hours to hours and minutes: 1 hour 2/3 hours * 60 minutes/hour = 40 minutes
First, convert the fractional part of the delay into minutes. There are 60 minutes in an hour, so multiply the fraction by 60.
2
Scheduled time: 2:15 PM Delay: 1 hour 40 minutes
Now we have the delay broken down into hours and minutes.
3
Add hours: 2 + 1 = 3 hours Add minutes: 15 + 40 = 55 minutes
Add the hours and minutes separately to the scheduled arrival time.
4
Actual arrival time: 3:55 PM
Combine the new hours and minutes to get the final arrival time.

Answer: 3:55 PM (Option C)

Question 9Grade 7 • NS
The population of a small town decreased by 3.5% in 2022 and then decreased by an additional 1.25% in 2023 (relative to the original population). What was the total percentage decrease over the two years?
A. 4.85%
B. 2.25%
C. 4.75%
D. 4.50%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
3.5% + 1.25%
When two percentage decreases are relative to the original amount, the total percentage decrease is found by adding the individual percentage decreases.
2
3.50 + 1.25 ------ 4.75
Align the decimal points by adding a zero to 3.5 to make it 3.50. Then add the numbers column by column, starting from the rightmost digit. In the hundredths place: 0 + 5 = 5. In the tenths place: 5 + 2 = 7. In the ones place: 3 + 1 = 4.

Answer: 4.75% (Option C)

Question 10Grade 7 • NS
A patient's weight changed from 180 1/2 pounds to 175 3/4 pounds. What is the total change in weight? (in simplest form)
A. 4 3/4 pounds
B. -5 1/4 pounds
C. 5 1/4 pounds
D. -4 3/4 pounds
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
175 3/4 - 180 1/2
To find the total change in weight, subtract the initial weight from the final weight. A decrease will result in a negative value.
2
Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions: 175 3/4 = (175 * 4 + 3) / 4 = (700 + 3) / 4 = 703/4 180 1/2 = (180 * 2 + 1) / 2 = (360 + 1) / 2 = 361/2
Convert both mixed numbers to improper fractions. This is often easier for subtraction, especially when the first fraction is smaller than the second.
3
Find a common denominator: 361/2 = (361 2) / (2 2) = 722/4
The common denominator for 4 and 2 is 4. Convert 361/2 to an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 4.
4
703/4 - 722/4 = (703 - 722) / 4 = -19/4
Subtract the numerators and keep the common denominator. Since 703 is less than 722, the result is negative.
5
-19/4 = -4 3/4
Convert the improper fraction back to a mixed number. 19 divided by 4 is 4 with a remainder of 3. Keep the negative sign.

Answer: -4 3/4 pounds (Option D)
