Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A construction worker needs to calculate the length of a support cable. The length can be expressed as √18 feet. Simplify this radical expression to find the exact length of the cable.
A. 2√3 feet
B. 3√2 feet
C. 9√2 feet
D. 6 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The factors of 18 are 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18. The perfect square factors are 1 and 9. The largest perfect square factor is 9.
To simplify a radical, we look for the largest perfect square that divides the number under the radical.
2
√18 = √(9 × 2)
Express 18 as a product of its largest perfect square factor and another number.
3
√(9 × 2) = √9 × √2
The square root of a product is equal to the product of the square roots: √(ab) = √a × √b.
4
√9 × √2 = 3 × √2 = 3√2
Calculate the square root of 9, which is 3, and leave the non-perfect square under the radical.

Answer: 3√2 feet (Option B)
