Skill ID: 5.NF.020

Question 1Grade 5 • NF
Sarah has 1/2 of a dollar. She wants to share it equally among 4 friends. How much of a dollar will each friend receive, in simplest form?
A. 8
B. 9/2
C. 2
D. 1/8
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Unit fraction = 1/2 dollar, Whole number = 4 friends.
The problem asks to divide a unit fraction by a whole number.
2
4 = 4/1.
To divide a fraction by a whole number, convert the whole number into a fraction by placing it over 1.
3
(1/2) ÷ (4/1) = (1/2) * (1/4)
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal. The reciprocal of 4/1 is 1/4.
4
(1 1) / (2 4) = 1/8
Multiply the numerators and multiply the denominators.
5
1/8 is in simplest form.
The greatest common divisor of 1 and 8 is 1, so the fraction cannot be simplified further.

Answer: 1/8 (Option D)
