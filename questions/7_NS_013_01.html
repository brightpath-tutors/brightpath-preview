Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A chef used -1/4 cup of oil for a recipe. If they made 2/3 of the recipe, how much oil, in cups, did they use?
A. -3/7 cup
B. -1/6 cup
C. 1/6 cup
D. -2/7 cup
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial oil amount = -1/4 cup, Fraction of recipe made = 2/3
Identify the given values for the oil amount and the fraction of the recipe.
2
Amount of oil used = (-1/4) * (2/3)
Multiply the initial oil amount by the fraction of the recipe made to find the total oil used.
3
Numerator: -1 2 = -2, Denominator: 4 3 = 12
Multiply the numerators and the denominators separately.
4
Result = -2/12
Form the resulting fraction with the new numerator and denominator.
5
Simplify -2/12 by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, 2. (-2 ÷ 2) / (12 ÷ 2) = -1/6
Simplify the fraction to its simplest form.

Answer: -1/6 cup (Option B)

Question 10Grade 7 • NS
During a shopping trip, a customer noticed a discount of -7/8 on an item marked with an additional sale of -4/5 of the discounted price. What was the total discount fraction applied to the original price?
A. 7/10
B. -7/10
C. 28/40
D. 11/13
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
First discount = -7/8, Second discount = -4/5 (of the discounted price)
Identify the two discount fractions given in the problem. Note that a discount reduces the price, so the negative sign indicates this reduction.
2
Total discount fraction = (-7/8) * (-4/5)
To find the total effect of a discount on a discounted price, multiply the two fractions. Multiplying two negative numbers results in a positive number.
3
Cancel common factors: (8 in denominator and 4 in numerator becomes 2 in the denominator).
Simplify the multiplication by canceling out common factors before multiplying.
4
Remaining terms: -7 -1 / 2 5 = 7/10
Multiply the remaining numerators and denominators. Remember that a negative times a negative is a positive.
5
Result = 7/10
Form the resulting fraction. The fraction 7/10 is already in simplest form.

Answer: 7/10 (Option A)
