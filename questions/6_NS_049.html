Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A shopper buys items totaling $24.75. They also return an item for a $12.50 refund and use a coupon for $5.25 off the total. What is the net change in their money from these transactions, indicating money spent as a positive value?
A. $12.25
B. $17.50
C. $7.00
D. $42.50
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial spending = $24.75
This is the amount of money spent initially.
2
Amount after refund = $24.75 - $12.50 = $12.25
A refund reduces the amount of money spent, so we subtract it.
3
Amount after coupon = $12.25 - $5.25 = $7.00
A coupon also reduces the amount spent, so we subtract it from the current total.
4
Net change = $7.00
The final positive value indicates the net amount of money spent.

Answer: $7.00 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 6 • NS

A. 15.8 meters
B. 5.3 meters
C. 9.4 meters
D. 1.2 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Starting at 0 meters.
Sea level is our reference point.
2
Position after first dive = 0 - 8.5 = -8.5 meters
Diving down means decreasing vertical position, so we use a negative value.
3
Position after ascent = -8.5 + 3.2 = -5.3 meters
Ascending means increasing vertical position, so we add a positive value.
4
Final position = -5.3 - 4.1 = -9.4 meters
Diving down again means further decreasing vertical position.
5
Final depth = 9.4 meters
Depth is usually expressed as a positive value representing how far below the surface an object is. Therefore, -9.4 meters means a depth of 9.4 meters.

Answer: 9.4 meters (Option C)

B. 2 cups
C. 3 1/4 cups
D. 3/10 cups
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2.5 = 25/10 = 5/2
It's often easier to multiply fractions by fractions.
2
(3/4) (5/2) = (3 5) / (4 * 2) = 15/8
To find '2.5 times the recipe', we multiply the original amount by 2.5.
3
15/8 = 1 and 7/8
Divide the numerator by the denominator to get the whole number and the remainder over the denominator for the fraction.
4
Total sugar needed = 1 7/8 cups
This is the amount in simplest fractional form.

Answer: 1 7/8 cups (Option A)

Question 4Grade 6 • NS
A car travels at a constant speed of 60.5 miles per hour for 3.5 hours. It then reduces its speed by 15.25 mph for the next 2 hours. What is the total distance traveled?
A. 211.75 miles
B. 332.75 miles
C. 151.5 miles
D. 302.25 miles
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance 1 = Speed 1 × Time 1 = 60.5 mph × 3.5 hours = 211.75 miles
Distance is calculated by multiplying speed by time.
2
New speed = 60.5 mph - 15.25 mph = 45.25 mph
The speed was reduced, so we subtract the reduction from the original speed.
3
Distance 2 = New speed × Time 2 = 45.25 mph × 2 hours = 90.50 miles
Use the new speed and the time for the second leg to find the distance.
4
Total distance = Distance 1 + Distance 2 = 211.75 miles + 90.50 miles = 302.25 miles
Sum the distances from both parts of the journey to get the total.

Answer: 302.25 miles (Option D)

Question 6Grade 6 • NS
A chemical solution initially measures 5/8 liters. After an experiment, 0.3 liters evaporate, and then 1/5 of a liter is added. What is the final volume of the solution in liters, in simplest fractional form?
A. 12/25 liters
B. 1/8 liters
C. 21/40 liters
D. 9/8 liters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial: 5/8 liters Evaporates: 0.3 = 3/10 liters Added: 1/5 liters
To perform operations, all numbers should be in the same format. Fractions are chosen here for the final answer requirement.
2
Common denominator for 8, 10, and 5 is 40. 5/8 = (55)/(85) = 25/40 3/10 = (34)/(104) = 12/40 1/5 = (18)/(58) = 8/40
This allows for straightforward addition and subtraction of the fractional amounts.
3
Starting volume - evaporated amount + added amount 25/40 - 12/40 + 8/40 = (25 - 12 + 8) / 40 = (13 + 8) / 40 = 21/40
Subtract the evaporated amount and add the new amount to the initial volume.
4
Final volume = 21/40 liters
The fraction 21/40 is already in simplest form as 21 and 40 share no common factors other than 1.

Answer: 21/40 liters (Option C)

Question 7Grade 6 • NS
A carpenter needs to cut a piece of wood that is 7 3/16 inches long. From this, they cut off a piece that is 2.5 inches. How much wood is left, in simplest fractional form?
A. 4 11/16 inches
B. 5 5/16 inches
C. 9 11/16 inches
D. 4.68 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial length: 7 3/16 inches Cut piece: 2.5 inches = 2 1/2 inches
Converting 2.5 to a fraction (2 1/2) makes it easier to subtract from a mixed number.
2
2 1/2 = 2 8/16
The common denominator for 16 and 2 is 16.
3
7 3/16 - 2 8/16
We need to subtract 2 8/16 from 7 3/16.
4
Since 3/16 is less than 8/16, we borrow from the whole number: 7 3/16 = 6 + 16/16 + 3/16 = 6 19/16
Borrow 1 (which is 16/16) from the whole number part to make the fraction large enough for subtraction.
5
6 19/16 - 2 8/16 = (6 - 2) + (19/16 - 8/16) = 4 + 11/16 = 4 11/16
Subtract the whole numbers and then the fractions.
6
Wood left = 4 11/16 inches
The fraction 11/16 is in simplest form.

Answer: 4 11/16 inches (Option A)

Question 8Grade 6 • NS
A project manager estimated a task would take 1 3/4 hours. Due to unforeseen issues, the task took 2/3 longer than estimated. How long did the task actually take, in hours, in simplest fractional form?
A. 7/6 hours
B. 2 5/12 hours
C. 7/12 hours
D. 2 11/12 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Estimated time = 1 3/4 hours = (1*4 + 3)/4 = 7/4 hours
This simplifies calculations with other fractions.
2
Amount longer = (2/3) * (7/4) hours = 14/12 hours = 7/6 hours
The task took '2/3 *longer than estimated', so we multiply 2/3 by the estimated time.
3
Actual time = Estimated time + Amount longer = 7/4 hours + 7/6 hours
Since it took 'longer', we add this amount to the original estimate.
4
Common denominator for 4 and 6 is 12. 7/4 = (73)/(43) = 21/12 7/6 = (72)/(62) = 14/12
This allows for direct addition of the fractional parts.
5
Actual time = 21/12 + 14/12 = 35/12 hours 35/12 = 2 and 11/12 hours
Convert the improper fraction to a mixed number for the simplest fractional form.

Answer: 2 11/12 hours (Option D)

Question 9Grade 6 • NS
The population of a small town was 4,800. In one year, it increased by 1/16. The next year, it decreased by 0.05 of its new population. What is the final population after two years?
A. 5,100
B. 4,799.95
C. 4,860
D. 4,845
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Increase amount = 4800 × (1/16) = 300 Population after 1st year = 4800 + 300 = 5100
An increase by 1/16 means adding 1/16 of the original population to itself. Alternatively, multiply by (1 + 1/16).
2
Decrease amount = 5100 × 0.05 = 255 Final population = 5100 - 255 = 4845
The decrease is '0.05 of its new population', so we calculate 5% of the 5100, and then subtract it. Alternatively, multiply by (1 - 0.05).
3
Final population = 4,845
This is the population after both changes have occurred.

Answer: 4,845 (Option D)

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
A data server has 15 3/4 TB of storage. It is currently 0.6 full. If 1/3 of the remaining space is used for backups, how much storage space, in TB, is left for new data, in simplest fractional form?
A. 1 1/20 TB
B. 3 3/20 TB
C. 6 3/10 TB
D. 4 1/5 TB
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total storage = 15 3/4 TB = 15.75 TB
3/4 as a decimal is 0.75, so 15 3/4 is 15.75.
2
Currently full = 0.6 × 15.75 TB = 9.45 TB
0.6 'full' means 60% of the total capacity is used.
3
Remaining space = Total storage - Currently full = 15.75 TB - 9.45 TB = 6.3 TB
Subtract the used space from the total space.
4
Backup space = (1/3) × 6.3 TB = 2.1 TB
1/3 'of the remaining space' means we calculate 1/3 of 6.3 TB.
5
Space left = Remaining space - Backup space = 6.3 TB - 2.1 TB = 4.2 TB
Subtract the backup space from the remaining space to find what's left for new data.
6
4.2 TB = 42/10 TB = 21/5 TB = 4 1/5 TB
Simplify the fraction by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (2), then convert to a mixed number.

Answer: 4 1/5 TB (Option D)
