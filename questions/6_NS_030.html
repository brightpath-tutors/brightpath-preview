Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A shopper checks their bank account and sees a balance of fifteen dollars in debt. On a number line, what integer represents this balance?
A. -15
B. -5
C. 0
D. 15
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
'In debt' means money owed, which is a negative financial state.
When you are in debt, your balance is below zero, indicating a negative value.
2
The amount is fifteen dollars.
The magnitude of the debt is 15.
3
Negative + 15 = -15.
Therefore, fifteen dollars in debt is represented as the integer -15 on a number line, located 15 units to the left of 0.

Answer: -15 (Option A)
