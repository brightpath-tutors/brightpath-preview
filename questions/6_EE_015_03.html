Question 1Grade 6 • EE
Sarah had $15.25 in her piggy bank. After she received some money for her birthday, she now has $28.50. How much money did she receive for her birthday?
A. $12.25
B. $43.75
C. $13.25
D. $13.35
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let 'x' be the amount of money Sarah received for her birthday. The initial amount is $15.25 and the final amount is $28.50. The equation is: 15.25 + x = 28.50
Set up the one-step addition equation representing the problem.
2
To isolate 'x', subtract 15.25 from both sides of the equation: x = 28.50 - 15.25
Apply the inverse operation (subtraction) to solve for the unknown.
3
28.50 - 15.25 = 13.25
Perform the subtraction to find the value of 'x'.
4
x = 13.25
The amount of money Sarah received for her birthday is $13.25.

Answer: $13.25 (Option C)
