Question 1Grade 6 • NS
During a shopping trip, Lisa's bank account has a balance of -$25, while Tom's account has a balance of -$18. Who has the lower balance?
A. Lisa
B. They have the same balance
C. Cannot be determined
D. Tom
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
We need to compare two negative integer balances to determine which is lower. A lower balance means the number is numerically smaller.
In financial contexts, a lower balance signifies a smaller amount of money, which for negative numbers means a value further from zero in the negative direction.
2
Lisa's balance: -25 Tom's balance: -18
The dollar amounts are represented as negative integers because they are balances below zero.
3
-25 is to the left of -18 on a number line. Therefore, -25 < -18.
Numbers decrease in value as you move to the left on a number line. For negative numbers, the one with the larger absolute value is actually smaller.
4
Since -25 is less than -18, Lisa has the lower balance.
Lisa's balance is numerically smaller, indicating a lower amount of money in her account.

Answer: Lisa (Option A)
