D. 10 batches
E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
Total sugar = 5/6 kg, Sugar per batch = 1/12 kg

2
Operation: Division; Expression: (5/6) ÷ (1/12)
To find out how many batches can be made, divide the total sugar by the sugar used per batch.
3
(5/6) * (12/1)
Rewrite the division problem as multiplication by the reciprocal of the second fraction.
4
(5 12) / (6 1) = 60 / 6

10 batches
