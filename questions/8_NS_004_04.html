Skill ID: 8.NS.004.04

Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A shopper calculates that an item's discount is equivalent to a repeating decimal of 0.333... of its original price. Express this decimal as a fraction in simplest form.
A. 3/10
B. 3/1
C. 1/3
D. 3/9
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x = 0.333...
Set the repeating decimal equal to x.
2
10x = 3.333...
Multiply both sides by 10 because one digit repeats.
3
10x - x = 3.333... - 0.333... => 9x = 3
Subtract the original equation from the multiplied equation to eliminate the repeating part.
4
x = 3/9
Solve the linear equation for x.
5
x = 1/3
Simplify the fraction to its simplest form by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, 3.

Answer: 1/3 (Option C)

Question 7Grade 8 • NS

A. 128/99
B. 1 28/99
C. 28/99
D. 1 28/100
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Separate the whole number and the decimal part: 1 + 0.282828...
First, handle the whole number part separately and focus on converting the repeating decimal part to a fraction.
2
Let x = 0.282828...
Set the repeating decimal part equal to x.
3
100x = 28.282828...
Multiply both sides by 100 because two digits repeat.
4
100x - x = 28.282828... - 0.282828... => 99x = 28
Subtract the original equation (from Step 2) from the multiplied equation (from Step 3) to eliminate the repeating part.
5
x = 28/99
Solve the linear equation for x.
6
The fraction 28/99 is in simplest form as 28 (2^2 7) and 99 (3^2 11) share no common factors other than 1.
Simplify the fraction if possible. In this case, it's already in simplest form.
7
1 + 28/99 = 1 28/99
Combine the whole number part with the simplified fraction to get the final mixed number.

Answer: 1 28/99 (Option B)

Question 10Grade 8 • NS
A computer processor's clock cycle is measured to be 0.41666... nanoseconds. Express this precise measurement as a fraction in simplest form for hardware design specifications.
A. 416/1000
B. 375/900
C. 41/90
D. 5/12
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x = 0.41666...
Set the repeating decimal equal to x.
2
100x = 41.666...
Multiply both sides by 100 to move the decimal point just before the repeating part starts.
3
1000x = 416.666...
Multiply both sides by 1000 to move the decimal point past one full repeating cycle.
4
1000x - 100x = 416.666... - 41.666... => 900x = 375
Subtract the equation from Step 2 from the equation in Step 3 to eliminate the repeating part.
5
x = 375/900
Solve the linear equation for x.
6
x = 15/36
Simplify the fraction by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, 25 (375 ÷ 25 = 15, 900 ÷ 25 = 36).
7
x = 5/12
Further simplify the fraction by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, 3 (15 ÷ 3 = 5, 36 ÷ 3 = 12).

Answer: 5/12 (Option D)
