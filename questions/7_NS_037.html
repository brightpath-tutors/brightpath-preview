Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A store lost a total of $75 over 5 days due to damaged goods. If the loss was equally distributed each day, what was the average daily loss in dollars?
A. -15
B. 5
C. -70
D. 15
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total loss = -75 dollars (since it's a loss) Number of days = 5
Represent the total loss as a negative integer and the number of days as a positive integer.
2
Average daily loss = -75 ÷ 5
To find the average daily loss, divide the total loss by the number of days.
3
75 ÷ 5 = 15
First, divide the absolute values of the numbers.
4
A negative number divided by a positive number results in a negative number. So, -75 ÷ 5 = -15.
Apply the rule for dividing integers with different signs.

Answer: -15 (Option A)
