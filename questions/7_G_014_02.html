Question 1Grade 7 • G
In a school art project, two adjacent pieces of wood are cut to form a corner. If the two angles formed are complementary and one angle measures 35 degrees, what is the measure of the other angle?
A. 145 degrees
B. 65 degrees
C. 55 degrees
D. 90 degrees
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x be the unknown angle. Complementary angles sum to 90 degrees.
Identify the relationship between complementary angles and define the unknown variable.
2
35 + x = 90
Set up the equation based on the complementary angle relationship.
3
x = 90 - 35
Isolate the variable by subtracting 35 from both sides of the equation.
4
x = 55
Perform the subtraction to find the value of x.

Answer: 55 degrees (Option C)
