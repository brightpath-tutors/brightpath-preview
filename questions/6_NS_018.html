Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A shopper's bank account has a balance of $0. After making a purchase, the balance changes to -$5. What integer represents the new balance, and where would you position it on a horizontal number line?
A. -5
B. -0.5
C. -10
D. 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = $0, Change in balance = -$5
Identify the initial balance and the change in balance from the problem. A negative sign indicates a decrease.
2
New balance = 0 + (-5) = -5
Add the change in balance to the initial balance to find the new balance.
3
Position -5 on the number line.
Locate the integer -5 on the number line, which is 5 units to the left of 0.

Answer: -5 (Option A)
