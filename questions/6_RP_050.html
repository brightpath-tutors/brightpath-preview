2

Question 8Grade 6 • RP

A. 1:3
B. 14:42
C. 3:1
D. 1:4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Lattes: 42, Cappuccinos: 14
The problem provides the number of lattes and cappuccinos sold.
2
Total hot drinks = Lattes + Cappuccinos = 42 + 14 = 56
To find the ratio of cappuccinos to total hot drinks, we first need to sum the lattes and cappuccinos.
3
Ratio of cappuccinos to total hot drinks = 14:56 Divide both parts by their greatest common factor, 14: 14/14 : 56/14 = 1:4
