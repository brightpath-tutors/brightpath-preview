Score expression: 9^-1 points

2

Question 4Grade 8 • EE

D. 3/16
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Speed expression: (3/4)^-2 miles per minute
Identify the given expression for the train's speed.
2
Apply the negative exponent rule for fractions: (a/b)^-n = (b/a)^n
Recall that to handle a negative exponent on a fraction, you invert the fraction and make the exponent positive.
3

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
A new data storage unit has a capacity described by the expression (2^-4 * 3^2)^-1 gigabytes. What is the total storage capacity in gigabytes?
A. 1/2
B. 9/16
C. 16/9
D. 144
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Capacity expression: (2^-4 * 3^2)^-1
Identify the given expression for the storage capacity.
2
Apply the negative exponent rule to the outer exponent: (ab)^-n = 1/(ab)^n, or (ab)^-n = a^-n b^-n
Distribute the outer negative exponent to each term inside the parentheses, or take the reciprocal of the entire expression.
3
(2^-4)^-1 (3^2)^-1 = 2^(-4 -1) 3^(2 -1)
Apply the power of a power rule ( (a^m)^n = a^(m*n) ) to each term.
4
2^4 * 3^-2
Multiply the exponents for each base.
5
Calculate 2^4 = 16
Evaluate the term with the positive exponent.
6
Apply the negative exponent rule: 3^-2 = 1/3^2 = 1/9
Convert the negative exponent to a positive exponent by taking the reciprocal and evaluate.
7
16 * (1/9) = 16/9
Multiply the results to find the total storage capacity.

Answer: 16/9 (Option C)
