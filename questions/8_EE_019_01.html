Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A small business sells handmade greeting cards. The cost of materials to produce 2 cards is $6. Assuming the cost is proportional to the number of cards, what is the slope of the line representing the cost per card?
A. 0.33
B. 2
C. 3
D. 6
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1 (x1, y1) = (0 cards, $0), Point 2 (x2, y2) = (2 cards, $6)
Identify two points on the line. Since cost is proportional, the line passes through the origin (0,0).
2
Change in y (Δy) = y2 - y1 = $6 - $0 = $6
Calculate the change in cost (Δy).
3
Change in x (Δx) = x2 - x1 = 2 cards - 0 cards = 2 cards
Calculate the change in the number of cards (Δx).
4
Slope (m) = Δy / Δx = $6 / 2 cards = $3 per card
Divide the change in y by the change in x to find the slope.

Answer: 3 (Option C)

Question 4Grade 8 • EE
A car's fuel efficiency is tracked. After driving some distance, the fuel tank went from 15 gallons to 10 gallons, and the car traveled from 300 miles to 500 miles. What is the slope of the line representing the miles per gallon?
A. 20
B. 40
C. 0.025
D. 60
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1 (x1, y1) = (15 gallons, 300 miles), Point 2 (x2, y2) = (10 gallons, 500 miles)
Identify two points (gallons, miles). Note that gallons decreased while miles increased, representing consumption.
2
Change in y (Δy) = y2 - y1 = 500 miles - 300 miles = 200 miles
Calculate the change in distance (Δy).
3
Change in x (Δx) = x2 - x1 = 10 gallons - 15 gallons = -5 gallons
Calculate the change in fuel (Δx). The negative indicates fuel was consumed.
4
Slope (m) = Δy / Δx = 200 miles / (-5 gallons) = -40 miles per gallon
Divide the change in y by the change in x. The negative slope indicates as gallons decrease, miles increase. When reporting fuel efficiency, the absolute value is typically used.
5
Absolute slope = |-40| = 40 miles per gallon
State the slope as a positive value for miles per gallon.

Answer: 40 (Option B)
