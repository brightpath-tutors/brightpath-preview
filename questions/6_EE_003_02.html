B. 3t - 15
C. 15 - 3t
D. 15 - t
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial temperature = 15 degrees Celsius
Identify the starting temperature in the lab.
2
Decrease rate = 3 degrees per hour
Identify how much the temperature decreases each hour.
3
Time passed = t hours
Identify the variable representing the number of hours that have passed.
4
Total decrease = Decrease rate × Time passed = 3 × t = 3t

Final temperature = Initial temperature - Total decrease = 15 - 3t

D. 28 + d
E. None of the above

2
Cost per additional gigabyte = $3
