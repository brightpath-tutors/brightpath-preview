Question 1Grade 5 • NF
Liam bought 8 identical toy cars for $24. What is the cost of one toy car, expressed as a fraction?
A. $8/24
B. $24/8
C. $24 * 8
D. $24 - 8
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cost = $24
Identify the total cost.
2
Number of toy cars = 8
Identify the number of items purchased.
3
Cost per toy car = Total cost / Number of toy cars = $24 / 8
Express the cost of one toy car as a fraction by dividing the total cost by the number of cars.

Answer: $24/8 (Option B)
