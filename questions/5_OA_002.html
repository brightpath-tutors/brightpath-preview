A baker is preparing a special order of cupcakes. He bakes 3 batches of 12 cupcakes each. He then decorates 2 cupcakes from each batch. To find the total number of undecorated cupcakes, evaluate the expression: 3 × (12 - 2).
A. 30 undecorated cupcakes
B. 26 undecorated cupcakes
C. 34 undecorated cupcakes
D. 36 undecorated cupcakes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
12 - 2 = 10
First, evaluate the expression inside the parentheses to find the number of undecorated cupcakes per batch.
2
3 × 10 = 30
Next, multiply the number of undecorated cupcakes per batch by the total number of batches.
3
Total undecorated cupcakes = 30
State the final answer, which is the total number of undecorated cupcakes.

Answer: 30 undecorated cupcakes (Option A)
