Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A pet store has 12 parakeets and 8 canaries. What is the ratio of parakeets to canaries, in simplest form?
A. 3:2
B. 12:8
C. 8:12
D. 12:20
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of parakeets = 12 Number of canaries = 8
The ratio is requested for parakeets to canaries, in that order.
2
Ratio = 12:8
Write the numbers in the order specified by the question (parakeets first, then canaries).
3
To simplify 12:8, find the greatest common factor (GCF) of 12 and 8. Factors of 12: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12 Factors of 8: 1, 2, 4, 8 The GCF is 4. Divide both numbers by 4: 12 ÷ 4 = 3 8 ÷ 4 = 2 Simplified ratio = 3:2
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF to express it in simplest form.

Answer: 3:2 (Option A)

Question 9Grade 6 • RP
In a town with a total population of 2,400 people, there are 600 senior citizens. What is the ratio of senior citizens to the rest of the population (non-senior citizens), in simplest form?
A. 600:2400
B. 1:3
C. 3:1
D. 1:4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total population = 2,400 Senior citizens = 600 Non-senior citizens = Total population - Senior citizens Non-senior citizens = 2,400 - 600 = 1,800
The problem asks for a ratio of senior citizens to the 'rest of the population', so we first need to find the number of non-senior citizens.
2
Senior citizens = 600 Non-senior citizens = 1,800
The ratio is requested for senior citizens to non-senior citizens.
3
Ratio = 600:1,800
Write the numbers in the order specified by the question (senior citizens first, then non-senior citizens).
4
To simplify 600:1,800, find the greatest common factor (GCF) of 600 and 1,800. The GCF is 600. Divide both numbers by 600: 600 ÷ 600 = 1 1,800 ÷ 600 = 3 Simplified ratio = 1:3
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF to express it in simplest form.

Answer: 1:3 (Option B)
