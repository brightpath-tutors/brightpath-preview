Question 18Grade 6 • RP
A scientist observes that a certain bacteria culture doubles its population every 2.5 hours. If the initial population is 500 bacteria, what will be the population after 7.5 hours?
A. 4000 bacteria
B. 2500 bacteria
C. 1500 bacteria
D. 3000 bacteria
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of doubling periods = Total time / Doubling time = 7.5 hours / 2.5 hours = 3 periods
Since the population doubles, we need to find how many times it will double in the given time.
2
Final population = Initial population * 2^(number of doubling periods) Final population = 500 * 2^3 Final population = 500 * 8 Final population = 4000
For each doubling period, the population multiplies by 2. This is an exponential growth problem, specifically a doubling problem. We apply the doubling factor for each period.
3
4000 bacteria
The population will be 4000 bacteria after 7.5 hours.

Answer: 4000 bacteria (Option A)
