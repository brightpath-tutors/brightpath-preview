Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A store offers a promotional discount where customers receive $5 off their total bill for every item they purchase. If a customer buys 6 items, what is the total change in cost to the customer? (Represent a discount as a negative value).
A. 30
B. 11
C. -30
D. -11
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Discount per item: -$5 (since it's a reduction in cost). Number of items: 6.
We assign a negative value to the discount because it represents money being taken off the bill.
2
Total change in cost = Discount per item × Number of items Total change in cost = -5 × 6
To find the total change, we multiply the value of the discount by the quantity of items.
3
-5 × 6 = -30
When multiplying a negative integer by a positive integer, the product is always negative. 5 multiplied by 6 is 30, so -5 multiplied by 6 is -30.
4
The total change in cost is -$30.
A total change of -$30 means the customer's bill is reduced by $30.

Answer: -30 (Option C)
