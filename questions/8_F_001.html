Question 1Grade 8 • F
The cost of a movie ticket is $12. If C represents the total cost and T represents the number of tickets purchased, which equation represents the relationship between C and T?
A. C = 12T
B. T = 12C
C. C = T + 12
D. 12 = C + T
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The total cost (C) depends on the number of tickets (T) purchased. Each ticket costs $12.
This establishes that the cost is a function of the number of tickets.
2
If 1 ticket costs $12, then T tickets cost 12 * T. So, C = 12T.
The total cost is the price per ticket multiplied by the number of tickets.

Answer: C = 12T (Option A)

Question 8Grade 8 • F
The number of minutes, M, a phone app is used per day is 30 minutes plus 5 minutes for every gigabyte of data, G, consumed. Which equation represents this technology usage?
A. G = 5M + 30
B. M = 30G + 5
C. M = 5G + 30
D. M = (30 + 5)G
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fixed usage: 30 minutes. Variable usage: 5 minutes per gigabyte (G).
Break down the problem into a constant term and a term that depends on a variable.
2
The variable part is 5 * G = 5G. The total minutes M is the sum of the fixed and variable parts: M = 30 + 5G or M = 5G + 30.
Construct the equation by adding the constant to the product of the rate and the variable.

Answer: M = 5G + 30 (Option C)
