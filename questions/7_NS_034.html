Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A shopper has $50. They buy an item for $35, then return another item and get $10 back. What is their final balance?
A. $95
B. $75
C. $25
D. $5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance: +$50 Purchase: -$35 Return: +$10
Represent money received or started with as positive integers, and money spent as negative integers.
2
$50 + (-$35) = $15
Combine the initial balance with the amount spent. Since the signs are different, subtract the absolute values (50 - 35 = 15) and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value (which is positive 50).
3
$15 + $10 = $25
Add the amount from the return to the current balance. Both are positive, so add them directly.

Answer: $25 (Option C)
