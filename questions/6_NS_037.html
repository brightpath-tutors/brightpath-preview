Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A store lost a total of $150 over 6 days due to a faulty register. If the loss was equal each day, what was the average daily loss? (Represent loss as a negative value)
A. -$25
B. $156
C. $25
D. -$20
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total loss = -$150 (since it's a loss) Number of days = 6
We represent the loss as a negative integer.
2
Average daily loss = (-$150) / 6
To find the average, we divide the total by the number of units.
3
-150 / 6 = -25
When dividing a negative number by a positive number, the result is negative.

Answer: -$25 (Option A)

-420 / 6 = -70

2

3

-120 / 6 = -20
When dividing a negative number by a positive number, the result is negative.
