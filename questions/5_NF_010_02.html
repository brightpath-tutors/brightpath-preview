Question 1Grade 5 • NF
Sarah spent $15 equally on 4 identical notebooks. What is the cost of one notebook, expressed as a mixed number in simplest form?
A. $3 \frac{1}{3
B. $3 \frac{3}{4
C. $3
D. $4/15
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cost = $15, Number of notebooks = 4
Identify the total amount to be divided and the number of items.
2
Division expression: $15 \div 4$
Set up the division expression to find the cost per notebook.
3
$15 \div 4 = 3$ with a remainder of $3$
Perform the division. The quotient is the whole number part of the mixed number.
4
Fraction part = \(\frac{\text{remainder}}{\text{divisor}} = \frac{3}{4}\)
Form the fraction part using the remainder as the numerator and the divisor as the denominator.
5
Mixed number = $3 \frac{3}{4}$
Combine the whole number and the fraction to form the mixed number.
6
The fraction \(\frac{3}{4}\) is already in simplest form.
Check if the fraction can be simplified. In this case, it cannot, as 3 and 4 share no common factors other than 1.

Answer: $3 \frac{3}{4 (Option B)

Question 5Grade 5 • NF
A teacher distributes 34 colored pencils equally among 6 groups of students. How many colored pencils does each group receive, expressed as a mixed number in simplest form?
A. $5 \frac{1}{4}$ pencils
B. $5 \frac{2}{3}$ pencils
C. $5 \frac{4}{6}$ pencils
D. $6/34$ pencils
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total pencils = 34, Number of groups = 6
Identify the total number of pencils to be distributed and the number of groups.
2
Division expression: $34 \div 6$
Set up the division expression to find the number of pencils per group.
3
$34 \div 6 = 5$ with a remainder of $4$
Perform the division. The quotient is the whole number part of the mixed number.
4
Fraction part = \(\frac{\text{remainder}}{\text{divisor}} = \frac{4}{6}\)
Form the fraction part using the remainder as the numerator and the divisor as the denominator.
5
Simplify the fraction: \(\frac{4}{6} \div \frac{2}{2} = \frac{2}{3}\)

Mixed number = $5 \frac{2}{3}$
Combine the whole number and the simplified fraction to form the mixed number.
