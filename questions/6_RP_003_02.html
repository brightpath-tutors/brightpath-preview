Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A shop sells 8 blue shirts and 12 red shirts. What is the ratio of blue shirts to red shirts, in simplest form?
A. 4 to 6
B. 8 to 20
C. 12 to 8
D. 2 to 3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Blue shirts = 8, Red shirts = 12
Identify the number of blue shirts and red shirts from the problem.
2
Ratio of blue to red = 8 to 12
Write the initial ratio as blue shirts to red shirts.
3
GCF(8, 12) = 4
Find the greatest common factor (GCF) of 8 and 12 to simplify the ratio.
4
8 ÷ 4 = 2, 12 ÷ 4 = 3
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF.
5
Simplified ratio = 2 to 3
State the ratio in its simplest form.

Answer: 2 to 3 (Option D)
