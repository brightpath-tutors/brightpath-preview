Question 2Grade 6 • NS
A scientist measures a liquid to be 0.75 liters. What is this measurement expressed as a fraction in simplest form?
A. 75/100 liters
B. 3/4 liters
C. 7/5 liters
D. 1/3 liters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
0.75 means 75 hundredths, which can be written as 75/100.
The decimal 0.75 occupies the hundredths place, so it can be written as a fraction with a denominator of 100.
2
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD). The GCD of 75 and 100 is 25. 75 ÷ 25 = 3, 100 ÷ 25 = 4. So, 75/100 simplifies to 3/4.
Simplifying the fraction makes it easier to understand and work with.
3
The measurement is 3/4 liters.
Ensure the answer includes the correct unit.

Answer: 3/4 liters (Option B)
