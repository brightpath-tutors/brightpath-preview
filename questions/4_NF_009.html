Question 1Grade 4 • NF
Sarah had 7/8 of a dollar. She spent 3/8 of a dollar on a candy bar. How much of a dollar does she have left, in simplest form?
A. 10/8 dollar
B. 1/2 dollar
C. 4/8 dollar
D. 4/0 dollar
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial amount = 7/8 dollar, Amount spent = 3/8 dollar
Identify the initial amount of money Sarah had and the amount she spent.
2
7/8 - 3/8
To find out how much money Sarah has left, subtract the amount spent from the initial amount. Since the denominators are the same, subtract the numerators.
3
(7 - 3) / 8 = 4/8
Subtract the numerators (7 - 3 = 4) and keep the common denominator (8).
4
4 ÷ 4 / 8 ÷ 4 = 1/2
Simplify the fraction 4/8 by dividing both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor, which is 4.

Answer: 1/2 dollar (Option B)
