Identify the specific lengths and their frequencies from the line plot.
2
Total for 5/8 feet group = 3 * (5/8) = 15/8 feet
Multiply the length by its frequency for the first category.

Convert the mixed number to an improper fraction for easier addition.

Convert the resulting improper fraction to a mixed number and ensure it's in simplest form.

Question 9Grade 5 • MD
A small town's census recorded the fractional portion of a typical month (30 days) that new residents lived in the town before moving again. The line plot shows these durations. Line Plot: Duration of Stay (fraction of a month) 1/3 -- X 1/2 -- XX 2/3 -- XXX What is the average duration of stay, in simplest form, for all new residents shown in the line plot?
A. 5/9 month
B. 1/2 month
C. 1 month
D. 20/6 months
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
List all individual durations: 1/3, 1/2, 1/2, 2/3, 2/3, 2/3
Extract all individual data points from the line plot based on their frequencies.
2
Find a common denominator for 1/3 and 1/2 (LCM is 6): 2/6, 3/6, 3/6, 4/6, 4/6, 4/6
Convert all fractions to equivalent fractions with a common denominator to facilitate addition.
3
Sum all durations: 2/6 + 3/6 + 3/6 + 4/6 + 4/6 + 4/6 = 20/6 months
Add all the converted durations together.
4
Count total number of residents (data points): 1 + 2 + 3 = 6 residents
Sum the frequencies (number of 'X's) to find the total number of data points.
5
Calculate average: (20/6) / 6 = 20/6 * 1/6 = 20/36 months
Divide the total duration by the total number of residents to find the average.
6
Simplify the average: 20/36 = (20 ÷ 4) / (36 ÷ 4) = 5/9 month
Simplify the resulting fraction to its simplest form by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor.

Answer: 1/2 month (Option A)
