Question 8Grade 8 • EE

A. $1.2 \times 10^{-31}$
B. $1.2 \times 10^{15}$
C. $1.2 \times 10^{-184}$
D. $1.2 \times 10^{-15}$
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original decay time: $1.2 \times 10^{-23}$ seconds. Factor of increase: $10^8$.
We need to multiply the original decay time by the factor of increase.
2
\((1.2 \times 10^{-23}) \times (10^8)\)
To find the new decay time, we multiply the given values.
3
$1.2 \times 1 = 1.2$
The coefficient of $10^8$ is implicitly 1. We multiply the non-exponent parts.
4
$10^{-23} \times 10^8 = 10^{(-23 + 8)} = 10^{-15}$
When multiplying powers with the same base, we add their exponents.
5
$1.2 \times 10^{-15}$ seconds
The new decay time in standard form is $1.2 \times 10^{-15}$ seconds.

Answer: $1.2 \times 10^{-15}$ seconds (Option D)

To find the country's contribution, we multiply the given values.
3
$4.55 \times 3.2 = 14.56$
Multiply the non-exponent parts of the scientific notation numbers.
4

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
A fiber optic cable has a core diameter of $8.2 \times 10^{-6}$ meters. If a specialized cleaning tool has a tip width of $4.1 \times 10^{-7}$ meters, how many times wider is the cable's core than the cleaning tool's tip? Give your answer in standard form.
A. $2.0 \times 10^{-1}$
B. $2.0 \times 10^1$
C. $20 \times 10^0$
D. $2.0 \times 10^{-13}$
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cable core diameter: $8.2 \times 10^{-6}$ m. Cleaning tool tip width: $4.1 \times 10^{-7}$ m.
To find how many times wider the cable is, we divide the cable's diameter by the tool's tip width.
2
\((8.2 \times 10^{-6}) / (4.1 \times 10^{-7})\)
We need to divide the measurements.
3
$8.2 / 4.1 = 2.0$
Divide the non-exponent parts of the scientific notation numbers.
4
$10^{-6} / 10^{-7} = 10^{(-6 - (-7))} = 10^{(-6 + 7)} = 10^1$
When dividing powers with the same base, we subtract the exponent of the divisor from the exponent of the dividend.
5
$2.0 \times 10^1$
The cable's core is $2.0 \times 10^1$ times wider than the cleaning tool's tip. The coefficient is already between 1 and 10, so no further adjustment is needed.

Answer: $2.0 \times 10^1$ times wider (Option B)
