Question 1Grade 3 • OA
Sarah bought 2 packs of stickers. Each pack costs $3. How much did Sarah spend in total?
A. 6
B. 3
C. 8
D. 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of packs = 2, Cost per pack = $3
Identify the number of packs and the cost of each pack.
2
Total cost = 2 packs × $3/pack = $6
Multiply the number of packs by the cost per pack to find the total amount spent.
3
Answer: $6
State the final answer with the correct unit.

Answer: 6 (Option A)
