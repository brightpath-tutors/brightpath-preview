Question 1Grade 6 • EE
Emily had some money in her wallet. Her friend paid her $15 back, and now Emily has $40 in total. How much money did Emily have initially?
A. $35
B. $15
C. $55
D. $25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
x + 15 = 40
Represent the unknown initial amount as 'x'. Emily's initial money plus the $15 her friend paid her equals the total $40 she has now.
2
x + 15 - 15 = 40 - 15
To isolate the variable 'x', apply the subtraction property of equality by subtracting 15 from both sides of the equation.
3
x = 25
Perform the subtraction on both sides to find the value of 'x'.

Answer: $25 (Option D)
