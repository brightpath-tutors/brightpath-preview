D. 1200 - (250 * 150 + 40)

🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students

Total students = 250; Students per bus = 40
Identify the total number of students and the capacity of each bus.

Number of buses needed = ceil(250 / 40)

4
Total transportation cost = ceil(250 / 40) * 150

5

C. 13 350 - floor(13 / 5) 25
D. 13 * (350 - 25)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost per printer = $350; Number of printers = 13
Identify the base cost of each printer and the total number of printers bought.
2
Base cost = 13 * 350
Calculate the total cost without any discounts.
3
Discount applied for every 5 printers; Discount amount = $25
Identify the discount rule and amount.
4
Number of discounts = floor(13 / 5)
Calculate how many times the discount applies by dividing the total printers by 5 and rounding down.
5
Total discount = floor(13 / 5) * 25

Total cost = 13 350 - floor(13 / 5) 25
