Question 2Grade 6 • NS
During a gymnastics routine, Sarah completed 0.75 of her flips perfectly. Express this amount as a fraction in simplest form.
A. 75/100
B. 3/4
C. 3/5
D. 7/5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Decimal = 0.75
The problem states 0.75 of the flips were perfect.
2
The decimal 0.75 has digits '75' and two decimal places (hundredths). So, write it as a fraction with 100 as the denominator: 75/100.
The number of decimal places determines the power of 10 in the denominator (e.g., 0.X is X/10, 0.XX is XX/100).
3
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 75 and 100. Both are divisible by 25. Divide both numerator and denominator by 25: 75 ÷ 25 = 3 and 100 ÷ 25 = 4.
To simplify a fraction, divide both the numerator and denominator by their GCD.
4
The simplified fraction is 3/4.
The fraction 3/4 cannot be simplified further.

Answer: 3/4 (Option B)

Question 9Grade 6 • NS

B. 65.5/100
C. 655/1000
D. 655/100
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Percentage = 65.5%
The problem provides the percentage of the population.
2
Divide the percentage by 100: 65.5 ÷ 100 = 0.655.
Dividing by 100 moves the decimal point two places to the left.
3
The decimal 0.655 has three decimal places (thousandths), so write it as 655/1000.
The number of digits after the decimal point indicates the denominator (10, 100, 1000, etc.).
4
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 655 and 1000. Both are divisible by 5. Divide both numerator and denominator by 5: 655 ÷ 5 = 131 and 1000 ÷ 5 = 200.
Always simplify fractions to their simplest form by dividing by their GCD.
5
The simplified fraction is 131/200.
131 is a prime number, and 200 is not a multiple of 131, so the fraction cannot be simplified further.

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
A new computer chip processes 2/3 of its calculations in under a nanosecond. What percentage of calculations is this, rounded to the nearest tenth of a percent?
A. 66.7%
B. 66%
C. 0.667%
D. 66.6%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fraction = 2/3
The problem states 2/3 of calculations are processed quickly.
2
Divide the numerator by the denominator: 2 ÷ 3 = 0.6666... (a repeating decimal).
Division is necessary to convert a fraction to a decimal.
3
Multiply the decimal by 100: 0.6666... × 100 = 66.666...%.
To convert a decimal to a percentage, multiply by 100.
4
The digit in the hundredths place is 6. Since 6 is 5 or greater, round up the tenths digit. So, 66.666...% rounds to 66.7%.
Follow standard rounding rules to the specified precision.

Answer: 66.7% (Option A)
