Question 1Grade 7 • EE
Sarah bought 5 notebooks for $2.50 each and 5 pens for $1.00 each. Which expression uses the Distributive Property to represent the total cost?
A. 5 + $2.50 + $1.00
B. 5 $2.50 $1.00
C. $2.50 + $1.00
D. 5 * ($2.50 + $1.00)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Notebooks: 5, Price per notebook: $2.50. Pens: 5, Price per pen: $1.00.
Sarah bought 5 of each item.
2
Total cost = (5 $2.50) + (5 $1.00)
This represents the cost of notebooks plus the cost of pens.
3
Since 5 is a common factor, we can rewrite the expression as 5 * ($2.50 + $1.00).
The Distributive Property states a(b + c) = ab + ac. Here, a=5, b=$2.50, and c=$1.00.

Answer: 5 * ($2.50 + $1.00) (Option D)
