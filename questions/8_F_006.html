Question 1Grade 8 • F
A delivery service charges a flat fee of $5 plus $2 for every mile driven. Which equation represents the total cost, C, based on the number of miles, m?
A. C = 7m
B. C = 5m + 2
C. C = 2 + 5
D. C = 2m + 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The cost increases by $2 for every mile. So, the slope (m) is 2.
The 'for every mile' indicates a rate, which is the slope of the linear function.
2
The flat fee is $5, which is the cost when 0 miles are driven. So, the y-intercept (b) is 5.
The 'flat fee' is a starting cost, independent of miles, representing the y-intercept.
3
Using the form C = mx + b, substitute m = 2 and b = 5. The equation is C = 2m + 5.
The slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) is used to represent linear relationships, where m is the slope and b is the y-intercept.

Answer: C = 2m + 5 (Option D)

Question 2Grade 8 • F
Maya runs a marathon. After 1 hour, she has run 6 miles. After 2 hours, she has run 12 miles. Assuming she runs at a constant speed and starts from 0 miles, which equation represents the total distance, D, she has run after H hours?
A. D = 1H + 6
B. D = 6H + 6
C. D = 12H
D. D = 6H
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (1 hour, 6 miles) -> (1, 6) Point 2: (2 hours, 12 miles) -> (2, 12)
Extract time (independent variable) and distance (dependent variable) as coordinate pairs.
2
Slope (m) = (change in distance) / (change in hours) = (12 - 6) / (2 - 1) = 6 / 1 = 6.
The slope represents Maya's constant speed in miles per hour.
3
The problem states she 'starts from 0 miles'. So, at H=0, D=0. The y-intercept (b) is 0.
The starting point before any time has passed is the y-intercept.
4
Using D = mH + b, substitute m = 6 and b = 0. The equation is D = 6H + 0, or simply D = 6H.
Combine the calculated slope and y-intercept into the slope-intercept form.

Answer: D = 6H (Option D)

Question 3Grade 8 • F
A recipe states that the oven temperature should be set to 350°F, and it takes 15 minutes to preheat to that temperature. If the temperature, T, increases steadily from room temperature (70°F) to 350°F in 15 minutes, which equation best models the oven's temperature over time, t, in minutes?
A. T = 350 - 15t
B. T = (56/3)t + 70
C. T = 15t + 70
D. T = 350t + 70
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Room temperature is 70°F, which is the temperature at t=0. So, b = 70.
The starting temperature before preheating begins is the y-intercept.
2
Final temperature = 350°F at t = 15 minutes.
These values define the end point of the heating process.
3
Temperature increase = Final temperature - Initial temperature = 350°F - 70°F = 280°F.
This is the 'rise' component for calculating the slope.
4
Slope (m) = (temperature increase) / (time taken) = 280°F / 15 minutes = 56/3 ≈ 18.67°F per minute.
The slope is the rate at which the oven temperature increases per minute.
5
Using T = mt + b, substitute m = 56/3 and b = 70. The equation is T = (56/3)t + 70.
Combine the initial temperature and the rate of change into the slope-intercept form.

Answer: T = (56/3)t + 70 (Option B)

3

4

Question 10Grade 8 • F
A streaming service charges a monthly fee of $12.00, plus an additional $0.05 for every minute of streaming beyond a free allowance. If a customer streams for 180 minutes beyond the free allowance, their total bill is $21.00. Which equation accurately represents the total bill, B, for M minutes of streaming beyond the free allowance?
A. B = 0.05M + 12
B. B = 12M + 0.05
C. B = 12.05M
D. B = 0.05M + 21
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The additional charge is $0.05 for every minute of streaming. So, the slope (m) is 0.05.
The 'for every minute' phrase indicates a per-unit rate, which is the slope.
2
The monthly fee is $12.00, which is charged even if no minutes are streamed beyond the allowance (M=0). So, the y-intercept (b) is 12.
The fixed monthly fee is the cost when the additional streaming minutes are zero.
3
Using B = mM + b, substitute m = 0.05 and b = 12. The equation is B = 0.05M + 12.
Combine the identified per-minute charge and the fixed monthly fee into the slope-intercept form.
4
Given M=180, B=21. Let's check: B = 0.05(180) + 12 = 9 + 12 = 21. The equation is consistent with the given information.
Plugging the given values into the derived equation confirms its accuracy.

Answer: B = 0.05M + 12 (Option A)
