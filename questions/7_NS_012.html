Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Sarah owes her friend $30. If she wants to pay back an equal amount over 5 days, what is the change in her money each day?
A. -$35
B. $6
C. -$5
D. -$6
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total debt = -$30 (since she owes money). Number of days = 5.
Representing a debt as a negative integer is crucial for integer operations.
2
To find the average change per day, divide the total debt by the number of days: -30 ÷ 5.
Division helps distribute the total amount equally over the given number of days.
3
30 ÷ 5 = 6.
First, divide the absolute values of the numbers.
4
When dividing a negative number by a positive number, the result is negative. So, -30 ÷ 5 = -6.
The rule for dividing integers states that if the signs are different, the quotient is negative.
5
The change in her money each day is -$6.
The negative sign indicates a decrease in her money.

Answer: -$6 (Option D)
