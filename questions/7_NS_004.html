Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A checking account has a balance of $15. If a customer makes a withdrawal of $20, what is the new balance?
A. -$5
B. $35
C. -$35
D. $5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = $15. Withdrawal = -$20 (represented as a negative value because it's taken out).
A withdrawal decreases the account balance, so it's represented as a negative number.
2
$15 + (-$20) = -$5
To add a positive and a negative number, find the difference between their absolute values (|20| - |15| = 5). The sign of the result is the same as the sign of the number with the larger absolute value (20 is negative, so the result is negative).

Answer: -$5 (Option A)

Question 8Grade 7 • NS
A construction worker is digging a trench. He starts at ground level (0 feet). He digs down 8 feet, then fills in 3 feet, and then digs down another 10 feet. What is the final depth of the trench relative to ground level?
A. -1 feet
B. -15 feet
C. 15 feet
D. -21 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial depth = 0 feet.
Ground level is the reference point, represented by zero.
2
0 + (-8) = -8 feet.
Digging down means a negative change in depth.
3
-8 + 3 = -5 feet.
Filling in means a positive change in depth (reducing the depth).
4
-5 + (-10) = -15 feet.
Digging down again is another negative change. When adding two negative numbers, add their absolute values and keep the negative sign.

Answer: -15 feet (Option B)
