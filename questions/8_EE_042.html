Question 1Grade 8 • EE
Sarah bought 3 shirts and a pair of pants. Each shirt cost $12. If she spent a total of $50, what was the cost of the pants?
A. $47
B. $14
C. $62
D. $38
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost of 3 shirts = 3 * $12 = $36
Multiply the number of shirts by the cost per shirt.
2
Cost of pants = Total spent - Cost of shirts Cost of pants = $50 - $36 = $14
The remaining amount after buying shirts is the cost of the pants.

Answer: $14 (Option B)

Question 2Grade 8 • EE
A football team scored 7 touchdowns, each worth 6 points, and 3 field goals, each worth 3 points. If they also made 5 extra points, each worth 1 point, what was their total score?
A. 56 points
B. 50 points
C. 48 points
D. 60 points
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Touchdown points = 7 touchdowns * 6 points/touchdown = 42 points
Multiply the number of touchdowns by the points per touchdown.
2
Field goal points = 3 field goals * 3 points/field goal = 9 points
Multiply the number of field goals by the points per field goal.
3
Extra point points = 5 extra points * 1 point/extra point = 5 points
Multiply the number of extra points by the points per extra point.
4
Total score = 42 + 9 + 5 = 56 points
Add the points from touchdowns, field goals, and extra points.

Answer: 56 points (Option A)

C. 3.25 cups
D. 2.25 cups
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students

Butter = Flour + 0.75 cups Butter = 2 + 0.75 = 2.75 cups

Question 4Grade 8 • EE

A. 260 miles
B. 280 miles
C. 100 miles
D. 220 miles
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance 1 = Speed 1 * Time 1 Distance 1 = 60 mph * 3 hours = 180 miles
Multiply speed by time to find the distance traveled in the first segment.
2
Distance 2 = Speed 2 * Time 2 Distance 2 = 40 mph * 2 hours = 80 miles
Multiply speed by time to find the distance traveled in the second segment.
3
Total distance = Distance 1 + Distance 2 Total distance = 180 miles + 80 miles = 260 miles
Add the distances from both segments to get the total distance.

Answer: 260 miles (Option A)

Question 9Grade 8 • EE

A. 1,700 people
B. 1,400 people
C. 1,600 people
D. 1,300 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total increase = 150 people/year * 3 years = 450 people
Multiply the annual increase by the number of years.
2
Population after increase = 1,200 + 450 = 1,650 people
Add the total increase to the starting population.
3
Current population = 1,650 - 50 = 1,600 people

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
An art gallery sold 5 paintings for $350 each. They also sold 3 sculptures for \(X each. The total sales from these items were \)2,350. What was the price of one sculpture?
A. $300
B. $250
C. $200
D. $150
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Painting sales = 5 paintings * $350/painting = $1,750
Multiply the number of paintings by their individual price.
2
Sculpture sales = Total sales - Painting sales Sculpture sales = $2,350 - $1,750 = $600
Subtract painting sales from total sales to find sculpture sales.
3
Price per sculpture = Sculpture sales / Number of sculptures Price per sculpture = $600 / 3 = $200
Divide total sculpture sales by the number of sculptures.

Answer: $200 (Option C)
