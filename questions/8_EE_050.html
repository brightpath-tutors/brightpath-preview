Question 1Grade 8 • EE
The diameter of a certain virus is approximately 0.000000025 meters. Write this measurement in scientific notation.
A. 0.25 x 10^-7 meters
B. 2.5 x 10^-8 meters
C. 25 x 10^-9 meters
D. 2.5 x 10^8 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The non-zero digits are 2 and 5. To form a coefficient between 1 and 10, place the decimal point after the first non-zero digit: 2.5.
The coefficient in scientific notation must be a number greater than or equal to 1 and less than 10.
2
The original number is 0.000000025. To get 2.5, the decimal point must move 8 places to the right.
Count the number of places the decimal point is moved. This number will be the absolute value of the exponent.
3
Since the original number (0.000000025) is less than 1, the exponent will be negative.
If the original number is less than 1, the exponent is negative. If it's greater than 10, the exponent is positive.
4
Coefficient: 2.5, Exponent: -8. Result: 2.5 x 10^-8.
The final scientific notation form is the coefficient multiplied by 10 raised to the determined exponent.

Answer: 2.5 x 10^-8 meters (Option B)

E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1

In scientific notation, the number is expressed as a coefficient multiplied by 10 raised to an exponent.
2

A positive exponent means the standard form is a large number (greater than 10), so the decimal moves right. A negative exponent means the standard form is a small number (less than 1), so the decimal moves left.

The number of places moved is equal to the exponent. Fill any empty spaces with zeros.

The final number is the standard representation of the scientific notation.

Answer: 370,000,000,000,000 kilometers (Option D)

Question 10Grade 8 • EE

A. 1.03 x 10^8 people
B. 17.5 x 10^7 people
C. 1.75 x 10^8 people
D. 1.03 x 10^7 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The first number is 9.5 x 10^7. The second number is 8.0 x 10^6. To make exponents equal, convert 8.0 x 10^6 to a power of 10^7.
To add or subtract numbers in scientific notation, their powers of 10 must match. It's usually easiest to convert the smaller exponent to the larger exponent.
2
To change 10^6 to 10^7, we increase the exponent by 1. To compensate, move the decimal point in the coefficient 1 place to the left: 8.0 -> 0.8. So, 8.0 x 10^6 becomes 0.8 x 10^7.
When increasing the exponent, move the decimal to the left. When decreasing the exponent, move the decimal to the right. The number of places moved is equal to the change in the exponent.
3
9.5 x 10^7 + 0.8 x 10^7 = (9.5 + 0.8) x 10^7 = 10.3 x 10^7
Now that the powers of 10 are the same, add the coefficients directly and keep the common power of 10.
4
The current coefficient is 10.3. Move the decimal point one place to the left to get 1.03.
The coefficient in scientific notation must be a number between 1 (inclusive) and 10 (exclusive).
5
Since the decimal point in the coefficient was moved 1 place to the left, increase the exponent by 1: 7 + 1 = 8.
To maintain the value of the number, if the coefficient is made smaller, the exponent must be made larger by the same number of places.
6
1.03 x 10^8 people
The final answer is the adjusted coefficient multiplied by 10 raised to the adjusted exponent.

Answer: 1.03 x 10^8 people (Option A)
