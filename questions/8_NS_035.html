Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A shopper's bank account had a balance of $ -25. She then made a purchase that deducted $15 from her account. What is her new bank account balance?
A. -10
B. 10
C. 40
D. -40
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = -25. Deduction = 15.
The initial bank account balance is given as -25 dollars, and a purchase of 15 dollars is deducted.
2
New balance = -25 - 15
To find the new balance, subtract the deduction from the initial balance.
3
-25 - 15 = -25 + (-15)
Subtracting a positive number is equivalent to adding its negative counterpart.
4
-25 + (-15) = -40
When adding two negative numbers, add their absolute values (25 + 15 = 40) and keep the negative sign.

Answer: -40 (Option D)
