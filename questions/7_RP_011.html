Question 1Grade 7 • RP
A shirt costs $25. If there is a 20% discount, how much is the discount amount?
A. $5
B. $20
C. $0.20
D. $2.50
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Whole = $25, Percentage = 20%
The original price is the total amount (whole), and 20% is the part of that whole we want to find.
2
20% = 0.20
To perform calculations with percentages, convert them to decimal form by dividing by 100.
3
$25 × 0.20 = $5
Multiplying the original price by the discount percentage (as a decimal) gives the discount amount.

Answer: $5 (Option A)

Question 2Grade 7 • RP
In a basketball game, Leo made 15 out of 20 free throws. What percentage of his free throws did Leo make?
A. 75%
B. 15%
C. 20%
D. 0.75%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Part = 15 (made free throws), Whole = 20 (total free throws)
The part is the number of free throws made, and the whole is the total number of free throws attempted.
2
15 ÷ 20 = 0.75
This division gives the proportion of successful free throws as a decimal.
3
0.75 × 100% = 75%
To express a decimal as a percentage, multiply by 100 and add the percent symbol.

Answer: 75% (Option A)
