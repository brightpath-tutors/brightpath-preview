Question 1Grade 6 • RP
Sarah bought a pack of 12 pencils. She used 4 of them. What fraction of the pencils did she use, in simplest form?
A. 12/4
B. 4/12
C. 8/12
D. 1/3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Part used = 4 pencils, Whole pack = 12 pencils
Identify the number of pencils used (part) and the total number of pencils in the pack (whole).
2
Fraction = Part / Whole = 4 / 12
Express the part as a fraction of the whole by placing the number of used pencils over the total number of pencils.
3
Divide numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD). GCD(4, 12) = 4. 4 ÷ 4 = 1, 12 ÷ 4 = 3.
Simplify the fraction by dividing both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor.
4
Simplified fraction = 1/3
State the fraction in its simplest form.

Answer: 1/3 (Option D)
