Question 5Grade 4 • NF

B. 0.08
C. 0.4
D. 8.0
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial fraction of students with 'A' = 8/20
Form the fraction by placing the number of students with an 'A' over the total number of students.
2
Simplify 8/20 by dividing numerator and denominator by 2: 4/10
To scale the fraction to a denominator of 10, divide both the numerator and denominator by a common factor (20 ÷ 2 = 10, so 8 ÷ 2 = 4).
3
4/10 = 0.4
To convert a fraction with a denominator of 10 to a decimal, place the numerator in the tenths place.
4
Answer: 0.4
State the final answer as a decimal.
