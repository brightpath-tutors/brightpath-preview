Question 1Grade 5 • NF
A store is having a sale where an item is 1/2 off its original price. If you want to express this discount as an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 10, what would it be?
A. 2/10
B. 5/10
C. 1/10
D. 10/20
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original fraction = 1/2
Identify the given fraction.
2
Desired denominator = 10
Identify the target denominator for the equivalent fraction.
3
Factor to multiply denominator by: 10 ÷ 2 = 5
Determine the factor by which the original denominator must be multiplied to get the desired denominator.
4
Multiply numerator and denominator by the factor: (1 × 5) / (2 × 5) = 5/10
To create an equivalent fraction, multiply both the numerator and the denominator by the same factor.

Answer: 5/10 (Option B)
