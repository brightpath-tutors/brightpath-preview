Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A baker sells cookies for $2 each. If she already has $15 from a previous sale, which equation represents the total money (y) she will have after selling (x) cookies?
A. y = 2 - 15x
B. y = 15 - 2x
C. y = 2x + 15
D. y = 15x + 2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial amount = $15
The baker already has $15, which is the starting value or y-intercept.
2
Rate of change = $2 per cookie
For every cookie sold, the money increases by $2. This is the slope.
3
y = mx + b y = 2x + 15
Substitute the slope (m = 2) and the y-intercept (b = 15) into the slope-intercept form.

Answer: y = 2x + 15 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 7 • EE
A marathon runner starts at a pace of 8 miles per hour and decreases their speed by 0.5 miles per hour every 30 minutes. Which equation models the runner's speed (y) after (x) hours?
A. y = -1x + 8
B. y = -0.5x + 8
C. y = 0.5x + 8
D. y = 8x - 0.5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial speed = 8 mph
The starting speed is 8 mph, which is the y-intercept.
2
Decrease = 0.5 mph every 30 minutes Decrease per hour = 0.5 mph * 2 = 1 mph Rate of change = -1 mph per hour
Since the speed decreases every 30 minutes, we need to find the decrease per full hour. 30 minutes is half an hour, so in one hour, the speed decreases by twice 0.5 mph, which is 1 mph. The rate is negative because the speed is decreasing.
3
y = mx + b y = -1x + 8
Substitute the slope (m = -1) and the y-intercept (b = 8) into the slope-intercept form.

Answer: y = -1x + 8 (Option A)

Question 10Grade 7 • EE
A patient's blood pressure starts at 120 mmHg and decreases by 2 mmHg every 15 minutes after receiving medication. Which equation models their blood pressure (y) after (x) hours?

B. y = -8x + 120
C. y = 120x - 8
D. y = -2x + 120
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial blood pressure = 120 mmHg
The starting blood pressure is the y-intercept.
2
Decrease = 2 mmHg every 15 minutes 15 minutes = 1/4 hour Rate of change = -2 mmHg / (1/4 hour) = -8 mmHg per hour
Convert the given rate to change per hour. Since 15 minutes is 1/4 of an hour, the decrease per hour is 4 times the decrease per 15 minutes. The rate is negative because the blood pressure is decreasing.
3
y = mx + b y = -8x + 120
Substitute the slope (m = -8) and the y-intercept (b = 120) into the slope-intercept form.

Answer: y = -8x + 120 (Option B)
