Question 1Grade 7 • RP
If 3 pencils cost $0.75, how much would 7 pencils cost?
A. $5.25
B. $0.32
C. $2.25
D. $1.75
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
3 pencils / $0.75 = 7 pencils / x
We assume the cost is directly proportional to the number of pencils. Let x be the cost of 7 pencils.
2
3 x = 0.75 7
Multiply the numerator of the first ratio by the denominator of the second, and vice-versa.
3
3x = 5.25
Calculate the product of 0.75 and 7.
4
x = 5.25 / 3
Isolate x by performing the division.
5
x = 1.75
The cost of 7 pencils is $1.75.

Answer: $1.75 (Option D)
