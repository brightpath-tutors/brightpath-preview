We know that 1² = 1 and 2² = 4. Therefore, √2 is between 1 and 2.
Find the perfect squares closest to 2 to narrow down the range.
3
Let's test values: 1.4² = 1.96 1.5² = 2.25 Since 1.96 is closer to 2 (difference of 0.04) than 2.25 (difference of 0.25), √2 is closer to 1.4.

4
√2 ≈ 1.414... Rounding 1.414... to the nearest tenth gives 1.4.
