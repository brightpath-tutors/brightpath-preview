Divide Mixed Numbers

Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A baker has 5 1/4 pounds of dough. If each loaf of bread requires 1 3/4 pounds of dough, how many loaves can the baker make?
A. 5 loaves
B. 3 loaves
C. 2 loaves
D. 4 loaves
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
5 1/4 = (5 4 + 1) / 4 = 21/4 1 3/4 = (1 4 + 3) / 4 = 7/4
To divide mixed numbers, first convert them into improper fractions. Multiply the whole number by the denominator and add the numerator. Keep the same denominator.
2
21/4 ÷ 7/4 = 21/4 * 4/7
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal. The reciprocal of a/b is b/a.
3
(21 4) / (4 7) = 84 / 28
Multiply the numerators together and the denominators together.
4
84 / 28 = 3
Divide the numerator by the denominator to get the whole number of loaves.

Answer: 3 loaves (Option B)

Question 2Grade 6 • NS
A marathon runner completed a 26 1/5-mile race, and their average speed was 5 1/2 miles per hour. How long did it take the runner to finish the race, in simplest form?
A. 4 42/55 hours
B. 2 3/10 hours
C. 5 1/10 hours
D. 6 1/2 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
26 1/5 = (26 5 + 1) / 5 = 131/5 5 1/2 = (5 2 + 1) / 2 = 11/2
To find the time, we divide the total distance by the average speed. First, convert the mixed numbers to improper fractions.
2
131/5 ÷ 11/2 = 131/5 * 2/11
Dividing by a fraction is equivalent to multiplying by its reciprocal.
3
(131 2) / (5 11) = 262 / 55
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4
262 ÷ 55 = 4 with a remainder of 42 So, 262/55 = 4 42/55. This fraction cannot be simplified further.
Divide the numerator by the denominator. The quotient is the whole number, and the remainder over the original denominator forms the fraction.

Answer: 4 4/55 hours (Option A)

Question 3Grade 6 • NS
A chef has 18 3/4 cups of chicken broth. If each recipe calls for 2 1/2 cups of broth, how many recipes can the chef make, in simplest form?
A. 8 recipes
B. 7 1/2 recipes
C. 6 3/4 recipes
D. 9 recipes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
18 3/4 = (18 4 + 3) / 4 = 75/4 2 1/2 = (2 2 + 1) / 2 = 5/2
First, convert both mixed numbers into improper fractions to make division easier.
2
75/4 ÷ 5/2 = 75/4 * 2/5
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal. Flip the second fraction.
3
(75 2) / (4 5) = 150 / 20
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4
150 / 20 = 15/2 = 7 1/2
Simplify the fraction by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (10), then convert the improper fraction to a mixed number.

Answer: 7 1/2 recipes (Option B)

D. 8 pieces
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
14 2/3 = (14 3 + 2) / 3 = 44/3 2 1/4 = (2 4 + 1) / 4 = 9/4
Convert both mixed numbers to improper fractions to prepare for division.
2
44/3 ÷ 9/4 = 44/3 * 4/9
To divide by a fraction, multiply by its reciprocal.
3
(44 4) / (3 9) = 176 / 27
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4

Question 8Grade 6 • NS
A small town has a total area of 28 3/4 square miles. If the average population density is 6 1/4 people per square mile, what is the approximate population of the town, rounded to the nearest whole number?
A. 6 people
B. 5 people
C. 7 people
D. 4 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
28 3/4 = (28 4 + 3) / 4 = 115/4 6 1/4 = (6 4 + 1) / 4 = 25/4
To find the population, divide the total area by the population density. Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions.
2
115/4 ÷ 25/4 = 115/4 * 4/25
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal.
3
(115 4) / (4 25) = 460 / 100
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4
460 / 100 = 46/10 = 4.6 Rounding 4.6 to the nearest whole number gives 5.
Simplify the fraction and then convert to a decimal. Round the decimal to the nearest whole number as requested.

Answer: 5 people (Option B)

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
A company produces microchips, and a batch of 42 1/3 ounces of silicon is used. If each microchip requires 2 2/3 ounces of silicon, how many microchips can be produced from this batch, in simplest form?
A. 14 1/3 microchips
B. 15 7/8 microchips
C. 17 microchips
D. 16 1/4 microchips
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
42 1/3 = (42 3 + 1) / 3 = 127/3 2 2/3 = (2 3 + 2) / 3 = 8/3
Convert both mixed numbers to improper fractions before dividing.
2
127/3 ÷ 8/3 = 127/3 * 3/8
To divide by a fraction, multiply by its reciprocal.
3
(127 3) / (3 8) = 381 / 24
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4
381 / 24 = 127/8 (divide by 3) 127 ÷ 8 = 15 with a remainder of 7 So, 127/8 = 15 7/8.
Simplify the fraction by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (3). Then convert the improper fraction to a mixed number.

Answer: 15 7/8 microchips (Option B)
