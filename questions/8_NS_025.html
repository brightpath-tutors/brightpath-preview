Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A shopper is tallying their purchases. They bought a book for $12.50, a snack for $7.25, and a drink for $3.00. Which expression correctly uses the commutative property of addition to show a different order of adding these amounts?
A. 12.50 + 7.25 * 3.00
B. 7.25 + 12.50 + 3.00
C. 12.50 - 7.25 + 3.00
D. (12.50 + 7.25) + 3.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original expression: 12.50 + 7.25 + 3.00
The problem presents the amounts in a specific order.
2
a + b = b + a
This property states that changing the order of the addends does not change the sum. We need to find an option that rearranges the order of the numbers being added.
3
Possible rearrangement: 7.25 + 12.50 + 3.00
This new expression shows the same numbers added in a different order, demonstrating the commutative property.

Answer: 7.25 + 12.50 + 3.00 (Option B)

Question 8Grade 8 • NS
A technician works 8 and 1/3 hours per day for 5 days a week. For 2 of those days, they also work an extra 1/2 hour each day. Which expression correctly uses the distributive property to calculate the total hours worked in a week?
A. 5 * (8 + 1/3 + 1/2)
B. (5 8) + (1/3 2) + (1/2 * 2)
C. 5 8 + 5 (1/3) + 2 * (1/2)
D. 5 8 + 1/3 + 2 1/2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Normal daily work: 8 hours + 1/3 hour. Extra work on 2 specific days: 1/2 hour per day.
The total work involves a regular component over 5 days and an additional component over 2 of those days.
2
Total hours = 5 (8 + 1/3) + 2 (1/2)
The 5 days of regular work are represented by 5 times the sum of 8 and 1/3. The 2 days of extra work are 2 times 1/2. These two totals are added.
3
5 (8 + 1/3) = 5 8 + 5 * (1/3)
Distribute the multiplication by 5 to both the whole number and the fractional part of the mixed number.
4
Final expression: 5 8 + 5 (1/3) + 2 * (1/2)
This shows the result of applying the distributive property to the daily work component, combined with the extra hours.

Answer: 5 8 + 5 (1/3) + 2 * (1/2) (Option C)

Express your answer as a simplified fraction.

B. 3/2
C. 2/3
D. 1/5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial Population Growth Factor Decrease Factor = Initial Population
If a quantity returns to its original value after being multiplied by a factor, it must then be multiplied by the multiplicative inverse of that factor.
2
Growth Factor = 1.5
This is the number by which the population initially multiplied.
3
Growth Factor Decrease Factor = 1 1.5 Decrease Factor = 1
For the population to return to its original size, the product of the growth factor and the decrease factor must be 1.
4
Decrease Factor = 1 / 1.5
The decrease factor is the multiplicative inverse of the growth factor.
5
1.5 = 3/2 Decrease Factor = 1 / (3/2) = 2/3
To express the answer as a simplified fraction, convert 1.5 to 3/2 and then find its reciprocal.

Question 10Grade 8 • NS
A software developer is optimizing a calculation. The original formula is (15 (x + 0.5)) + (y 15). Using the properties of real numbers, rewrite this expression in a simpler form and then calculate the value if x = 3.2 and y = 4.8.
A. 127.5
B. 125
C. 112.5
D. 135
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original: (15 (x + 0.5)) + (y 15)
This is the initial formula provided.
2
15x + 15 0.5 + (y 15)
Distribute 15 to both 'x' and '0.5' inside the first set of parentheses.
3
15x + 15 * 0.5 + 15y
Change the order of multiplication in the term 'y * 15' to '15y' to prepare for further simplification.
4
15x + 15y + 15 * 0.5
Rearrange the terms to group the 'x' and 'y' terms together, which facilitates factoring.
5
15(x + y) + 15 * 0.5
Factor out the common factor of 15 from 15x and 15y.
6
x = 3.2, y = 4.8 15(3.2 + 4.8) + 15 * 0.5
Replace 'x' and 'y' with their numerical values.
7
15(8) + 7.5 = 120 + 7.5 = 127.5
Follow the order of operations: first, add inside the parentheses, then multiply, and finally add the results.

Answer: 127.5 (Option A)
