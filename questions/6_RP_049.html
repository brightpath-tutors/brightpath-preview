Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A store is having a sale. A shirt originally costs $28 and is on sale for $21. What is the ratio of the sale price to the original price, in simplest form?
A. 21:28
B. 1:3
C. 4:3
D. 3:4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Sale price = $21 Original price = $28
The problem asks for the ratio of the sale price to the original price, so these are the two quantities we need.
2
Ratio = 21:28
Place the sale price first and the original price second, as requested by the prompt.
3
Factors of 21: 1, 3, 7, 21 Factors of 28: 1, 2, 4, 7, 14, 28 GCF(21, 28) = 7
To simplify a ratio, divide both numbers by their greatest common factor.
4
21 ÷ 7 = 3 28 ÷ 7 = 4
Dividing both numbers by 7 gives us the simplified components of the ratio.
5
Simplified ratio = 3:4
The ratio of the sale price to the original price in simplest form is 3:4.

Answer: 3:4 (Option D)

Question 7Grade 6 • RP
A concrete mixture requires 3 bags of cement for every 7 bags of sand. If a builder uses 21 bags of cement, how many bags of sand are needed? What is the new ratio of cement to sand, in simplest form?
A. 7:3
B. 1:3
C. 21:49
D. 3:7
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial ratio (cement:sand) = 3:7 New amount of cement = 21 bags
We are given the base ratio and a new quantity for one part of the ratio.
2
Scaling factor = New cement ÷ Original cement = 21 ÷ 3 = 7
To find out how many times the recipe has been scaled up, divide the new amount of cement by the original amount of cement from the ratio.
3
New sand = Original sand × Scaling factor = 7 bags × 7 = 49 bags of sand
Multiply the original amount of sand by the same scaling factor to maintain the correct proportion.
4
New ratio = 21:49
The new amounts are 21 bags of cement and 49 bags of sand.
5
Factors of 21: 1, 3, 7, 21 Factors of 49: 1, 7, 49 GCF(21, 49) = 7 21 ÷ 7 = 3 49 ÷ 7 = 7
Divide both parts of the new ratio by their greatest common factor, which is 7.
6
Simplified ratio = 3:7
The new ratio of cement to sand, in simplest form, is 3:7. This is the same as the original ratio, as expected for equivalent ratios.

Answer: 3:7 (Option D)

Question 9Grade 6 • RP
In a small town, the ratio of adults to children is 5:2. If there are 1,400 people in the town, how many children are there? What is the ratio of children to the total population, in simplest form?
A. 5:7
B. 400:1400
C. 2:5
D. 2:7
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Adults:Children = 5:2 Total parts = 5 (adults) + 2 (children) = 7 parts
The ratio represents parts of a whole. To find the total number of parts that make up the whole population, add the ratio components.
2
Total population = 1,400 people Value per part = Total population ÷ Total parts = 1400 ÷ 7 = 200 people/part
Divide the total population by the total number of ratio parts to find out how many people each 'part' of the ratio represents.
3
Number of children = Children's parts × Value per part = 2 × 200 = 400 children
Multiply the number of parts for children by the value of one part to find the total number of children.
4
Ratio = 400 (children) : 1400 (total population)
The problem asks for the ratio of children to the total population. We now have both values.
5
Divide both numbers by 100: 400 ÷ 100 = 4, 1400 ÷ 100 = 14 New ratio = 4:14
Since both numbers are multiples of 100, we can divide by 100 to simplify.
6
Factors of 4: 1, 2, 4 Factors of 14: 1, 2, 7, 14 GCF(4, 14) = 2
Find the GCF of 4 and 14 to further simplify the ratio.
7
4 ÷ 2 = 2 14 ÷ 2 = 7
Dividing both numbers by 2 gives us the simplified components of the ratio.
8
Simplified ratio = 2:7
The ratio of children to the total population in simplest form is 2:7.

Answer: 2:7 (Option D)
