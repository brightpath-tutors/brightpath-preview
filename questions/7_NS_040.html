Question 4Grade 7 • NS
A submarine descended 15.3 meters, then ascended 8.7 meters. What is the submarine's final change in depth relative to its starting point?
A. -6.6 meters
B. 24.0 meters
C. -24.0 meters
D. 6.6 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Descent of 15.3 meters = -15.3 Ascent of 8.7 meters = +8.7
Descent means moving downwards, so it's represented by a negative number. Ascent means moving upwards, so it's a positive number.
2
-15.3 + 8.7
To find the final change in depth, add the two values.
3
Since the numbers have different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value: 15.3 - 8.7 = 6.6. Since |-15.3| > |8.7|, the sum will be negative.
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract their absolute values. The sum takes the sign of the number with the larger absolute value.
4
Final change in depth = -6.6 meters
The submarine's final change in depth is -6.6 meters, meaning it is 6.6 meters below its starting point.

Answer: -6.6 meters (Option A)

Question 5Grade 7 • NS
During a science experiment, the temperature of a solution started at -4 1/2 degrees Celsius. It then increased by 6 1/4 degrees Celsius. What is the final temperature of the solution, in simplest form?
A. 1 1/4 degrees Celsius
B. -1 3/4 degrees Celsius
C. 2 1/4 degrees Celsius
D. 1 3/4 degrees Celsius
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial temperature = -4 1/2 °C Increase = +6 1/4 °C
A decrease is negative, an increase is positive. We need to add these values.
2
-4 1/2 = -(4*2 + 1)/2 = -9/2 6 1/4 = (6*4 + 1)/4 = 25/4
Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions to make addition easier: multiply the whole number by the denominator and add the numerator. Keep the original sign.
3
The least common multiple of 2 and 4 is 4.
To add fractions, they must have the same denominator.
4
-9/2 * 2/2 = -18/4 25/4 remains 25/4
Multiply the numerator and denominator of -9/2 by 2 to get an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 4.
5
-18/4 + 25/4 = (-18 + 25)/4 = 7/4
Add the numerators and keep the common denominator. When adding integers with different signs, subtract their absolute values and keep the sign of the larger absolute value.
6
7/4 = 1 with a remainder of 3 = 1 3/4
Divide the numerator by the denominator. The quotient is the whole number, and the remainder becomes the new numerator over the original denominator. The fraction 3/4 is in simplest form.

Answer: 1 3/4 degrees Celsius (Option D)

Total change in volume = 0.55 liters

E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
3 + 8 = 11
First, add the whole number portions of the mixed numbers.
2
5/6 + 1/3
Next, add the fractional portions. They have different denominators, so we need a common one.
3
The least common multiple of 6 and 3 is 6.
The smallest common denominator for 6 and 3 is 6.
4
1/3 * 2/2 = 2/6
Convert 1/3 to an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 6 by multiplying the numerator and denominator by 2.

B. -1 9/10 hours
C. 6 1/10 hours
D. 1 9/10 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial time relative to noon = -2 3/5 hours Task duration = +4 1/2 hours
Before noon is negative, duration is positive. We need to add these values.
2
-2 3/5 = -(2*5 + 3)/5 = -13/5 4 1/2 = (4*2 + 1)/2 = 9/2
Converting to improper fractions makes finding a common denominator and adding easier.
3
The least common multiple of 5 and 2 is 10.
To add fractions, they must have the same denominator.
4
-13/5 * 2/2 = -26/10 9/2 * 5/5 = 45/10
Multiply the numerator and denominator of each fraction by the appropriate factor to get the common denominator.
5
-26/10 + 45/10 = (-26 + 45)/10 = 19/10

19/10 = 1 with a remainder of 9 = 1 9/10
Divide the numerator by the denominator. The quotient is the whole number part, and the remainder is the new numerator. The fraction 9/10 is in simplest form.

B. -10.875

D. 10.875
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial value = -7.125 Value added = 3 3/4
We need to add these two rational numbers.
2
3 3/4 = 3 + 3/4 = 3 + 0.75 = 3.75

3
-7.125 + 3.75
Now we have two decimals to add, one negative and one positive.
4
Since the numbers have different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value: 7.125 - 3.75 = 3.375. Since |-7.125| > |3.75|, the sum will be negative.
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract their absolute values. The sum takes the sign of the number with the larger absolute value.
5
Final value = -3.375
The final value after the second module's calculation is -3.375.
