D. $18,000
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
P = $20,000 r = 10% = 0.10 t = 1 year
The initial value is the purchase price of the car. The decay rate is given as a percentage, which must be converted to a decimal. The time is 1 year.
2
A = 20000 * (1 - 0.10)^1
Substitute the identified values into the formula.
3
1 - 0.10 = 0.90
The decay factor represents the percentage of the value remaining after each period.
4
A = 20000 (0.90)^1 A = 20000 0.90 A = 18000
Multiply the initial value by the decay factor raised to the power of the time period.

Answer: $18,000 (Option D)
