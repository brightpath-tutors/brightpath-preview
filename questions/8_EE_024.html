Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A grocery store sells individual bags of pretzels. If 2 bags cost $3 and 5 bags cost $7.50, what is the slope that represents the change in cost per bag?
A. $4.50/bag
B. $0.67/bag
C. $3.00/bag
D. $1.50/bag
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (number of bags, cost) = (2, $3) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (number of bags, cost) = (5, $7.50)
The problem provides two data points where the number of bags is the independent variable (x) and the cost is the dependent variable (y).
2
Change in cost = y2 - y1 = $7.50 - $3.00 = $4.50
To find the 'rise', subtract the first cost from the second cost.
3
Change in bags = x2 - x1 = 5 - 2 = 3 bags
To find the 'run', subtract the first number of bags from the second number of bags.
4
Slope (m) = Change in y / Change in x = $4.50 / 3 bags = $1.50/bag
The slope is the ratio of the change in y to the change in x. This represents the cost per bag.

Answer: $1.50/bag (Option D)
