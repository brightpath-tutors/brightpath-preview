Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Maria has a checking account balance of $25. She makes a purchase of $30 using her debit card. What is her new account balance?
A. -$5
B. -$55
C. $5
D. $55
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = $25 (positive value)
A positive value represents money Maria has in her account.
2
Purchase = $30 (negative change)
A purchase reduces the account balance, so it's represented as a negative change.
3
New balance = 25 + (-30)
To find the new balance, we add the initial balance and the change.
4
25 + (-30) = -5
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract their absolute values (30 - 25 = 5) and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Since |-30| is greater than |25|, the result is negative.

Answer: -$5 (Option A)

B. -14°F
C. -6°F
D. 6°F
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Target temperature = -10°F
This is the desired final temperature.
2
Current temperature = -4°F
This is the starting temperature.
3
Change needed = Target temperature - Current temperature = -10 - (-4)
To find the change, we subtract the current temperature from the target temperature.
4
-10 - (-4) = -10 + 4
Subtracting a negative number is the same as adding its positive counterpart.
5
-10 + 4 = -6
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract their absolute values (10 - 4 = 6) and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Since |-10| is greater than |4|, the result is negative.

Answer: -6°F (Option C)
