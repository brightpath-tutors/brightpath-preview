Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A new collector's coin has a perfectly circular shape with a radius of 2 cm. What is the exact area of the coin?
A. 8 cm²
B. 2π cm²
C. 4π cm²
D. 12.56 cm²
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
A = πr²
The area of a circle is calculated by multiplying pi (π) by the square of its radius (r).
2
A = π(2 cm)²
The radius of the coin is 2 cm. Substitute this value for 'r' in the formula.
3
A = π(4 cm²) = 4π cm²
Squaring 2 cm gives 4 cm². The exact area is 4π cm². Since π is an irrational number, 4π is also an irrational number.

Answer: 4π cm² (Option C)

Question 2Grade 7 • NS
A square baseball field has sides of 90 feet. What is the exact length of the diagonal from home plate to second base?
A. 127.28 feet
B. 90√2 feet
C. 90 feet
D. 180 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The diagonal forms the hypotenuse of a right-angled triangle, with the sides of the square as the legs (a = 90, b = 90).
In a square, the diagonal cuts it into two right-angled isosceles triangles.
2
a² + b² = c² => 90² + 90² = c²
The Pythagorean theorem states that in a right-angled triangle, the square of the hypotenuse (c) is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides (a and b).
3
8100 + 8100 = c² => 16200 = c²
Square 90 (90*90 = 8100) and add the results.
4
c = √16200 = √(8100 * 2) = 90√2 feet
Take the square root of 16200. To simplify, find the largest perfect square factor of 16200, which is 8100. Since √8100 = 90, the simplified form is 90√2 feet. Since √2 is an irrational number, 90√2 is also an irrational number.

Answer: 90√2 feet (Option B)

Question 4Grade 7 • NS
A drone flies a path that can be modeled as the hypotenuse of a right triangle. If it flies 3 miles north and 4 miles east, what is the exact length of its path?
A. 7 miles
B. √25 miles
C. √7 miles
D. 5 miles
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The drone's path is the hypotenuse of a right triangle, with legs a = 3 miles and b = 4 miles.
The north and east movements form the perpendicular sides of a right triangle.
2
a² + b² = c² => 3² + 4² = c²
The Pythagorean theorem relates the lengths of the sides of a right-angled triangle.
3
9 + 16 = c² => 25 = c²
3² = 9 and 4² = 16. Summing them gives 25.
4
c = √25 = 5 miles
The square root of 25 is 5. Since 5 is a whole number, the length of the path is a rational number, not an irrational one.

Answer: 5 miles (Option D)

E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1

2
V = (4/3)π(3)³

Question 8Grade 7 • NS
The period (T) of a pendulum, which is the time it takes to complete one swing, is given by the formula T = 2π√(L/g). If a pendulum has a length (L) of 0.5 meters and g is exactly 9.8 m/s², what is the exact value of √(L/g)?
A. √(5/98) seconds
B. √(1/196) seconds
C. 5/98 seconds
D. 0.226 seconds
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
√(0.5 / 9.8)
Replace L with 0.5 and g with 9.8 in the expression under the square root.
2
0.5 = 1/2, 9.8 = 98/10
To avoid introducing approximations, express the decimal numbers as fractions.
3
√( (1/2) / (98/10) ) = √( (1/2) * (10/98) ) = √( 10 / 196 )
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal. Then multiply the fractions.
4
√( 10 / 196 ) = √( (2 5) / (2 98) ) = √(5/98) seconds
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor, which is 2. The exact value is √(5/98). Since 5/98 is not a perfect square, √(5/98) is an irrational number.

Answer: √(5/98) seconds (Option A)

Question 9Grade 7 • NS
A certain rare species' population (P) growth over time (t) can be modeled by P = 100 * (1 + r)^t. If the population doubles every √5 years, what is the exact annual growth rate (r)?
A. 2^(√5) - 1
B. 2^(1/√5) - 1
C. (2-1)/√5
D. 0.447
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
If the population doubles, P becomes 2P. The time (t) is √5 years. So, 2P = P * (1 + r)^√5.
Substitute the condition for doubling and the given time into the population growth model.
2
Divide both sides by P: 2 = (1 + r)^√5
Isolate the exponential term to solve for the growth rate.
3
2^(1/√5) = ( (1 + r)^√5 )^(1/√5) => 2^(1/√5) = 1 + r
To undo an exponent of √5, raise both sides to the power of 1/√5. Recall that (x^a)^b = x^(a*b).
4
r = 2^(1/√5) - 1
Subtract 1 from both sides to find the exact annual growth rate. Since √5 is an irrational number, 1/√5 is irrational. Raising a rational base (2) to an irrational exponent (1/√5) generally results in an irrational number. Subtracting a rational number (1) from an irrational number results in an irrational number.

Answer: 2^(1/√5) - 1 (Option B)

D. 100/3 megabytes/second
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Rate = (Amount of Data) / (Time) = 100 megabytes / √3 seconds
The rate is defined as the quantity processed per unit of time.
2
(100 / √3) * (√3 / √3) = 100√3 / 3 megabytes/second
To rationalize the denominator, multiply both the numerator and the denominator by √3. This eliminates the square root from the denominator. Since √3 is an irrational number, 100√3/3 is also an irrational number.

Answer: 100√3 / 3 megabytes/second (Option C)
