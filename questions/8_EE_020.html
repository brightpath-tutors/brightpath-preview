Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A square display shelf has an area of 144 square inches. What is the length of one side of the shelf?
A. 72 inches
B. 288 inches
C. 12 inches
D. 20736 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
For a square, Area = Side length × Side length, or Area = (Side length)².
The area of a square is found by squaring its side length. To find the side length from the area, we need to perform the inverse operation, which is taking the square root.
2
Side length = √Area Side length = √144
Substitute the given area into the formula for finding the side length.
3
Since 12 × 12 = 144, √144 = 12.
The square root of 144 is 12, because 12 multiplied by itself equals 144.
4
Side length = 12 inches.
The length of one side of the display shelf is 12 inches.

Answer: 12 inches (Option C)
