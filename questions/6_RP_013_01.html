Skill ID: 6.RP.013.01

Question 1Grade 6 • RP
After a discount, a video game costs $27. If the discount was 10%, what was the original price of the video game?
A. $29.70
B. $24.30
C. $30.00
D. $37.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
100% - 10% = 90%
Calculate the percentage of the original price that remains after the discount.
2
Let P be the original price. 0.90 * P = $27
Set up an equation where the remaining percentage (as a decimal) multiplied by the original price equals the discounted price.
3
P = $27 / 0.90 = $30
Solve for P by dividing the discounted price by the remaining percentage.

Answer: $30.00 (Option C)

Question 10Grade 6 • RP
A new software update downloaded 75 MB in 15 seconds. If this was 2.5% of the total update size, what is the total size of the software update in MB?
A. 1.875 MB
B. 30 MB
C. 3,000 MB
D. 750 MB
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let S be the total update size. 2.5% of S = 75 MB
Identify the given part (75 MB downloaded) and the percentage it represents (2.5%). Set up an equation.
2
0.025 * S = 75
Convert the percentage to a decimal. Remember to move the decimal point two places to the left, so 2.5% becomes 0.025.
3
S = 75 / 0.025 = 3,000
Solve for S by dividing the downloaded amount by the decimal percentage.

Answer: 3,000 MB (Option C)
