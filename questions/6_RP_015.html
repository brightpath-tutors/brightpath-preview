Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A toy car originally cost $15. During a sale, its price was reduced to $12. What was the percent decrease in the price of the toy car?
A. 25%
B. 15%
C. 20%
D. 3%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Decrease = Original Price - New Price = $15 - $12 = $3
Subtract the new price from the original price to find the amount of reduction.
2
Percent Decrease = (Decrease / Original Price) 100% = ($3 / $15) 100% = 0.2 * 100% = 20%
Divide the decrease amount by the original price and multiply by 100 to express it as a percentage.

Answer: 20% (Option C)
