Question 1Grade 7 • RP
John buys 1/2 pound of cheese for $3/4. What is the ratio of the cost (in dollars) to the weight (in pounds) in simplest form?
A. 2:3
B. 3:8
C. 5:4
D. 3:2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost = $3/4, Weight = 1/2 pound
Identify the given quantities for cost and weight.
2
Ratio = (Cost) : (Weight) = (3/4) : (1/2)
Form the ratio of cost to weight as specified in the problem.
3
(3/4) ÷ (1/2)
To simplify a ratio of fractions, divide the first fraction by the second fraction.
4
(3/4) × (2/1)
To divide by a fraction, multiply by its reciprocal. The reciprocal of 1/2 is 2/1.
5
(3 × 2) / (4 × 1) = 6/4
Multiply the numerators and multiply the denominators.
6
6/4 = 3/2
Simplify the resulting fraction to its lowest terms by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD), which is 2.
7
3:2
Express the simplified fraction as a ratio.

Answer: 3:2 (Option D)
