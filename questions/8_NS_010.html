Question 1Grade 8 • NS
The temperature in Anchorage, Alaska was 5 degrees Celsius at noon. By midnight, the temperature had dropped to -3 degrees Celsius. What is the difference in temperature between noon and midnight?
A. 8 degrees Celsius
B. -8 degrees Celsius
C. -2 degrees Celsius
D. 2 degrees Celsius
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial temperature = 5 degrees Celsius Final temperature = -3 degrees Celsius
We need to find the change from 5 degrees to -3 degrees.
2
Difference = Initial temperature - Final temperature Difference = 5 - (-3)
Subtract the final temperature from the initial temperature to find the change.
3
5 - (-3) = 5 + 3
Subtracting a negative number is the same as adding its positive counterpart.
4
5 + 3 = 8
Add the numbers to get the final difference.

Answer: 8 degrees Celsius (Option A)
