Question 1Grade 8 • NS
Sarah had $50 in her bank account. She made a purchase of $75 using her debit card. What is her new bank balance?
A. -$125
B. -$25
C. $125
D. $25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = $50. Purchase = $75.
A purchase means money is taken out of the account, which is a negative change.
2
50 - 75
Start with the initial balance and subtract the amount of the purchase.
3
50 - 75 = -25
When subtracting a larger number from a smaller number, the result is negative. The difference between 75 and 50 is 25, so the balance is -$25.
4
New balance = -$25
Sarah's account is overdrawn by $25.

Answer: -$25 (Option B)

Question 2Grade 8 • NS
A football team gained 8 yards on one play and then lost 12 yards on the next play. What was the net change in yardage for the two plays?
A. 20 yards
B. 4 yards
C. -20 yards
D. -4 yards
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Gained 8 yards = +8. Lost 12 yards = -12.
Gains are positive, losses are negative.
2
8 + (-12) or 8 - 12
To find the net change, add the integer representations of the plays.
3
8 - 12 = -4
Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value (12 - 8 = 4) and use the sign of the number with the larger absolute value (-12), which is negative.
4
Net change = -4 yards
The team had a net loss of 4 yards.

Answer: -4 yards (Option D)

Question 3Grade 8 • NS

A. 120 feet
B. -180 feet
C. -120 feet
D. 180 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Descended 150 feet = -150. Rose 30 feet = +30.
Descending means moving down, which is negative. Rising means moving up, which is positive.
2
-150 + 30
To find the final depth, add the integer representations of the movements.
3
-150 + 30 = -120
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value (150 - 30 = 120) and use the sign of the number with the larger absolute value (-150), which is negative.
4
Final depth = -120 feet
The submarine is 120 feet below the surface.

Answer: -120 feet (Option C)

B. -7 degrees Celsius
C. 7 degrees Celsius
D. -23 degrees Celsius
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial temperature = -15°C. Dropped by 8°C.
Dropping temperature means subtracting a positive value from the current temperature.
2
-15 - 8
Start with the initial temperature and subtract the amount it dropped.
3
-15 - 8 = -23
When subtracting a positive number from a negative number, you move further into the negative direction on a number line. Add the absolute values (15 + 8 = 23) and keep the negative sign.
4
New temperature = -23°C
The temperature is now -23 degrees Celsius.

Answer: -23 degrees Celsius (Option D)

Question 6Grade 8 • NS

A. 375 degrees Fahrenheit
B. 415 degrees Fahrenheit
C. 335 degrees Fahrenheit
D. 285 degrees Fahrenheit
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Desired temperature = 350°F. Set 40°F too low, so actual initial temperature = 350 - 40 = 310°F.
Being 'too low' means subtracting from the desired value.
2
Current temperature = 310°F. Increased by 25°F, so final temperature = 310 + 25.
An increase means adding to the current value.
3
310 + 25 = 335
Perform the addition.
4
Final temperature = 335°F
The oven reached 335 degrees Fahrenheit.

Answer: 335 degrees Fahrenheit (Option C)

Question 7Grade 8 • NS
Over three months, a person's weight changed by -2.5 kg, then +1.8 kg, and finally -0.7 kg. What was the total change in weight?
A. 5.0 kg
B. -1.4 kg
C. 0 kg
D. 1.4 kg
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Changes are -2.5 kg, +1.8 kg, and -0.7 kg.
Negative values represent weight loss, positive values represent weight gain.
2
-2.5 + 1.8 + (-0.7) or -2.5 + 1.8 - 0.7
To find the total change, sum all the individual changes.
3
-2.5 + 1.8 = -0.7
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger (2.5 - 1.8 = 0.7) and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value (-2.5), which is negative.
4
-0.7 - 0.7 = -1.4
When subtracting a positive number from a negative number, or adding two negative numbers, add their absolute values (0.7 + 0.7 = 1.4) and keep the negative sign.
5
Total change = -1.4 kg
The person had a net loss of 1.4 kg.

Answer: -1.4 kg (Option B)

Question 8Grade 8 • NS

A. 55 feet
B. -40 feet
C. 25 feet
D. -25 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Starts at +15 feet. Digs down 30 feet = -30. Digs another 10 feet deeper = -10.
Above sea level is positive, digging down is negative.
2
15 + (-30) + (-10) or 15 - 30 - 10
Combine the initial elevation with all the changes.
3
15 - 30 = -15
Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger (30 - 15 = 15) and keep the sign of the larger absolute value (-30), which is negative.
4
-15 - 10 = -25
When subtracting a positive number from a negative number, or adding two negative numbers, add their absolute values (15 + 10 = 25) and keep the negative sign.
5
Final elevation = -25 feet
The trench is 25 feet below sea level.

Answer: -25 feet (Option D)

Question 9Grade 8 • NS
A small town had a population change of -150 residents in January, +200 in February, and -120 in March. If the initial population was 1500, what was the population at the end of March?
A. 1430 residents
B. 1970 residents
C. 1670 residents
D. -70 residents
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial population = 1500. Changes: -150, +200, -120.
Negative values mean population decrease, positive values mean population increase.
2
Net change = -150 + 200 - 120
Sum the monthly changes to find the overall change.
3
-150 + 200 = 50
Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger (200 - 150 = 50) and keep the sign of the larger absolute value (+200), which is positive.
4
50 - 120 = -70
Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger (120 - 50 = 70) and keep the sign of the larger absolute value (-120), which is negative. The net change is -70 residents.
5
Final population = 1500 + (-70) = 1500 - 70 = 1430
The final population is the initial population plus the net change.
6
Final population = 1430 residents
The town had 1430 residents at the end of March.

Answer: 1430 residents (Option A)

Question 10Grade 8 • NS
A laptop battery starts at 80% charge. It loses 15% charge during an update, gains 5% while charging, and then loses another 30% during use. What is the final battery percentage?
A. -40%
B. 130%
C. 100%
D. 40%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial charge = 80%. Changes: loses 15% = -15%, gains 5% = +5%, loses 30% = -30%.
Losses are negative changes, gains are positive changes.
2
80 + (-15) + 5 + (-30) or 80 - 15 + 5 - 30
Start with the initial charge and add/subtract each change.
3
80 - 15 = 65
Subtract the loss from the initial charge.
4
65 + 5 = 70
Add the gain to the current charge.
5
70 - 30 = 40
Subtract the final loss from the current charge.
6
Final percentage = 40%
The laptop battery has 40% charge remaining.

Answer: 40% (Option D)
