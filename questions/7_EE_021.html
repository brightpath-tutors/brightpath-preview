Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A cubic display box has a volume of 8 cubic feet. What is the length of one side of the box?
A. 2.67 feet
B. 8 feet
C. 2 feet
D. 4 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The volume of a cube is given by the formula V = s³, where V is the volume and s is the side length. We are given the volume V = 8 cubic feet and need to find the side length s.
Identify the relationship between the volume of a cube and its side length.
2
s³ = 8
Substitute the given volume into the formula.
3
s = ³√8 s = 2
To find the side length, take the cube root of the volume. The cube root of 8 is 2 because 2 × 2 × 2 = 8.
4
The length of one side of the box is 2 feet.
Ensure the answer includes the correct units.

Answer: 2 feet (Option C)
