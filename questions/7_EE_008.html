Question 1Grade 7 • EE
Sarah had $25 in her wallet. After buying a new book, she had $18 left. How much did the book cost?
A. $7
B. $17
C. $43
D. $13
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial money = $25, Money left = $18, Cost of book = x
We know how much money Sarah started with and how much she had left. We need to find the cost of the book.
2
25 - x = 18
The initial amount minus the cost of the book equals the money left.
3
25 - x = 18 x = 25 - 18 x = 7
To isolate x, subtract 18 from both sides, or subtract 18 from 25.
4
The book cost $7.
The cost of the book is $7.

Answer: $7 (Option A)

Step-by-step solution

We know the total points and points in the second half. We need to find points in the first half.

Total distance = 180 miles, Time = 3 hours, Average speed = s

3
