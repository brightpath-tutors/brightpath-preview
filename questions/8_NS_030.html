Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A person's bank account has a balance of -$25. They make a deposit of $50. What is their new balance?
A. $25
B. $75
C. -$25
D. -$75
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = -$25, so we represent it as -25.
Amounts owed or below zero are represented by negative integers. Amounts deposited are positive.
2
Deposit = $50, so we represent it as +50.
Deposits increase the balance, so they are positive.
3
New balance = -25 + 50.
To find the new total, we add the changes to the starting value.
4
Start at -25 on the number line. Since we are adding a positive number (50), move 50 units to the right.
Moving right on a number line represents addition of a positive value, and moving left represents subtraction or addition of a negative value.
5
-25 + 50 = 25.
When adding integers with different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. |50| - |-25| = 50 - 25 = 25. Since 50 is positive and has the larger absolute value, the result is positive.
6
The new balance is $25.
The final numerical result represents the new bank balance.

Answer: $25 (Option A)

Question 3Grade 8 • NS
The ideal temperature for a certain ingredient is 10°C. Due to an error, the temperature dropped to -3°C. How many degrees must the temperature increase to reach the ideal temperature?
A. -7°C
B. 13°C
C. -13°C
D. 7°C
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Current temperature = -3°C.
The problem states the temperature dropped to -3°C.
2
Target temperature = 10°C.
The problem states the ideal temperature is 10°C.
3
Increase needed = Target temperature - Current temperature = 10 - (-3).
To find out how much a value needs to increase to reach a target, subtract the current value from the target value.
4
Subtracting a negative number is equivalent to adding its positive counterpart: 10 - (-3) = 10 + 3.
On a number line, subtracting a negative means moving to the right, which is the same as adding a positive number.
5
10 + 3 = 13.
Basic addition of positive integers.
6
The temperature must increase by 13°C.
The calculated difference is the required temperature increase.

Answer: 13°C (Option B)

B. -55
C. -15
D. -5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Starting address = 0.
The problem specifies starting at 0.
2
0 + 30 = 30.
Moving to a positive address means adding that value.
3
30 - 50 = -20.
Jumping back means moving in the negative direction, so subtract.
4
-20 + 25 = 5. (This is the current position).
Moving forward means moving in the positive direction, so add.
5
The target is 10 units to the left of the current position (5). So, 5 - 10 = -5.
Moving to the left on a number line means subtracting. The current position is the starting point for this final movement.
6
The final target memory address is -5.
The final numerical result is the target memory address.
