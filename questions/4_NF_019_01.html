Question 8Grade 4 • NF
A stopwatch recorded a runner's sprint as 7/10 of a second. What is this time expressed as a decimal in hundredths?
A. 0.07
B. 0.7
C. 0.70
D. 7.0
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Given fraction = 7/10
Identify the given fraction of a second.
2
Convert to equivalent fraction with denominator 100
To express this fraction as a decimal in hundredths, first convert it to an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 100.
3
(7 10) / (10 10) = 70/100
Multiply both the numerator and denominator by 10 to get a denominator of 100.
4
Decimal form = 0.70
Convert the fraction 70/100 to a decimal by placing '70' after the decimal point, ensuring the '0' is in the hundredths place.

Answer: 0.70 (Option C)
