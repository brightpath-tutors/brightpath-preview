Question 1Grade 8 • F
Alex invests $500 in a savings account that offers an annual interest rate of 4% compounded annually. What will be the value of his investment after 1 year?
A. $500.04
B. $520.00
C. $540.00
D. $504.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
P = $500 r = 4% = 0.04 t = 1 year
The initial investment is $500. The annual interest rate is 4%, which is 0.04 as a decimal. The investment period is 1 year.
2
A = 500(1 + 0.04)^1
Substitute the identified values into the formula.
3
1 + 0.04 = 1.04
Add the growth rate to 1 to find the growth factor.
4
1.04^1 = 1.04
Since the time is 1 year, the growth factor remains 1.04.
5
A = 500 * 1.04 = 520
Multiply the initial amount by the growth factor to find the final value.
6
The value of the investment after 1 year will be $520.00.
The investment will grow to $520.00 after one year.

Answer: $520.00 (Option B)
