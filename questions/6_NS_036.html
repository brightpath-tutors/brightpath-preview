Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A store's profit decreased by $9 each day for 3 days. What was the total change in profit over these 3 days?
A. 27
B. 12
C. -27
D. -6
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Daily decrease = -9 Number of days = 3
A decrease in profit is represented by a negative number. The problem asks for the total change over multiple days.
2
Total change = (-9) * 3
To find the total change, multiply the change per day by the number of days.
3
9 * 3 = 27 A negative number multiplied by a positive number results in a negative product. So, (-9) * 3 = -27.
First, multiply the absolute values of the numbers. Then, apply the rule for multiplying integers: a negative integer multiplied by a positive integer yields a negative product.

Answer: -27 (Option C)
