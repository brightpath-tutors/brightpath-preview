Question 1Grade 6 • EE
Sarah divided her total shopping bill equally among 4 friends. Each friend paid $15. What was Sarah's total shopping bill?
A. $60
B. $3.75
C. $11
D. $19
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let 'b' represent Sarah's total shopping bill. The problem states that the total bill divided among 4 friends resulted in each friend paying $15. This can be written as the equation: b / 4 = 15
Translate the word problem into a mathematical equation where 'b' is the unknown total bill.
2
To find the value of 'b', we need to isolate it. Currently, 'b' is being divided by 4. The inverse operation of division is multiplication. Therefore, multiply both sides of the equation by 4: (b / 4) 4 = 15 4
Apply the multiplication property of equality to both sides of the equation to undo the division.
3
b = 15 * 4 = 60
Perform the multiplication to calculate the total bill.
4
Sarah's total shopping bill was $60.
State the final answer with the correct units.

Answer: $60 (Option A)
