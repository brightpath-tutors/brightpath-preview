C. c / 3
D. c - 3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost per chocolate bar = $3
Identify the cost of one chocolate bar.
2
Number of chocolate bars = c
Identify the unknown quantity represented by the letter 'c'.

Total cost = 3c
