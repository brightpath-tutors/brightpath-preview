The problem states that 3 bags of oranges cost $5.
2
New bags / Original bags = 9 / 3 = 3
To find the cost of 9 bags, we need to find out how many times larger 9 bags are compared to the original 3 bags. This factor is 3.
3

Question 10Grade 6 • RP
A data server can transfer 40 gigabytes of data in 5 minutes. At this rate, how long will it take to transfer 100 gigabytes of data, in minutes?
A. 20 minutes
B. 8 minutes
C. 12.5 minutes
D. 15 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Gigabytes : Minutes = 40 : 5
The server transfers 40 gigabytes in 5 minutes.
2
Minutes per gigabyte = 5 minutes / 40 gigabytes = 0.125 minutes/gigabyte
To find the total time for 100 gigabytes, first determine the time it takes to transfer 1 gigabyte.
3
Total minutes = 0.125 minutes/gigabyte * 100 gigabytes = 12.5 minutes
Multiply the time per gigabyte by the new amount of data to find the total time required.

Answer: 12.5 minutes (Option C)
