Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A construction crew is building a cubic water tank that can hold 27,000 cubic feet of water. What is the length of one side of the tank?
A. 9,000 feet
B. 30 feet
C. 164.3 feet
D. 90 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
V = s³ 27,000 = s³
The formula for the volume of a cube relates the volume to the side length cubed.
2
s = ³√27,000
To isolate 's', we take the cube root of both sides of the equation.
3
30 × 30 × 30 = 27,000 s = 30
By recognizing that 27 is 3 cubed, we can determine that 27,000 is 30 cubed.

Answer: 30 feet (Option B)

2
