B. 2x^2
C. x + 2
D. 3x
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Singles amount = x, Fives amount = 2x
Identify the given amounts of money in terms of 'x'.
2
Total money = x + 2x

3
Total money = 1x + 2x = (1+2)x = 3x
Combine the like terms by adding their coefficients.
