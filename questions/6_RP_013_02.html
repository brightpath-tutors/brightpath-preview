Finding the Whole from Percent

Skill ID: 6.RP.013.02

Question 1Grade 6 • RP
After a discount, a video game costs $27, which is 75% of its original price. What was the original price of the video game?
A. $102.00
B. $45.00
C. $36.00
D. $20.25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Part = $27, Percent = 75%
Identify the known part (discounted price) and the percent this part represents of the whole (original price).
2
Equation: Part = Percent / 100 Whole => 27 = 75/100 Whole
Set up a proportion or equation where 'Whole' is the unknown original price. Convert the percentage to a decimal or fraction.
3
Whole = 27 / (75/100) = 27 / 0.75 = 36
Solve for the Whole by dividing the Part by the decimal equivalent of the Percent.
4
Original price = $36.00
State the original price of the video game.

Answer: $36.00 (Option C)
