Question 10Grade 7 • NS
Over a period of 1.25 hours, the total change in data transferred was -15 3/5 megabytes. What was the average change in transfer amount per minute, expressed as a decimal rounded to two decimal places?
A. 0.21 MB/minute
B. -12.48 MB/minute
C. -0.16 MB/minute
D. -0.21 MB/minute
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
1.25 hours × 60 minutes/hour = 75 minutes
Convert the total time from hours to minutes.
2
-15 3/5 = -(15 × 5 + 3) / 5 = -78/5
Convert the mixed number representing the total data change to an improper fraction.
3
-78/5 = -15.6
Convert the improper fraction to a decimal for easier division with decimals.
4
-15.6 / 75
Set up the division problem: total data change divided by total minutes.
5
15.6 ÷ 75 = 0.208
Perform the division.
6
-0.208 ≈ -0.21
Apply the negative sign and round the result to two decimal places.

Answer: -0.21 MB/minute (Option D)
