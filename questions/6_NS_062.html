Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A submarine descends 120 feet in 4 minutes. What is the average vertical speed of the submarine in feet per minute?
A. -30 feet per minute
B. -116 feet per minute
C. 30 feet per minute
D. 124 feet per minute
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Vertical distance = -120 feet (descending) Time = 4 minutes
Descending motion is represented by a negative value for vertical distance.
2
Average vertical speed = -120 feet / 4 minutes = -30 feet per minute
Dividing a negative integer by a positive integer results in a negative integer. The unit is feet per minute.

Answer: -30 feet per minute (Option A)
