Question 1Grade 9 • N-RN
A shopper buys a small toy for $1/2 and a sticker for $1/4. What is the total cost, expressed as a single fraction in simplest form?
A. 3/4
B. 2/6
C. 3/8
D. 1/3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fractions to add: 1/2, 1/4
Identify the given fractions representing the costs.
2
LCD(2, 4) = 4
Find the least common denominator (LCD) for the two fractions.
3
1/2 = (1 2) / (2 2) = 2/4
Convert the first fraction to an equivalent fraction with the LCD.
4
1/4 = 1/4
The second fraction already has the LCD.
5
2/4 + 1/4 = 3/4
Add the numerators of the equivalent fractions while keeping the common denominator.
6
GCF(3, 4) = 1, so 3/4 is in simplest form.
Check if the resulting fraction can be simplified further by finding the greatest common factor (GCF) of the numerator and denominator.

Answer: 3/4 (Option A)
