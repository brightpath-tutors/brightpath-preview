Question 1Grade 6 • NS
Sarah's bank account shows a balance of -$32. What is the absolute value of this amount?
A. 64
B. 0
C. 32
D. -32
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The balance is -$32.
We need to find the absolute value of the bank balance.
2
Absolute value is the distance of a number from zero on a number line, and distance is always a non-negative value.
The absolute value operation makes any number, whether positive or negative, its positive counterpart.
3
$|-32| = 32$
The absolute value of -32 is 32, representing a debt of $32.

Answer: 32 (Option C)

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
An artist draws a point at coordinate -11 on a number line representing a canvas. They then add another point at coordinate 6. What is the sum of the absolute values of these two positions?
A. 17
B. -5
C. 5
D. 11
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Absolute value of first position = $|-11| = 11$.
The absolute value of -11 is its distance from zero.
2
Absolute value of second position = $|6| = 6$.
The absolute value of 6 is its distance from zero.
3
Sum = $11 + 6 = 17$.
Add the two absolute values together to get the final answer.

Answer: 17 (Option A)
