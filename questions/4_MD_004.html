Question 1Grade 4 • MD
Sophia ran 3 kilometers on Monday and 2 kilometers on Tuesday. How many meters did she run in total?
A. 50 meters
B. 5000 meters
C. 5 kilometers
D. 500 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total distance in kilometers = 3 km + 2 km = 5 km
Add the distances Sophia ran on Monday and Tuesday to find the total distance in kilometers.
2
1 km = 1000 meters
Recall the conversion factor between kilometers and meters.
3
Total distance in meters = 5 km * 1000 meters/km = 5000 meters
Multiply the total distance in kilometers by the conversion factor to get the total distance in meters.

Answer: 5000 meters (Option B)

C. 500 centimeters
D. 50 centimeters
E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
Total length in micrometers = 500 micrometers/chip * 100 chips = 50000 micrometers

2
1 micrometer = 0.0001 centimeters
