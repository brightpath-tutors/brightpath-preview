Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A grocery store sells 6 cans of tuna for $9.00. What is the unit price of one can of tuna?
A. $1.50
B. $0.67 per can
C. $1.50 per can
D. $54.00 per can
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cost = $9.00, Number of cans = 6
Identify the total cost and the number of cans.
2
Unit price = $9.00 / 6 cans = $1.50
To find the unit price, divide the total cost by the number of cans.
3
Unit rate = $1.50 per can
State the unit rate with the appropriate units.

Answer: $1.50 per can (Option C)
