Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A shop owner wants to arrange 18 pens and 24 notebooks into identical gift packs. Each pack must have the same number of pens and the same number of notebooks, with no items left over. To find the greatest number of identical packs, she needs to factor the expression 18p + 24n, where p is pens and n is notebooks. What is the factored expression?
A. 6(3p + 4n)
B. 6(3p + 24n)
C. 3(6p + 8n)
D. 2(9p + 12n)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The terms are 18p and 24n.
First, identify the individual terms in the expression.
2
Coefficients: 18, 24 Factors of 18: 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18 Factors of 24: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24 GCF(18, 24) = 6.
Determine the greatest common factor (GCF) of the numerical coefficients.
3
Divide each term by the GCF: 18p ÷ 6 = 3p 24n ÷ 6 = 4n
Divide each term in the expression by the GCF found in the previous step.
4
6(3p + 4n)
Place the GCF outside the parentheses and the results of the division inside.

Answer: 6(3p + 4n) (Option A)

Step-by-step solution
1
The expression r² - 7r + 10 is a quadratic trinomial in the form ax² + bx + c, where a=1, b=-7, and c=10.
Recognize that this is a trinomial where the coefficient of the squared term is 1, indicating a specific factoring method.
2
We need two numbers that multiply to 10 (c) and add to -7 (b). Factors of 10: (1, 10), (2, 5), (-1, -10), (-2, -5) Sums of these factors: 1+10=11, 2+5=7, -1-10=-11, -2-5=-7. The numbers are -2 and -5.

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
A tech company is designing a new chip. The area of a square component is represented by the expression c² - 36. Factor this expression to determine the side lengths of the component.
A. (c + 6)²
B. (c - 6)²
C. (c - 6)(c + 6)
D. c(c - 36)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The expression c² - 36 is a difference of two squares, a² - b².
Recognize that the expression consists of two perfect squares separated by a subtraction sign, indicating the difference of squares factoring pattern.
2
For a² - b²: a² = c² => a = c b² = 36 => b = 6
Find the square root of each term to determine the 'a' and 'b' values for the formula.
3
The formula is a² - b² = (a - b)(a + b). Substitute 'a' and 'b': (c - 6)(c + 6).
Substitute the identified 'a' and 'b' values into the difference of squares formula to get the factored expression.

Answer: (c - 6)(c + 6) (Option C)
