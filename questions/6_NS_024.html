Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A new video game costs $59.99. What type of number best describes the cost of the video game?
A. Rational Number
B. Irrational Number
C. Whole Number
D. Integer
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The cost of the video game is $59.99.
Identify the given number from the problem.
2
$59.99 can be written as the fraction 5999/100.
A decimal number that terminates can always be expressed as a fraction where the numerator is an integer and the denominator is a power of 10.
3
Since 5999 and 100 are integers and 100 is not zero, 59.99 is a rational number.
By definition, a rational number is any number that can be expressed as a fraction p/q, where p and q are integers and q ≠ 0.

Answer: Rational Number (Option A)

Question 2Grade 6 • NS
In a swimming competition, Sarah finished her race in 28.35 seconds, and Emily finished in 28.5 seconds. Who was faster?
A. The data is insufficient to determine.
B. Sarah
C. They finished at the same time.
D. Emily
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Sarah's time: 28.35 seconds Emily's time: 28.5 seconds
Identify the times for each swimmer.
2
To compare accurately, ensure both numbers have the same number of decimal places: 28.5 = 28.50.
Adding a zero at the end of a decimal does not change its value but helps in place-value comparison.
3
Compare 28.35 and 28.50: Whole number parts: 28 (equal) Tenths place: 3 for Sarah, 5 for Emily Hundredths place: 5 for Sarah, 0 for Emily
Start comparing digits from the leftmost place value. If the whole number parts are the same, move to the tenths place, then hundredths, and so on.
4
Since 3 (in 28.35) is less than 5 (in 28.50) in the tenths place, 28.35 is less than 28.50. A smaller time means a faster speed.
The smaller the time, the faster the swimmer. Therefore, Sarah is faster.

Answer: Sarah (Option B)

Elevations: -75, 110, 0, -30.

Quiz score: 95 Project score: 88.5 Homework score: 4/5

Classify the integer score based on the definition of rational numbers.

Question 7Grade 6 • NS
A carpenter needs to cut three pieces of wood with lengths: 4 1/4 inches, 4.3 inches, and 4 7/10 inches. To ensure they are cut correctly, he wants to order them from shortest to longest. What is the correct order?
A. 4.3 inches, 4 1/4 inches, 4 7/10 inches
B. 4 1/4 inches, 4.3 inches, 4 7/10 inches
C. 4 1/4 inches, 4 7/10 inches, 4.3 inches
D. 4 7/10 inches, 4.3 inches, 4 1/4 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Lengths: 4 1/4 inches, 4.3 inches, 4 7/10 inches.
List all the given lengths.
2
Convert all mixed numbers and fractions to decimals for easier comparison: 4 1/4 = 4 + 1/4 = 4 + 0.25 = 4.25 inches. 4.3 inches (already decimal). 4 7/10 = 4 + 7/10 = 4 + 0.7 = 4.7 inches.
Converting to a common format (decimals) simplifies the comparison process.
3
Compare the decimal values: 4.25, 4.3, 4.7. - The whole number part (4) is the same for all. - Compare the tenths place: 2 (from 4.25), 3 (from 4.3), 7 (from 4.7).
Compare the numbers digit by digit from left to right.
4
Order from shortest to longest: 4.25 (which is 4 1/4) 4.3 4.7 (which is 4 7/10)
Arrange the original lengths according to their decimal equivalents in ascending order.

Answer: 4 1/4 inches, 4.3 inches, 4 7/10 inches (Option B)

Question 8Grade 6 • NS
A painter tracked the time spent on a mural over three days: 6.5 hours on Monday, 7 3/4 hours on Tuesday, and 5 hours and 45 minutes on Wednesday. What was the average time (in hours, rounded to the nearest hundredth) the painter spent on the mural each day?
A. 6.67 hours
B. 6.75 hours
C. 6.66 hours
D. 6.58 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Monday: 6.5 hours Tuesday: 7 3/4 hours Wednesday: 5 hours and 45 minutes
List the time spent each day.
2
Convert all times to decimal hours: Monday: 6.5 hours Tuesday: 7 3/4 hours = 7 + (3 ÷ 4) = 7 + 0.75 = 7.75 hours. Wednesday: 5 hours and 45 minutes = 5 + (45 ÷ 60) hours = 5 + 0.75 = 5.75 hours.
Convert all measurements to a consistent unit (decimal hours) for easier calculation. There are 60 minutes in an hour, so 45 minutes is 45/60 of an hour.
3
Total time = 6.5 + 7.75 + 5.75 = 20 hours.
Add the decimal hours for each day to find the total time spent.
4
Average time = Total time / Number of days = 20 hours / 3 days = 6.666...
Calculate the average by dividing the total time by the number of days (3).
5
Round 6.666... to the nearest hundredth. The digit in the thousandths place (6) is 5 or greater, so round up the digit in the hundredths place. Result: 6.67 hours.
Follow rounding rules to express the average time to the specified precision.

Answer: 6.67 hours (Option A)

B. 1285.7 people per square mile
C. 128.5 people per square mile
D. 128.6 people per square mile
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Population = 450 people Area = 3.5 square miles
Identify the given population and area.
2
Population density = Population ÷ Area
Understand that population density is calculated by dividing the total population by the total land area.
3
Density = 450 ÷ 3.5
Set up the division problem.
4
To divide by a decimal, move the decimal point in the divisor (3.5) to the right until it is a whole number (35). Do the same for the dividend (450), adding a zero: 4500 ÷ 35. 4500 ÷ 35 ≈ 128.5714...
Perform the division. It is often easier to divide by a whole number, so adjust both numbers by multiplying by a power of 10.
5
Round 128.5714... to the nearest tenth. The digit in the hundredths place (7) is 5 or greater, so round up the digit in the tenths place. Result: 128.6.
Apply rounding rules to express the density to the nearest tenth.
