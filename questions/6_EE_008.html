Question 1Grade 6 • EE
Maya bought a toy for $12.50. She now has $8.00 left. How much money (m) did Maya have before buying the toy?
A. $4.50
B. $19.50
C. $21.50
D. $20.50
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let 'm' be the money Maya had before buying the toy. She spent $12.50, so 'm' decreased by $12.50, leaving her with $8.00. The equation is: m - 12.50 = 8.00
The problem describes a situation where an initial amount of money is reduced by a purchase, leaving a remaining amount. This translates to a subtraction equation.
2
To undo the subtraction of 12.50 from 'm', we need to add 12.50 to both sides of the equation: m - 12.50 + 12.50 = 8.00 + 12.50
To solve for 'm', we perform the inverse operation of subtraction, which is addition, on both sides of the equation to maintain balance.
3
m = 20.50
Adding 8.00 and 12.50 gives the total initial amount of money.
4
Maya had $20.50 before buying the toy.
The value of 'm' represents the money Maya had initially.

Answer: $20.50 (Option D)
