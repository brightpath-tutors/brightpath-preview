Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A baker needs to use at least 15 pounds of flour for a large cake order. Let 'f' represent the amount of flour in pounds. Which inequality represents this situation?
A. f ≤ 15
B. f < 15
C. f ≥ 15
D. f > 15
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The phrase 'at least' means the amount of flour must be greater than or equal to 15 pounds.
Identify the key phrase that indicates the type of inequality. 'At least' translates to greater than or equal to.
2
Using 'f' for flour, the inequality is f ≥ 15.
Represent the situation mathematically using the variable and the inequality symbol.

Answer: f ≥ 15 (Option C)
