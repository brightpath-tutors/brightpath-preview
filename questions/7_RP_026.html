Question 1Grade 7 • RP
At a pet store, there are 16 cats and 20 dogs. What is the ratio of cats to dogs in simplest form?
A. 5:4
B. 16:20
C. 4:9
D. 4:5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of cats = 16 Number of dogs = 20
The question asks for the ratio of cats to dogs, so we list the number of cats first, then the number of dogs.
2
Ratio of cats to dogs = 16:20
The initial ratio is 16 cats to 20 dogs.
3
Divide both numbers by their greatest common factor (GCF). The GCF of 16 and 20 is 4. 16 ÷ 4 = 4 20 ÷ 4 = 5 Simplified ratio = 4:5
To simplify, find the largest number that divides evenly into both parts of the ratio. In this case, 4.

Answer: 4:5 (Option D)

Question 4Grade 7 • RP

A. 3:4
B. 120:160
C. 3:1
D. 4:3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance on highway = 120 miles Distance on city road = 40 miles
We need these two distances to calculate the total distance and the desired ratio.
2
Total distance = Distance on highway + Distance on city road Total distance = 120 miles + 40 miles = 160 miles
The ratio requires the total distance, which is the sum of both parts of the journey.
3
Ratio = 120:160
The initial ratio is 120 miles on the highway to 160 miles total.
4
Divide both numbers by their greatest common factor (GCF). The GCF of 120 and 160 is 40. 120 ÷ 40 = 3 160 ÷ 40 = 4 Simplified ratio = 3:4
To simplify, divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF, which is 40.

Answer: 3:4 (Option A)

Question 8Grade 7 • RP
A meeting lasted for 2 hours and 15 minutes. What is the ratio of the meeting's duration in minutes to a full 3-hour workday, in simplest form?
A. 135:180
B. 4:3
C. 2:3
D. 3:4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2 hours = 2 * 60 minutes = 120 minutes Meeting duration = 120 minutes + 15 minutes = 135 minutes
To compare accurately, both quantities must be in the same unit. Convert hours to minutes by multiplying by 60.
2
3 hours = 3 * 60 minutes = 180 minutes
Convert the 3-hour workday to minutes.
3
Ratio = 135:180
The initial ratio is 135 minutes of meeting to 180 minutes of workday.
4
Find the GCF of 135 and 180. The GCF is 45. 135 ÷ 45 = 3 180 ÷ 45 = 4 Simplified ratio = 3:4
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF, which is 45.

Answer: 3:4 (Option D)

Question 9Grade 7 • RP
In a town with a population of 1800 people, it is known that 5 out of every 9 residents are adults. What is the ratio of adults to children in this town in simplest form?
A. 9:5
B. 4:5
C. 5:9
D. 5:4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Given: 5 out of every 9 residents are adults. This implies that for every 9 residents, 5 are adults. Number of children = Total parts - Adult parts = 9 - 5 = 4 parts.
The initial ratio given is a part-to-whole (adults to total residents). We need to derive the part-to-part ratio of adults to children.
2
Ratio of adults to children = 5:4
Since 5 parts are adults and 4 parts are children for every 9 residents, the ratio is 5:4.
3
The numbers 5 and 4 have no common factors other than 1, so the ratio 5:4 is already in simplest form.
The ratio 5:4 cannot be simplified further.

Answer: 5:4 (Option D)

B. 175:250
C. 7:10
D. 7:3
E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
Total users = 250 Users with smooth installation = 175
These are the given quantities from the problem.
2
Users with issues = Total users - Users with smooth installation Users with issues = 250 - 175 = 75
Subtract the users with smooth installation from the total users to find those who encountered issues.
3
Ratio = 175:75
The initial ratio is 175 users with smooth installation to 75 users with issues.
4
Find the GCF of 175 and 75. The GCF is 25. 175 ÷ 25 = 7 75 ÷ 25 = 3 Simplified ratio = 7:3
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCF, which is 25.
