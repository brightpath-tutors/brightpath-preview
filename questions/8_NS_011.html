Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A store decreases the price of a certain item by $3 each week. What is the total change in price after 4 weeks?
A. 12
B. -12
C. -1
D. -7
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Price decrease per week = -$3 Number of weeks = 4
A decrease in price is represented by a negative integer. The number of weeks is a positive integer.
2
Total change = (-3) * 4
To find the total change, multiply the weekly price change by the number of weeks.
3
(-3) * 4 = -12
When multiplying a negative integer by a positive integer, the product is negative. Multiply the absolute values (3 * 4 = 12) and then apply the negative sign.
4
The total change in price is -$12.
The negative sign indicates a decrease in price.

Answer: -12 (Option B)
