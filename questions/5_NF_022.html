Question 1Grade 5 • NF

B. 4 cakes
C. 2 cakes
D. 8 cakes
E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
4 ÷ 1/2
To find out how many cakes can be made, divide the total amount of sugar by the amount needed per cake.
2
4 × 2/1
Dividing by a fraction is the same as multiplying by its reciprocal.
3
8
Perform the multiplication to get the total number of cakes.

Answer: 8 cakes (Option D)
