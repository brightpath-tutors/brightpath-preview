Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A store sells 4 notebooks for $7.00. How much would 12 such notebooks cost?
A. $28.00
B. $21.00
C. $10.50
D. $14.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Unit cost = $7.00 / 4 notebooks = $1.75 per notebook.
To find out how much one notebook costs, divide the total cost by the number of notebooks.
2
Total cost = $1.75/notebook * 12 notebooks = $21.00.
Multiply the unit cost by the desired number of notebooks to find the total cost.

Answer: $21.00 (Option B)

Question 9Grade 6 • RP
A town's population grew from 3,500 to 4,200 in 5 years. If the growth rate per year remains proportional, what will the population be after an additional 10 years from the 4,200 population?
A. 7,000 people
B. 4,900 people
C. 6,300 people
D. 5,600 people
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Growth = 4,200 - 3,500 = 700 people.
Subtract the initial population from the population after 5 years to find the increase.
2
Annual rate = 700 people / 5 years = 140 people per year.
Divide the total growth by the number of years to find the growth per year.
3
Increase = 140 people/year * 10 years = 1,400 people.
Multiply the annual growth rate by the additional 10 years to find the total increase during that period.
4
New population = 4,200 + 1,400 = 5,600 people.
Add the calculated increase to the population after 5 years to find the projected population.

Answer: 5,600 people (Option D)

Question 10Grade 6 • RP
A computer downloads a 1.5 GB file in 120 seconds. At this rate, how long would it take to download a 4 GB file, in minutes?
A. 3.2 minutes
B. 10.67 minutes
C. 8 minutes
D. 5.33 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Speed = 1.5 GB / 120 seconds = 0.0125 GB/second.
Divide the file size by the time taken to find the download speed.
2
Time in seconds = 4 GB / 0.0125 GB/second = 320 seconds.
Divide the new file size by the download speed to find the total time in seconds.
3
Time in minutes = 320 seconds / 60 seconds/minute = 5.333... minutes ≈ 5.33 minutes.
Divide the total seconds by 60 to convert the time into minutes. Round to two decimal places.

Answer: 5.33 minutes (Option D)
