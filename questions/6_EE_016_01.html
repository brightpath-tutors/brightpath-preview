Question 1Grade 6 • EE
Sarah had some money. After spending $15 on a new book, she had $23 left. How much money did Sarah have initially?
A. $8
B. $15
C. $38
D. $23
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Let x be the initial amount of money Sarah had. The problem can be represented as: x - 15 = 23
Set up the equation based on the given information. The initial amount minus the amount spent equals the amount left.
2
x - 15 + 15 = 23 + 15
To isolate the variable 'x', apply the addition property of equality by adding 15 to both sides of the equation.
3
x = 38
Perform the addition on both sides to find the value of x.
4
Sarah initially had $38.
State the final answer in the context of the problem.

Answer: $38 (Option C)
