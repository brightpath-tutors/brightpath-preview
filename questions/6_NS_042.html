Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A store is having a sale where all items are 1/4 off the original price. If a shirt originally costs $28.00, how much money is saved on the shirt?
A. $7.00
B. $112.00
C. $21.00
D. $1.12
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Discount = 1/4, Original Price = $28
The problem states the discount is 1/4 off the original price of $28.
2
(1/4) * 28 = 28/4
To find the amount saved, multiply the fraction representing the discount by the total price.
3
28 / 4 = 7
Divide the numerator by the denominator to get the final amount.
4
Amount saved = $7.00
The result represents the money saved on the shirt.

Answer: $7.00 (Option A)

Question 5Grade 6 • NS
Sarah spent 3/4 of an hour studying for her math test. She spent 2/3 of that time reviewing fractions. What fraction of an hour did Sarah spend reviewing fractions, in simplest form?
A. 6/12 hour
B. 1/2 hour
C. 5/7 hour
D. 9/8 hour
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total study time = 3/4 hour, Fraction on fractions = 2/3
The problem specifies a total duration and a portion of that duration dedicated to a specific task.
2
(3/4) (2/3) = (3 2) / (4 * 3) = 6/12
To find 'a fraction of a fraction', multiply the two fractions together (numerator by numerator, denominator by denominator).
3
6/12 = 1/2
Find the greatest common factor (GCF) of the numerator and denominator (GCF of 6 and 12 is 6) and divide both by it.
4
Time reviewing fractions = 1/2 hour
The fraction represents the portion of an hour Sarah spent on reviewing fractions.

Question 7Grade 6 • NS
A construction worker needs to cut a metal rod that is 5 1/2 meters long into pieces that are 1/4 of its original length. What is the length of one of these pieces, in simplest form?
A. 22 meters
B. 1 3/8 meters
C. 5 1/8 meters
D. 5 2/8 meters
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
5 1/2 = (5 * 2 + 1) / 2 = 11/2
To multiply a mixed number, it is usually easiest to convert it to an improper fraction first.
2
(11/2) (1/4) = (11 1) / (2 * 4) = 11/8
Multiply the numerators and multiply the denominators.
3
11/8 = 1 with a remainder of 3, so 1 3/8
Divide the numerator by the denominator (11 ÷ 8 = 1 with 3 remaining) to get the whole number and the new numerator, keeping the original denominator.
4
Length of one piece = 1 3/8 meters
The length of one piece of the metal rod is 1 3/8 meters.

Answer: 1 3/8 meters (Option B)

Distance traveled = 1/2 mile
