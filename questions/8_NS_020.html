Question 1Grade 8 • NS
A baker uses 1/4 of a block of butter for a batch of cookies. Express this fraction as a terminating decimal.
A. 0.14
B. 1.4
C. 0.4
D. 0.25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
1 ÷ 4 = 0.25
A terminating decimal is a decimal that has a finite number of digits after the decimal point. To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator by the denominator.

Answer: 0.25 (Option D)
