Fraction as Sum of Unit Fractions

Skill ID: 4.NF.006

Question 1Grade 4 • NF
Sarah spent 2/5 of a dollar on candy. Which expression shows 2/5 as a sum of unit fractions?
A. 2/1 + 2/1 + 2/1 + 2/1 + 2/1
B. 1/2 + 1/2 + 1/2 + 1/2 + 1/2
C. 1/5 + 1/5
D. 1/5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The fraction is 2/5.
Identify the given fraction representing the amount Sarah spent.
2
A unit fraction for 2/5 has a numerator of 1 and the same denominator, which is 1/5.
Determine the unit fraction associated with the given fraction.
3
The numerator of 2/5 is 2, so we need to add the unit fraction 2 times. 1/5 + 1/5.
Express the fraction as a sum of its unit fractions, repeating the unit fraction as many times as the numerator.

Answer: 1/5 + 1/5 (Option C)
