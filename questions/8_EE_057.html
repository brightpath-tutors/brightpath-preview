Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A clothing store charges a base fee for custom t-shirts plus a cost per shirt. The total cost, y, for x shirts is given by the equation y = 8x + 25. What is the base fee charged by the store?
A. $8
B. $17
C. $33
D. $25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The slope-intercept form of a linear equation is y = mx + b, where 'm' is the slope (rate of change) and 'b' is the y-intercept (initial value).
In real-world contexts, the y-intercept often represents an initial or fixed value, while the slope represents a rate.
2
Given equation: y = 8x + 25 Comparing to y = mx + b, we have m = 8 and b = 25.
Here, 'm' (8) represents the cost per shirt, and 'b' (25) represents the fixed base fee.
3
The base fee is the initial cost when no shirts are produced (x=0), which corresponds to the y-intercept.
Therefore, the base fee charged by the store is $25.

Answer: $25 (Option D)

Question 4Grade 8 • EE
A car travels at a constant speed. After 1 hour, it has traveled 60 miles. After 3 hours, it has traveled 180 miles. Write an equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) to represent the distance traveled, y, after x hours.
A. y = 60x
B. y = 60x + 1
C. y = 120x
D. y = 60x + 60
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (1 hour, 60 miles) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (3 hours, 180 miles)
Each pair of (time, distance) forms a coordinate pair.
2
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) m = (180 - 60) / (3 - 1) m = 120 / 2 m = 60
The slope represents the speed of the car in miles per hour.
3
Using point (1, 60) and m = 60 in y = mx + b: 60 = 60(1) + b 60 = 60 + b b = 60 - 60 b = 0
The y-intercept represents the initial distance when x=0 hours. A 'b' of 0 means the car started at 0 miles at 0 hours.
4
With m = 60 and b = 0, the equation is y = 60x + 0.
This simplifies to y = 60x.

Answer: y = 60x (Option A)

Question 6Grade 8 • EE
A scientist is tracking the growth of a plant. On day 2, the plant is 8 cm tall. On day 5, it is 17 cm tall. Assuming linear growth, write an equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) that models the plant's height, y, after x days.
A. y = 3x + 8
B. y = 3x + 17
C. y = 3x + 2
D. y = 1/3x + 2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (2 days, 8 cm) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (5 days, 17 cm)
Each pair of (day, height) forms a coordinate pair.
2
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) m = (17 - 8) / (5 - 2) m = 9 / 3 m = 3
The slope represents the plant's growth rate in cm per day.
3
Using point (2, 8) and m = 3 in y = mx + b: 8 = 3(2) + b 8 = 6 + b b = 8 - 6 b = 2
The y-intercept represents the initial height of the plant at day 0.
4
With m = 3 and b = 2, the equation is y = 3x + 2.
This equation models the plant's height over time.

Answer: y = 3x + 2 (Option C)

Question 8Grade 8 • EE
A digital clock displays time in minutes. A timer is set for a certain duration. After 5 minutes have passed, there are 115 minutes remaining. After 15 minutes have passed, there are 85 minutes remaining. Write an equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) to represent the minutes remaining, y, after x minutes have passed.
A. y = -3x + 130
B. y = -3x + 115
C. y = 3x + 130
D. y = 3x + 100
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (5 minutes passed, 115 minutes remaining) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (15 minutes passed, 85 minutes remaining)
Each pair of (minutes passed, minutes remaining) forms a coordinate pair.
2
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) m = (85 - 115) / (15 - 5) m = -30 / 10 m = -3
The slope represents the rate at which minutes remaining decrease per minute passed.
3
Using point (5, 115) and m = -3 in y = mx + b: 115 = -3(5) + b 115 = -15 + b b = 115 + 15 b = 130
The y-intercept represents the initial total duration of the timer when 0 minutes have passed.
4
With m = -3 and b = 130, the equation is y = -3x + 130.
This equation models the minutes remaining on the timer.

Answer: y = -3x + 130 (Option A)

Question 9Grade 8 • EE
The population of a small town has been decreasing linearly. In 2010 (x=0), the population was 4,500. By 2018 (x=8), the population had dropped to 3,700. Write an equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) to model the town's population, y, x years after 2010.
A. y = 100x + 4500
B. y = -100x + 3700
C. y = -800x + 4500
D. y = -100x + 4500
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (0 years, 4500 population) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (8 years, 3700 population)
The year 2010 is defined as x=0, which directly gives the y-intercept. 2018 is 8 years after 2010.
2
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) m = (3700 - 4500) / (8 - 0) m = -800 / 8 m = -100
The slope represents the rate of population decrease per year.
3
Since x=0 corresponds to the year 2010, the population at x=0 is the y-intercept.
So, b = 4500.
4
With m = -100 and b = 4500, the equation is y = -100x + 4500.
This equation models the town's population over time.

Answer: y = -100x + 4500 (Option D)

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
A company manufactures computer chips. The cost to produce 100 chips is $2,500. The cost to produce 300 chips is $6,500. Assuming the relationship between cost and chips produced is linear, write an equation in slope-intercept form (y = mx + b) representing the total cost, y, to produce x chips.
A. y = 20x - 500
B. y = 20x + 2500
C. y = 20x + 500
D. y = 40x - 1500
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Point 1: (x1, y1) = (100 chips, $2500) Point 2: (x2, y2) = (300 chips, $6500)
Each pair of (number of chips, cost) forms a coordinate pair.
2
m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) m = (6500 - 2500) / (300 - 100) m = 4000 / 200 m = 20
The slope represents the cost per chip produced.
3
Using point (100, 2500) and m = 20 in y = mx + b: 2500 = 20(100) + b 2500 = 2000 + b b = 2500 - 2000 b = 500
The y-intercept represents the fixed cost of production (e.g., overhead, equipment) when 0 chips are produced.
4
With m = 20 and b = 500, the equation is y = 20x + 500.
This equation models the total cost to produce x chips.

Answer: y = 20x + 500 (Option C)
