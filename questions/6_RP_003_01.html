Question 4Grade 6 • RP
A car traveled 120 miles in 2 hours. Write the ratio of miles to hours in simplest form.
A. 60:2
B. 120:2
C. 60:1
D. 2:120
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Miles traveled = 120
Identify the first quantity, the number of miles traveled.
2
Hours taken = 2
Identify the second quantity, the number of hours taken.
3
Initial ratio of miles to hours = 120 : 2
Write the initial ratio using colon notation before simplifying.
4
GCD(120, 2) = 2
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 120 and 2 to simplify the ratio.
5
Simplified ratio = (120 ÷ 2) : (2 ÷ 2) = 60 : 1
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCD to express it in simplest form.
6
Answer: 60:1
State the final simplified ratio.

Answer: 60:1 (Option C)
