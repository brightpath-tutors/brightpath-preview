Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Sarah had $8 in her account. She then made a purchase that debited $5 from her account. What is her new balance?
A. $13
B. -$3
C. $3
D. $0
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Starting balance: $8
The initial amount in Sarah's account is the starting point on the number line.
2
Purchase debited: $5, which is adding -5
A debit means money is taken out, so it's represented as adding a negative integer.
3
Starting at 8 on the number line, move 5 units to the left.
To add a negative integer, move to the left on the number line by the absolute value of the integer.
4
8 - 5 = 3
Count 5 units to the left from 8 to find the new position, which is the final balance.

Answer: $3 (Option C)
