Question 2Grade 6 • NS
In a technology report, a device's charging speed is stated as 0.75 of a full charge per hour. What fraction represents this speed in simplest form?
A. 75/10
B. 7/5
C. 1/4
D. 3/4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Decimal = 0.75
Identify the given decimal.
2
0.75 = 75/100
Write the decimal as a fraction by placing the digits after the decimal point over the corresponding power of 10. 0.75 means 75 hundredths.
3
GCD(75, 100) = 25
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and the denominator.
4
75 ÷ 25 = 3, 100 ÷ 25 = 4
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their GCD to simplify the fraction.
5
3/4
State the simplified fraction.

Answer: 3/4 (Option D)

Question 9Grade 6 • NS
A financial transaction resulted in a profit of $12.375. Express this profit as a mixed number in simplest form.
A. 12 37/100
B. 12 375/1000
C. 12 3/8
D. 12.375/1
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Decimal = 12.375
Identify the given decimal number.
2
Whole number part = 12
Separate the whole number part from the decimal part.
3
Decimal part = 0.375
Isolate the decimal part.
4
0.375 = 375/1000
Convert the decimal part to a fraction by placing the digits after the decimal point over the corresponding power of 10. 0.375 means 375 thousandths.
5
GCD(375, 1000) = 125
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and the denominator of the fractional part.
6
375 ÷ 125 = 3, 1000 ÷ 125 = 8
Divide both the numerator and the denominator by their GCD to simplify the fraction.
7
Simplified fraction = 3/8
State the simplified fractional part.
8
Mixed number = 12 3/8
Combine the whole number part and the simplified fractional part to form the mixed number.

Answer: 12 3/8 (Option C)

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
A car traveled a distance of 145.6 miles in 2.5 hours. What is the average speed of the car as a mixed number in simplest form?
A. 58 12/50 miles per hour
B. 1456/25 miles per hour
C. 58.24 miles per hour
D. 58 6/25 miles per hour
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance = 145.6 miles, Time = 2.5 hours
Identify the given distance and time.
2
Average speed = Distance / Time = 145.6 / 2.5
Calculate the average speed by dividing the distance by the time.
3
145.6 / 2.5 = 58.24
Perform the division to get the speed in decimal form.
4
Whole number part = 58
Separate the whole number part from the decimal part of the speed.
5
Decimal part = 0.24
Isolate the decimal part of the speed.
6
0.24 = 24/100
Convert the decimal part to a fraction. 0.24 means 24 hundredths.
7
GCD(24, 100) = 4, 24 ÷ 4 = 6, 100 ÷ 4 = 25
Find the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and the denominator, then simplify the fraction.
8
Mixed number = 58 6/25 miles per hour
Combine the whole number part and the simplified fractional part to form the mixed number.

Answer: 58 1/5 miles per hour (Option D)
