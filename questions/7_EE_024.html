Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A car travels 120 miles in 2 hours. What is the car's average speed in miles per hour, in simplest form?
A. 240 miles/hour
B. 120 miles/hour
C. 0.0167 miles/hour
D. 60 miles/hour
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance = 120 miles, Time = 2 hours
The problem provides the total distance traveled and the total time taken.
2
Speed = Distance / Time
Slope in a distance-time context represents speed.
3
Speed = 120 miles / 2 hours = 60 miles/hour
Divide the total distance by the total time to find the average speed.

Answer: 60 miles/hour (Option D)
