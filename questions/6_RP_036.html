Percent of a Number

Skill ID: 6.RP.036

Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A baker made 40 cookies. If 20% of them were chocolate chip, how many chocolate chip cookies did the baker make?
A. 8 chocolate chip cookies
B. 20 chocolate chip cookies
C. 2 chocolate chip cookies
D. 5 chocolate chip cookies
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cookies = 40, Percentage of chocolate chip cookies = 20%
Identify the given values: the total number of cookies and the percentage that are chocolate chip.
2
20% = 20/100 = 0.20
To calculate a percentage of a number, first convert the percentage into its decimal form by dividing by 100.
3
40 * 0.20 = 8
Multiply the total quantity by the decimal equivalent of the percentage to find the part.
4
The baker made 8 chocolate chip cookies.
The result is the number of chocolate chip cookies.

Answer: 8 chocolate chip cookies (Option A)
