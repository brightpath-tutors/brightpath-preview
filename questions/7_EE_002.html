Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A shop sells t-shirts for $12 each and hats for $8 each. The cost (C) for buying t t-shirts and h hats is given by the expression 12t + 8h. If a customer buys 3 t-shirts and 2 hats, what is the total cost?
A. $31
B. $25
C. $52
D. $46
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
t = 3, h = 2
The problem states that the customer buys 3 t-shirts (t) and 2 hats (h).
2
C = 12(3) + 8(2)
Replace 't' with 3 and 'h' with 2 in the given expression.
3
C = 36 + 16
Multiply 12 by 3 to get 36, and 8 by 2 to get 16. According to the order of operations, multiplication comes before addition.
4
C = 52
Add 36 and 16 to find the total cost.

Answer: $52 (Option C)

E. None of the above

t = 3

M = 120 - 5(3)^2 + 10(3)
Replace 't' with 3 in the given expression.
3
