Question 1Grade 6 • NS
Maria had $25 in her bank account. She deposited $10 and then withdrew $18. What is her current account balance?
A. $23
B. $27
C. $17
D. $53
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance: $25. Deposited: +$10. Withdrew: -$18.
Starting amount is positive. Depositing money adds to the balance (positive change). Withdrawing money removes from the balance (negative change).
2
$25 + $10 = $35
Add the deposited amount to the initial balance.
3
$35 - $18 = $17
Subtract the withdrawn amount from the balance after the deposit.

Answer: $17 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 6 • NS
A football team gained 7 yards on one play and then lost 12 yards on the next play. What is the team's total change in yardage?
A. -19 yards
B. 5 yards
C. -5 yards
D. 19 yards
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Gained 7 yards: +7. Lost 12 yards: -12.
Gaining yards is a positive change, and losing yards is a negative change.
2
7 + (-12) = 7 - 12 = -5
Combine the positive and negative changes. When adding a positive and a negative number, subtract their absolute values and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value.

Answer: -5 yards (Option C)

Question 3Grade 6 • NS
The temperature in a freezer was -8°C. If the temperature increased by 5°C, what is the new temperature?
A. -3°C
B. -13°C
C. 3°C
D. 13°C
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial temperature: -8°C. Increased by: +5°C.
A temperature below zero is represented by a negative number. An increase in temperature is a positive change.
2
-8 + 5 = -3
Combine the initial negative temperature with the positive change. Subtract the absolute values (8 - 5 = 3) and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value, which is negative in this case.

Answer: -3°C (Option A)

Question 5Grade 6 • NS
On a math test, correct answers are worth 8 points and incorrect answers are worth -3 points. Sarah answered 15 questions correctly and 5 questions incorrectly. What is her total score?
A. 90 points
B. 135 points
C. 105 points
D. 135 points
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
15 correct answers * 8 points/answer = 120 points.
Multiply the number of correct answers by the points for each correct answer.
2
5 incorrect answers * -3 points/answer = -15 points.
Multiply the number of incorrect answers by the points for each incorrect answer (which is a negative value).
3
120 + (-15) = 120 - 15 = 105.
Add the points from correct answers and the points from incorrect answers to find the total score. Adding a negative is equivalent to subtracting.

Answer: 105 points (Option C)

Question 7Grade 6 • NS
An ancient civilization began in 450 BCE and ended in 210 CE. How many years did this civilization exist? (Note: There is no year 0 between 1 BCE and 1 CE).
A. 650 years
B. 659 years
C. 240 years
D. 660 years
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
450 BCE can be thought of as -450. 210 CE is +210.
In timelines, BCE years are often treated as negative and CE years as positive, with the transition point around year 0.
2
The span from -450 to +210 is 210 - (-450) = 210 + 450 = 660 years.
To find the duration between a negative and a positive point, subtract the earlier year from the later year. Subtracting a negative is equivalent to adding a positive.
3
Since there is no year 0, subtract 1 from the total span: 660 - 1 = 659 years.
When calculating years across BCE/CE, the mathematical subtraction counts year 0, which does not exist in historical calendars. Therefore, one year must be subtracted from the sum of the absolute values of the BCE and CE years.

Answer: 659 years (Option B)

Question 8Grade 6 • NS
Over three months, a patient's weight changed as follows: lost 5 kg, gained 3 kg, and then lost another 7 kg. If their initial weight was 72 kg, what is their final weight?
A. 81 kg
B. 63 kg
C. 57 kg
D. 87 kg
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Lost 5 kg: -5. Gained 3 kg: +3. Lost another 7 kg: -7.
Weight loss is a negative change, and weight gain is a positive change.
2
-5 + 3 + (-7) = -2 + (-7) = -9 kg.
Sum the individual changes. First, combine -5 and 3 to get -2. Then, combine -2 and -7 to get -9.
3
Initial weight + total change = 72 + (-9) = 72 - 9 = 63 kg.
Add the total weight change to the patient's initial weight. Adding a negative is the same as subtracting.

Answer: 63 kg (Option B)

Question 9Grade 6 • NS
The population of a small town was 12,500 at the beginning of the year. Over the year, it increased by 350, then decreased by 180, and finally increased by 95. What was the town's population at the end of the year?
A. 12,475
B. 12,385
C. 12,945
D. 12,765
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Increased by 350: +350. Decreased by 180: -180. Increased by 95: +95.
Population increases are positive changes, and decreases are negative changes.
2
350 + (-180) + 95 = 170 + 95 = 265.
Sum the individual changes. First, combine 350 and -180 to get 170. Then, combine 170 and 95 to get 265.
3
Initial population + total change = 12,500 + 265 = 12,765.
Add the total population change to the initial population.

Answer: 12,765 (Option D)

Question 10Grade 6 • NS
A mobile phone plan starts with 20 GB of data. In the first week, the user downloaded 12 GB. In the second week, they used 7 GB. Then, they received a bonus of 5 GB for a promotion. What is the user's current data balance?
A. 6 GB
B. 44 GB
C. 10 GB
D. -4 GB
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial data: +20 GB. Downloaded: -12 GB. Used: -7 GB. Bonus: +5 GB.
Starting data is positive. Downloading and using data are negative changes. Receiving a bonus is a positive change.
2
(-12) + (-7) + 5 = -19 + 5 = -14 GB.
Sum the individual changes. First, combine -12 and -7 to get -19. Then, combine -19 and 5 to get -14.
3
Initial data + total change = 20 + (-14) = 20 - 14 = 6 GB.
Add the total data change to the initial data. Adding a negative is the same as subtracting.

Answer: 6 GB (Option A)
