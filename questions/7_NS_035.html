Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A shopper has $50 in their account. They spend $75. What is the new balance?
A. 25
B. -125
C. 125
D. -25
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Subtracting a number is the same as adding its opposite. So, 50 - 75 becomes 50 + (-75).
To subtract integers, you can change the operation to addition and change the sign of the second number to its opposite.
2
To add integers with different signs, find the difference between their absolute values and use the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Absolute value of 50 is |50| = 50. Absolute value of -75 is |-75| = 75. The difference is 75 - 50 = 25. Since |-75| (75) is greater than |50| (50), the result takes the sign of -75, which is negative.
When adding numbers with different signs, the magnitude of the result is the difference of their absolute values. The sign matches the number with the greater absolute value.
3
The new balance is -25.
The final balance is the result of the integer subtraction.

Answer: -25 (Option D)

2039 years have passed.

Question 9Grade 7 • NS
A town's population was -250 (a deficit compared to a target). After a month, the population changed to -180 (still a deficit). What was the change in population during the month? (Final - Initial)
A. 430
B. -70
C. -430
D. 70
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial population change is -250. Final population change is -180.
Clearly define the starting and ending points for the calculation.
2
Change = Final - Initial = -180 - (-250).
The change is always calculated as the final value minus the initial value.
3
Subtracting a negative number is the same as adding its positive opposite. So, -180 - (-250) becomes -180 + 250.
To subtract a negative integer, change the subtraction to addition and change the sign of the number being subtracted to its positive opposite.
4
To add integers with different signs, find the difference between their absolute values and use the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Absolute value of -180 is |-180| = 180. Absolute value of 250 is |250| = 250. The difference is 250 - 180 = 70. Since |250| (250) is greater than |-180| (180), the result takes the sign of 250, which is positive.
When adding numbers with different signs, the magnitude of the result is the difference of their absolute values. The sign matches the number with the greater absolute value.
5
The change in population during the month was 70 people.
The final answer represents the net change in population.

Answer: 70 (Option D)
