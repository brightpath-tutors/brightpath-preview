Question 1Grade 8 • EE
Maria wants to buy a new jacket for $45. She has $12 saved and earns $7 per hour babysitting. What is the minimum number of hours (h) Maria must babysit to afford the jacket?
A. h ≥ 4.71
B. h ≥ 33
C. h ≤ 4.71
D. h ≥ 5.28
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
7h + 12 ≥ 45
Set up the inequality: Maria's earnings (7h) plus her savings ($12) must be greater than or equal to the jacket's cost ($45).
2
7h ≥ 45 - 12 7h ≥ 33
Subtract 12 from both sides of the inequality to isolate the term with h.
3
h ≥ 33 / 7 h ≥ 4.7142...
Divide both sides by 7 to solve for h. Round to two decimal places.
4
h ≥ 4.71
The minimum number of hours Maria must babysit is 4.71 hours.

Answer: h ≥ 4.71 (Option A)

Question 5Grade 8 • EE
Emily needs an average score of at least 90 on her 5 math tests to get an 'A'. Her scores on the first 4 tests are 88, 92, 85, and 95. What score (x) must she get on her fifth test to achieve an 'A'?
A. x ≥ 100
B. x ≤ 90
C. x ≥ 90
D. x ≥ 80
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
(88 + 92 + 85 + 95 + x) / 5 ≥ 90
Set up the inequality: The sum of all 5 test scores divided by 5 must be greater than or equal to 90.
2
(360 + x) / 5 ≥ 90
Sum the known test scores: 88 + 92 + 85 + 95 = 360.
3
360 + x ≥ 90 * 5 360 + x ≥ 450
Multiply both sides of the inequality by 5 to clear the denominator.
4
x ≥ 450 - 360 x ≥ 90
Subtract 360 from both sides to solve for x.
5
x ≥ 90
Emily must score at least 90 on her fifth test.

Answer: x ≥ 90 (Option C)

A. m > 4
B. m < 4
C. m > -4
D. m < -4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
23 - 2m < 15
Set up the inequality: The current temperature (23) minus the decrease over time (2m) must be less than 15.
2
-2m < 15 - 23 -2m < -8
Subtract 23 from both sides of the inequality to isolate the term with m.
3
m > -8 / -2 m > 4
Divide both sides by -2. Remember to flip the inequality sign when dividing by a negative number.
4
m > 4
More than 4 minutes must pass for the temperature to be within the required range.

Answer: m > 4 (Option A)

2
3.5t ≤ 25 - 8.5 3.5t ≤ 16.5
