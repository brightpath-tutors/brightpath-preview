Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A circular pizza has an area of 113.04 square inches. What is the approximate radius of the pizza? Use 3.14 for \(\pi\). Round to the nearest whole number.
A. 6 inches
B. 3 inches
C. 18 inches
D. 12 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Area = \(\pi r^2\)
The area of a circle is given by the formula \(A = \pi r^2\), where \(r\) is the radius.
2
$113.04 = 3.14 \times r^2$
We are given the area (113.04 sq in) and told to use 3.14 for \(\pi\).
3
\(r^2 = \frac{113.04}{3.14} = 36\)
Divide both sides by 3.14 to isolate \(r^2\).
4
\(r = \sqrt{36} = 6\)
The radius is the square root of 36.
5
6 inches
Since 6 is a whole number, no rounding is needed.

Answer: 6 inches (Option A)
