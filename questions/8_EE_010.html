Question 1Grade 8 • EE
Sarah is saving money to buy a new bicycle. She already has $45 saved. She earns $15 each week by doing chores. If the bicycle costs $210, how many weeks (w) will it take her to save enough money?
A. 11 weeks
B. 17 weeks
C. 10 weeks
D. 14 weeks
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
45 + 15w = 210
Sarah starts with $45 and adds $15 each week (15w) until she reaches $210.
2
15w = 210 - 45 15w = 165
To isolate the term with 'w', subtract 45 from both sides of the equation.
3
w = 165 / 15 w = 11
Divide both sides by 15 to find the value of 'w'.

Answer: 11 weeks (Option A)

Question 4Grade 8 • EE

A. 90 mph
B. 85 mph
C. 95 mph
D. 77 mph
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The 0.5-hour break does not contribute to the distance traveled. The distance is only covered during the 3 hours of travel.
The problem asks for speed, which is distance over time. The break time is irrelevant for calculating travel speed.
2
3s = 270
Distance (270 miles) equals speed (s) multiplied by time (3 hours).
3
s = 270 / 3 s = 90
Divide the total distance by the travel time to find the speed.

A. 11 planks
B. 12 planks
C. 9 planks
D. 10 planks
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Length used = 8 planks * 1.5 meters/plank = 12 meters
Multiply the number of planks already cut by the length of each plank.
2
Remaining length = 27 meters - 12 meters = 15 meters
Subtract the used length from the total length.
3
1.5p = 15
The length per plank (1.5) times the number of remaining planks (p) equals the remaining length (15).
4
p = 15 / 1.5 p = 10

Question 8Grade 8 • EE
An animal shelter's cat population increased by 15 in the first month, then decreased by 7. At the end of two months, the population was half of its original size, plus 25 more cats. If the original population was 'x' cats, what was the original population?
A. 34 cats
B. 30 cats
C. 28 cats
D. 32 cats
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
x + 15 - 7 = x/2 + 25
The change in population (x + 15 - 7) equals half the original population (x/2) plus 25.
2
x + 8 = x/2 + 25
Combine the constants on the left side (15 - 7 = 8).
3
x - x/2 + 8 = 25 x/2 + 8 = 25
Move all terms with 'x' to one side. x - x/2 is x/2.
4
x/2 = 25 - 8 x/2 = 17
Isolate the 'x/2' term by subtracting 8 from both sides.
5
x = 17 * 2 x = 34
Multiply both sides by 2 to find the value of x.

Answer: 24 cats (Option A)
