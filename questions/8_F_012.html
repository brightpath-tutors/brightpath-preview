Question 1Grade 8 • F
A baker starts the day with 30 cupcakes. Every hour, she bakes 5 more cupcakes. How many cupcakes will she have after 4 hours?
A. 45 cupcakes
B. 55 cupcakes
C. 35 cupcakes
D. 50 cupcakes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial cupcakes (a_1) = 30 Cupcakes baked per hour (d) = 5
The baker starts with 30 cupcakes, so this is our initial value. She adds 5 cupcakes every hour, which is the constant difference.
2
We want to find the number of cupcakes after 4 hours. Since the initial term is at hour 0, after 4 hours will be the 5th term (a_5) of the sequence.
If we consider the start as hour 0 (a_1), then after 1 hour is a_2, after 2 hours is a_3, and so on. So, after 4 hours, it will be the 5th term (n=5).
3
The formula for the nth term of an arithmetic sequence is a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d. Substitute a_1 = 30, d = 5, and n = 5: a_5 = 30 + (5-1) * 5 a_5 = 30 + (4) * 5 a_5 = 30 + 20 a_5 = 50
Plug the values into the formula to find the total number of cupcakes.

Answer: 50 cupcakes (Option D)
