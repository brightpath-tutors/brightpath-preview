Question 1Grade 7 • RP
A shop sells a pack of 18 pens for $6. What is the ratio of pens to dollars in simplest form?
A. 9:3
B. 3:1
C. 12:1
D. 1:3
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
The ratio of pens to dollars is 18:6.
We write the number of pens first, then the number of dollars, as given in the problem.
2
Factors of 18: 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18 Factors of 6: 1, 2, 3, 6 The GCF of 18 and 6 is 6.
To simplify a ratio, we need to find the largest number that divides evenly into both parts of the ratio.
3
18 ÷ 6 = 3 6 ÷ 6 = 1 So, the simplified ratio is 3:1.
Dividing both numbers by their GCF gives us the ratio in its simplest form, meaning there are no common factors greater than 1.

Answer: 3:1 (Option B)
