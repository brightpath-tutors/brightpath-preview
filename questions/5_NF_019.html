Question 1Grade 5 • NF
A baker has 1/2 of a pie left. If he wants to divide it equally among 4 customers, what fraction of the whole pie will each customer receive?
A. 2/4 of the pie
B. 4/2 of the pie
C. 1/2 of the pie
D. 1/8 of the pie
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
1/2 ÷ 4
Represent the problem as a division expression: a unit fraction divided by a whole number.
2
1/2 × 1/4
To divide by a whole number, multiply by its reciprocal. The reciprocal of 4 is 1/4.
3
1 × 1 / 2 × 4 = 1/8
Multiply the numerators and multiply the denominators to find the resulting fraction in simplest form.

Answer: 1/8 of the pie (Option D)
