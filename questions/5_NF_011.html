Question 1Grade 5 • NF
Sophia needs to measure 1/4 of a 12-ounce bottle of paint for her art project. How many ounces of paint does she need?
A. 4 ounces
B. 3 ounces
C. 12 ounces
D. 1 ounce
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fraction = 1/4, Total quantity = 12 ounces
Identify the given fraction and the total quantity.
2
(1/4) * 12
Multiply the fraction by the total quantity to find the part.
3
12 / 4 = 3
Divide the total quantity by the denominator and multiply by the numerator (which is 1 here).
4
3 ounces
State the final answer with units.

Answer: 3 ounces (Option B)
