Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A jacket originally priced at $80 is now on sale for $68. What is the percent decrease in the price of the jacket?
A. 15%
B. 0.15%
C. 17.7%
D. 12%
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
$80 - $68 = $12
First, calculate the amount of decrease by subtracting the new price from the original price.
2
($12 / $80) 100% = 0.15 100% = 15%
Next, divide the amount of decrease by the original price. Then, multiply the result by 100 to express it as a percentage.

Answer: 15% (Option A)
