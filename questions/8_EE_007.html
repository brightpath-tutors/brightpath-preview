Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A baker uses 'b' bags of flour for 5 batches of cookies. Each bag contains 2.5 pounds of flour. Write an expression that represents the total amount of flour used for 5 batches of cookies.
A. 2.5 / b
B. 5b
C. 2.5b
D. b + 2.5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of bags of flour = b, Amount of flour per bag = 2.5 pounds.
We are given the variable for bags of flour and the weight per bag.
2
To find the total amount of flour, multiply the number of bags by the amount of flour per bag.
Total amount is found by multiplying the unit amount by the number of units.
3
Total flour = 2.5 * b = 2.5b
The expression 2.5b represents the total amount of flour in pounds.

Answer: 2.5b (Option C)

B. 10p

D. 3p + 7
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
First half points = p, Second half points = 2p + 7.
We are given expressions for points in each half.
2
Total points = (p) + (2p + 7)
To find the total, we add the points from both halves.

B. c + 3
C. 7c + 3
D. 12c - 10
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Chocolate for cake = 4c - 2, Chocolate for brownies = 3c + 5.
We are given expressions for the amount of chocolate used for each.
2
Total chocolate = (4c - 2) + (3c + 5)
To find the total, we add the amounts used for the cake and brownies.

Question 4Grade 8 • EE
A car travels for 't' hours at a speed of 65 mph. Then it travels for 't - 2' hours at a speed of 70 mph. Write an expression that represents the total distance traveled.
A. 135t + 140
B. 70t - 130
C. 135t - 140
D. 135t - 2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Distance = speed × time. Distance1 = 65t.
Using the formula d = rt for the first leg of the journey.
2
Distance2 = 70(t - 2).
Using the formula d = rt for the second leg, substituting the given speed and time expression.
3
70(t - 2) = 70t - 70 * 2 = 70t - 140.
Multiply 70 by each term inside the parentheses.
4
Total distance = Distance1 + Distance2 = 65t + (70t - 140).
Add the expressions for the two distances.
5
65t + 70t - 140 = 135t - 140.
Combine the 't' terms: 65t + 70t = 135t. The constant term -140 remains.

Answer: 135t - 140 (Option C)

Question 8Grade 8 • EE

B. (3.50 + 12.75)(m + n) - 5
C. 3.50m + 12.75n - 5
D. 3.50(m - 5) + 12.75n
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost of comic books = 3.50m.
Multiply the price per comic by the number of comics.
2
Cost of graphic novels = 12.75n.
Multiply the price per novel by the number of novels.
3
Total cost before discount = 3.50m + 12.75n.

Total cost = 3.50m + 12.75n - 5.

Question 9Grade 8 • EE
A population of 'P' deer doubles every 5 years. Write an expression that represents the deer population after 't' years, where 't' is a multiple of 5.
A. P * (t/5)
B. P + 2^(t/5)
C. P * 2^(t/5)
D. P * 2t
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial population = P, Doubling period = 5 years.
We are given the initial population and how often it doubles.
2
Number of doubling periods = t / 5.
If it doubles every 5 years, in 't' years it will double t/5 times.
3
Population after 't' years = P 2^(number of doubling periods) = P 2^(t/5).
The population grows exponentially, so we use the initial population multiplied by 2 raised to the power of the number of doubling periods.

Answer: P * 2^(t/5) (Option C)

Question 10Grade 8 • EE
A technician charges '45 + 25h' dollars for repairing a device, where 'h' is the number of hours worked. If they also charge a '10% service fee' on the labor cost (25h), write an expression for the total bill.
A. 45 + 25h + 10
B. 45 + 25h + 0.10
C. 45 + 27.5h
D. 1.10(45 + 25h)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Base fee = $45, Labor cost = 25h dollars.
The initial expression is 45 + 25h.
2
Service fee = 10% of 25h = 0.10 * 25h = 2.5h.
Convert the percentage to a decimal and multiply by the labor cost.
3
Total bill = (45 + 25h) + 2.5h.
The service fee is an additional charge.
4
45 + 25h + 2.5h = 45 + 27.5h.
Combine the 'h' terms: 25h + 2.5h = 27.5h. The constant term 45 remains.

Answer: 45 + 27.5h (Option C)
