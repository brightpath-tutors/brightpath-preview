Question 1Grade 7 • NS
The temperature in Anchorage is -5 degrees Celsius, and the temperature in Fairbanks is -10 degrees Celsius. Which city has a colder temperature?
A. Anchorage
B. Both are the same
C. It depends on the time of day
D. Fairbanks
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
On a number line, numbers to the left are colder (smaller).
Colder temperatures are represented by smaller numbers. On a number line, -10 is to the left of -5.
2
-5 degrees C vs -10 degrees C
We need to determine which of these two negative numbers is smaller.
3
-10 < -5
Since -10 is less than -5, Fairbanks has a colder temperature.

Answer: Fairbanks (Option D)

Question 2Grade 7 • NS
During a game, a football team gained 15 yards on one play and lost 8 yards on another play. Which play resulted in a greater change in field position (magnitude)?
A. The gain of 15 yards
B. It depends on the starting position
C. The loss of 8 yards
D. Both are the same
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Gain: +15 yards; Loss: -8 yards
A gain is positive, a loss is negative.
2
Magnitude of +15 is |+15| = 15; Magnitude of -8 is |-8| = 8
Magnitude refers to the absolute value, or the distance from zero, regardless of direction.
3
15 > 8
Since 15 is greater than 8, the gain of 15 yards resulted in a greater change in field position.

Answer: The gain of 15 yards (Option A)
