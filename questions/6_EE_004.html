B. 14
C. 18
D. 21
E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
The problem asks for the total number of stickers Sarah bought. She bought 3 packs, and each pack has two types of stickers.
Identify the quantities and what the question is asking for.
2
We can express this as 3 multiplied by the sum of 4 and 2: 3(4 + 2).
Set up the expression using the given numbers and the distributive property concept.
3
Multiply 3 by each number inside the parentheses: (3 × 4) + (3 × 2).
