Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A store sells a shirt for $15 and a pair of pants for $22. If a customer buys s shirts and p pairs of pants, the total cost is given by the expression 15s + 22p. Calculate the total cost if a customer buys 2 shirts and 1 pair of pants.
A. $40
B. $52
C. $59
D. $37
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Given expression: 15s + 22p Given values: s = 2, p = 1 Substitute: 15(2) + 22(1)
Replace 's' with 2 and 'p' with 1 in the expression.
2
15 * 2 = 30 22 * 1 = 22 So, the expression becomes: 30 + 22
Multiplication takes precedence over addition. Calculate the product of each term.
3
30 + 22 = 52
Add the results of the multiplication to find the total cost.

Answer: $52 (Option B)

B. 210 miles
C. 270 miles
D. 170 miles
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1

Replace 'v' with 50 and 't' with 3 in the expression.

4

2

The battery life (in hours) of a new smartphone is modeled by the expression B(t) = 50 - 2t^2 / (t + 1) where t is the age of the phone in years. Calculate the battery life for a phone that is t = 2 years old. Round to two decimal places.
A. 44.67 hours
B. 48.67 hours
C. 47 hours
D. 47.33 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Given expression: 50 - 2t^2 / (t + 1) Given value: t = 2 Substitute: 50 - 2(2)^2 / (2 + 1)
Replace 't' with 2 in the expression.
2
(2 + 1) = 3 2^2 = 4 So, the expression becomes: 50 - 2(4) / 3
According to the order of operations, parentheses are evaluated first, then exponents.
3
2 * 4 = 8 So, the expression becomes: 50 - 8 / 3
Perform multiplication before division or subtraction.
4
8 / 3 ≈ 2.6666... So, the expression becomes: 50 - 2.6666...
Perform division before subtraction.
5
50 - 2.6666... = 47.3333... Rounded to two decimal places: 47.33
Subtract the values and round the final result as instructed.

Answer: 47.33 hours (Option D)
