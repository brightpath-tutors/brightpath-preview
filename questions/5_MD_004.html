Question 1Grade 5 • MD
A fabric store recorded the lengths of ribbon pieces sold in yards, as shown on the line plot below. `` X X X X X X <---|---|---|--- 1/4 1/2 3/4 (Yards) `` If a customer bought all the 1/4 yard ribbons and one of the 1/2 yard ribbons shown, what is the total length of ribbon purchased?
A. 3/4 yards
B. 1 yard
C. 2/3 yards
D. 1 1/4 yards
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Number of 1/4 yard ribbons = 3 Number of 1/2 yard ribbons bought = 1
Identify the quantities of each ribbon length purchased from the line plot based on the problem description.
2
Total length of 1/4 yard ribbons = 3 * (1/4) yard = 3/4 yard
Calculate the total length for the 1/4 yard ribbons by multiplying the length by the number of pieces.
3
Total length of 1/2 yard ribbons = 1 * (1/2) yard = 1/2 yard
Calculate the total length for the 1/2 yard ribbons by multiplying the length by the number of pieces.
4
Combined total length = 3/4 yard + 1/2 yard
Add the total lengths of both types of ribbons purchased.
5
Find a common denominator for 3/4 and 1/2. The common denominator is 4. 1/2 = 2/4
Convert 1/2 to an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 4.
6
Combined total length = 3/4 + 2/4 = 5/4 yards
Add the fractions with the common denominator.
7
Convert the improper fraction to a mixed number: 5/4 = 1 1/4 yards
Divide the numerator by the denominator to express the answer as a mixed number in simplest form.

Answer: 1 1/4 yards (Option D)

A. 5/8 minutes
B. 1 7/16 minutes
C. 2 1/2 minutes
D. 23/40 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Counts from the line plot: 1/4 minute: 3 runners 1/2 minute: 2 runners 3/4 minute: 4 runners 1 minute: 1 runner
Identify the time and the number of runners for each data point from the line plot.
2
Total number of runners = 3 + 2 + 4 + 1 = 10 runners
Sum the number of 'X's on the line plot to find the total number of runners.
3
Total time for 1/4 min runners = 3 * (1/4) = 3/4 minutes Total time for 1/2 min runners = 2 * (1/2) = 1 minute Total time for 3/4 min runners = 4 * (3/4) = 3 minutes Total time for 1 min runners = 1 * (1) = 1 minute

4
Overall total time = 3/4 + 1 + 3 + 1 = 3/4 + 5 = 5 3/4 minutes
Sum all the individual total times to get the overall total time spent by all runners.
5

7
Average time = 23/4 * (1/10) = 23/40 minutes
Multiply by the reciprocal of the divisor to perform the division. The fraction 23/40 is in simplest form.
