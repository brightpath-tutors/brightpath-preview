Question 1Grade 7 • EE
A grocery store received a shipment of items. They received 3c cartons of cereal, 5b boxes of pasta, 2c more cartons of cereal, and 4b more boxes of pasta. Write an expression in simplest form to represent the total number of items received.
A. 5c + b
B. 14cb
C. 14(c + b)
D. 5c + 9b
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Like terms are terms that have the same variable raised to the same power. In this expression, 3c and 2c are like terms because they both have the variable c. 5b and 4b are like terms because they both have the variable b.
To combine like terms, first identify which terms can be grouped together.
2
(3c + 2c) + (5b + 4b)
Rearrange the expression to place like terms next to each other, using parentheses for clarity.
3
(3 + 2)c = 5c
Add the numerical coefficients of the terms that have the variable c.
4
(5 + 4)b = 9b
Add the numerical coefficients of the terms that have the variable b.
5
5c + 9b
Combine the results from step 3 and step 4 to get the final simplified expression.

Answer: 5c + 9b (Option D)

Question 9Grade 7 • EE
A wildlife researcher is tracking populations. There are 150a deer, 75b bears, and 200c coyotes in a region. Over a month, 30a deer migrated out, 15b bears were born, and 50c coyotes were culled. Write an expression in simplest form for the current population of these animals.
A. 120a + 90b + 150c
B. 120a + 60b + 150c
C. 360abc
D. 180a + 90b + 250c
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Like terms are 150a and -30a (terms with 'a'), 75b and 15b (terms with 'b'), and 200c and -50c (terms with 'c').
Identify and group terms that represent the same type of animal.
2
(150a - 30a) + (75b + 15b) + (200c - 50c)
Arrange the expression by grouping similar animal populations for easier calculation.
3
(150 - 30)a = 120a
Subtract the number of deer that migrated out from the initial population.
4
(75 + 15)b = 90b
Add the number of bears born to the initial population.
5
(200 - 50)c = 150c
Subtract the number of coyotes culled from the initial population.
6
120a + 90b + 150c
Combine the results of each animal group to form the final expression.

Answer: 120a + 90b + 150c (Option A)
