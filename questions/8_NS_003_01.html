Sarah bought a half-price item. If the item's original price was a whole number of dollars, what is the decimal representation of the fraction 1/2 that she paid?

B. 0.05
C. 2.0
D. 0.5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fraction = 1/2
Identify the fraction that needs to be converted to a decimal.
2
Divide numerator by denominator: 1 ÷ 2
To convert a fraction to a decimal, perform long division by dividing the numerator by the denominator.
3
Set up long division: 2 into 1.0
Since 1 is less than 2, place a 0 in the quotient, add a decimal point, and append a zero to the dividend to make it 1.0.
4
10 ÷ 2 = 5
Divide 10 by 2, which results in 5. Place this 5 after the decimal point in the quotient.
5
2 × 5 = 10; 10 - 10 = 0
Multiply 2 by 5 and subtract the product from 10. The remainder is 0, indicating the division terminates.
6
Result: 0.5
The decimal representation of 1/2 is 0.5.

Answer: 0.5 (Option D)
