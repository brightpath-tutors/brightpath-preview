Question 1Grade 6 • NS
A store decreases the price of a certain item by $3 each week for 5 weeks. What is the total change in price over these 5 weeks?
A. $15
B. -$2
C. -$15
D. $2
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Decrease in price per week = -$3 Number of weeks = 5
A decrease in price is represented by a negative integer. So, -$3 is the change per week.
2
Total change = (change per week) × (number of weeks) Total change = -3 × 5
To find the total change, multiply the change per week by the number of weeks.
3
-3 × 5 = -15
When multiplying a negative integer by a positive integer, the product is negative. 3 × 5 = 15, so -3 × 5 = -15.
4
The total change in price is -$15.
The negative sign indicates a decrease in price.

Answer: -$15 (Option C)
