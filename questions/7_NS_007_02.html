Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Maria had a balance of $25.50 in her bank account. She then made a transaction that changed her balance by -$15.75. What is her new account balance?
A. $41.25
B. $10.25
C. $9.75
D. -$9.75
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial balance = $25.50, Change in balance = -$15.75
Identify the initial bank account balance and the change in balance. A transaction that changes the balance by a negative amount indicates a withdrawal or decrease.
2
New balance = 25.50 + (-15.75)
To find the new balance, add the change in balance (a negative decimal) to the initial positive balance.
3
25.50 - 15.75
Adding a negative number is equivalent to subtracting its absolute value from the positive number.
4
9.75
Perform the subtraction. Align the decimal points and subtract column by column, borrowing as needed.
5
New balance = $9.75
Since the initial positive balance was greater than the absolute value of the negative change, the result is positive. State the final answer with the correct currency unit.

Answer: $9.75 (Option C)

A. 2.0 strokes
B. 1.0 strokes
C. -1.0 strokes
D. -2.0 strokes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Initial score = 0, First hole = -0.5, Second hole = +1.5
Identify the initial score and the stroke changes for each hole. A negative score is below par, and a positive score is above par.
2
Total score = 0 + (-0.5) + 1.5
Add the stroke changes from each hole to the initial score to find the total score relative to par.
3
Total score = -0.5 + 1.5
Simplify the expression by combining the numbers. Adding 0 does not change the value.
4
|1.5| - |-0.5| = 1.5 - 0.5 = 1.0
When adding a negative number and a positive number, subtract their absolute values.
5
Total score = 1.0
Assign the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Since |1.5| > |-0.5|, the result is positive. State the final score.
