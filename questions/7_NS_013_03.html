Question 1Grade 7 • NS
A customer returns a damaged item. The store policy states that a penalty factor of -0.25 will be applied to the item's original price for the refund. If the item's original price was $16.00, what is the resulting change in refund amount due to the penalty?
A. $15.75
B. $4.00
C. -$4.00
D. $64.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Original price = $16.00, Penalty factor = -0.25
Identify the given values for the original price and the penalty factor.
2
Calculation: $16.00 \times (-0.25)
To find the resulting change in refund amount, multiply the original price by the penalty factor.
3
Multiply the magnitudes: $16 \times 0.25 = 4.00$
First, multiply the absolute values of the numbers.
4
Apply the sign rule: Positive \times Negative = Negative, so $4.00 \times (-0.25) = -4.00$
Since one number is positive and the other is negative, the product will be negative.
5
Final answer: -$4.00
State the final change in the refund amount.

Answer: -$4.00 (Option C)

Question 8Grade 7 • NS
A grandfather clock is malfunctioning. It begins to lose time at a rate of -1 1/3 minutes per hour. If this continues for 5 3/4 hours, what is the total amount of time lost?
A. 7 2/3 minutes
B. -2 1/5 minutes
C. 1.1 minutes
D. -7 2/3 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Rate of time loss = -1 1/3 minutes/hour, Duration = 5 3/4 hours
Identify the given rate of time loss and the duration.
2
Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions: -1 1/3 = -(1 \times 3 + 1)/3 = -4/3, and 5 3/4 = (5 \times 4 + 3)/4 = 23/4
Convert both mixed numbers to improper fractions for multiplication.
3
Calculation: (-4/3) \times (23/4)
Multiply the rate of time loss by the duration to find the total time lost.
4
Multiply numerators and denominators: (-4 \times 23) / (3 \times 4) = -92 / 12
Multiply the numerators together and the denominators together.
5
Simplify the fraction: -92 / 12 = -23 / 3 (divide by 4)
Simplify the fraction by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor, which is 4.
6
Convert improper fraction to mixed number: -23/3 = -7 2/3
Convert the improper fraction back into a mixed number for the final answer.
7
Final answer: -7 2/3 minutes
State the total amount of time lost.
