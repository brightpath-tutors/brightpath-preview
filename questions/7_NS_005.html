Question 1Grade 7 • NS
Sarah's bank account currently has a balance of $0. She makes a deposit of $25 and then makes a withdrawal of $10. If her account balance is represented on a number line, what integer represents her final balance?
A. 10
B. -15
C. 35
D. 15
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
0 + 25 = 25
Deposits increase the balance, so we add.
2
25 - 10 = 15
Withdrawals decrease the balance, so we subtract.
3
Final balance = 15
The integer representing her final balance is 15.

Question 8Grade 7 • NS
A construction worker is digging a foundation that goes 12 feet below ground level. The top of the building will be 50 feet above ground level. If ground level is represented by 0 on a number line, what is the vertical distance between the deepest part of the foundation and the top of the building?
A. 62
B. 38
C. 50
D. -62
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Deepest foundation = -12 feet, Top of building = +50 feet
Below ground is negative, above ground is positive.
2
Distance = Top of building - Deepest foundation
Distance is always positive. On a number line, it's the absolute difference between the two coordinates.
3
50 - (-12) = 50 + 12 = 62
Subtracting a negative number is the same as adding its positive counterpart.
4
Vertical distance = 62 feet
The final answer is 62.

Answer: 62 (Option A)
