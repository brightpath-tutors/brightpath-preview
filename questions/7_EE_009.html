Question 1Grade 7 • EE
Sarah bought a book for $12 and 3 pens. She spent a total of $21. How much did each pen cost?
A. $9
B. $3
C. $7
D. $1
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
12 + 3p = 21
The total cost ($21) is the sum of the fixed cost of the book ($12) and the variable cost of 3 pens (3p).
2
3p = 21 - 12 3p = 9
To isolate the term with the variable (3p), perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.
3
p = 9 / 3 p = 3
To solve for p, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: $3 (Option B)

Question 2Grade 7 • EE
A runner completed a race in 25 minutes. This was 5 minutes less than twice his practice time. How long was his practice time?
A. 30 minutes
B. 15 minutes
C. 12.5 minutes
D. 10 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2x - 5 = 25
The race time (25 minutes) is 5 minutes less than twice the practice time (2x).
2
2x = 25 + 5 2x = 30
To isolate the term with the variable (2x), perform the inverse operation of subtraction, which is addition.
3
x = 30 / 2 x = 15
To solve for x, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: 15 minutes (Option B)

Question 3Grade 7 • EE
A recipe calls for 4 cups of sugar. This is 1 cup more than half the amount of flour needed. How much flour is needed?
A. 2 cups
B. 6 cups
C. 5 cups
D. 8 cups
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
0.5f + 1 = 4
The amount of sugar (4 cups) is 1 cup more than half the amount of flour (0.5f).
2
0.5f = 4 - 1 0.5f = 3
To isolate the term with the variable (0.5f), perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.
3
f = 3 * 2 f = 6
To solve for f, perform the inverse operation of multiplying by 0.5 (or 1/2), which is multiplying by 2.

Answer: 6 cups (Option B)

Question 4Grade 7 • EE
A bus traveled for 3 hours at a constant speed. It then increased its speed by 10 mph for the remaining 2 hours of its journey. The total distance covered was 230 miles. What was its initial speed in mph?
A. 46 mph
B. 50 mph
C. 42 mph
D. 40 mph
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
3s + 2(s + 10) = 230
The total distance (230 miles) is the sum of the distance traveled in the first 3 hours (3s) and the distance traveled in the next 2 hours at an increased speed (2 * (s + 10)).
2
3s + 2s + 20 = 230
Apply the distributive property to remove the parentheses.
3
5s + 20 = 230
Combine the 's' terms.
4
5s = 230 - 20 5s = 210
To isolate the term with the variable (5s), perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.
5
s = 210 / 5 s = 42
To solve for s, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: 42 mph (Option C)

Question 5Grade 7 • EE
A teacher distributed 71 pencils to her students. Each of her 28 regular students received 2 pencils. The remaining pencils were given to a special club, where each member received 5 pencils. How many members are in the special club?
A. 3 members
B. 4 members
C. 2 members
D. 14 members
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
2 * 28 + 5x = 71
The total pencils (71) is the sum of pencils for regular students (2 * 28) and pencils for club members (5x).
2
56 + 5x = 71
Perform the multiplication first according to the order of operations.
3
5x = 71 - 56 5x = 15
To isolate the term with the variable (5x), perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.
4
x = 15 / 5 x = 3
To solve for x, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: 3 members (Option A)

Question 6Grade 7 • EE
A scientist is tracking a chemical reaction. The temperature started at 20°C and decreased by 3°C every minute. After a certain time, the temperature was -1°C. How many minutes passed?
A. 8 minutes
B. 6 minutes
C. 7 minutes
D. 21 minutes
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
20 - 3t = -1
The final temperature (-1°C) is the initial temperature (20°C) minus the total decrease in temperature (3°C per minute for 't' minutes).
2
-3t = -1 - 20 -3t = -21
To isolate the term with the variable (-3t), perform the inverse operation of addition (the 20 is positive), which is subtraction.
3
t = -21 / -3 t = 7
To solve for t, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division. Dividing two negative numbers results in a positive number.

Answer: 7 minutes (Option C)

Question 7Grade 7 • EE
A construction worker has a 120-inch wooden beam. He makes 3 cuts, and each cut wastes 1 inch of material. If he ends up with 4 equal pieces, what is the length of each piece?
A. 39 inches
B. 29 inches
C. 30 inches
D. 29.25 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
120 - (3 * 1) = 4x
The total length of the pieces (4x) is the original beam length (120 inches) minus the total material wasted by cuts (3 cuts * 1 inch/cut).
2
120 - 3 = 4x
Perform the multiplication inside the parentheses first.
3
117 = 4x
Simplify the left side of the equation.
4
x = 117 / 4 x = 29.25
To solve for x, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: 29.25 inches (Option D)

3
r = 35 * 2 r = 70

Question 9Grade 7 • EE
The population of a town increased by 1500 people over 5 years. If the population then decreased by 200 people each year for the next 3 years, and the current population is 12,900, what was the initial population?
A. 12,000
B. 12,300
C. 11,400
D. 13,800
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
P + 1500 - (3 * 200) = 12900
The current population (12,900) is the initial population (P) plus the total increase (1500) minus the total decrease (3 years * 200 people/year).
2
P + 1500 - 600 = 12900
Perform the multiplication inside the parentheses first.
3
P + 900 = 12900
Simplify the left side of the equation by combining the numerical values.
4
P = 12900 - 900 P = 12000
To solve for P, perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.

Answer: 12,000 people (Option A)

Question 10Grade 7 • EE
A patient needs to lose 30 pounds. They lost 5 pounds in the first month, and then continued to lose 2.5 pounds per week. How many weeks did it take them to lose the remaining weight after the first month?
A. 12 weeks
B. 25 weeks
C. 14 weeks
D. 10 weeks
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
5 + 2.5w = 30
The total weight to lose (30 pounds) is the sum of the initial weight lost (5 pounds) and the weight lost per week (2.5w).
2
2.5w = 30 - 5 2.5w = 25
To isolate the term with the variable (2.5w), perform the inverse operation of addition, which is subtraction.
3
w = 25 / 2.5 w = 10
To solve for w, perform the inverse operation of multiplication, which is division.

Answer: 10 weeks (Option D)
