2

E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution

Initial amount = 1 1/3 cups Added amount = 2/5 cups
Identify the initial and added amounts of broth.
2
1 1/3 + 2/5
Set up the addition problem.
3

Find the least common multiple (LCM) of the denominators (3 and 5) to find a common denominator.
