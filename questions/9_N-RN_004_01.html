Question 1Grade 9 • N-RN
A store offers a discount on an item. The price reduction is equivalent to calculating (81)^(1/4) of the original price. What is this value?
A. 3
B. 20.25
C. 9
D. 4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
(81)^(1/4)
The problem asks to evaluate the expression 81 raised to the power of 1/4.
2
81^(1/4) = 4√81
Convert the rational exponent to a radical expression. The denominator of the exponent (4) becomes the index of the radical, and the numerator (1) remains the exponent of the radicand.
3
4√81 = 3
Find the fourth root of 81. This means finding a number that, when multiplied by itself four times, equals 81. Since 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 = 81, the fourth root of 81 is 3.

Answer: 3 (Option A)

√25 = 5

Question 9Grade 9 • N-RN
The projected growth of a rare plant population is given by (4^(1/3) * 4^(1/6))^(12) individuals. Calculate the total growth.
A. 4096 individuals
B. 16384 individuals
C. 32 individuals
D. 512 individuals
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
(4^(1/3) * 4^(1/6))^(12)
The expression represents the plant population growth. First, simplify the terms inside the parentheses.
2
4^(1/3) * 4^(1/6) = 4^(1/3 + 1/6)
Apply the product rule for exponents: x^m * x^n = x^(m+n). Add the exponents.
3
1/3 + 1/6 = 2/6 + 1/6 = 3/6 = 1/2
Find a common denominator for the fractions and add them.
4
(4^(1/2))^(12)
Substitute the simplified exponent back into the expression.
5
4^(1/2 * 12)
Apply the power of a power rule: (x^m)^n = x^(m*n). Multiply the exponents.
6
1/2 * 12 = 6
Perform the multiplication of the exponents.
7
4^6 = 4096
Calculate 4 raised to the power of 6 (4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 × 4 = 4096).

Answer: 4096 individuals (Option A)
