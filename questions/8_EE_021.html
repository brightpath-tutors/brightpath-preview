Question 1Grade 8 • EE
A small cube-shaped gift box has a volume of 8 cubic inches. What is the length of one side of the box?
A. 4 inches
B. 8 inches
C. 16 inches
D. 2 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
For a cube, Volume = side side side = side^3.
The volume of a cube is found by cubing its side length.
2
8 = s^3
We are given the volume (8 cubic inches) and need to find the side length (s).
3
s = \(\sqrt[3]{8}\) s = 2
To find the side length, take the cube root of the volume. The cube root of 8 is 2, because 2 2 2 = 8.
4
The length of one side of the box is 2 inches.
Remember to include the appropriate units for length.

Answer: 2 inches (Option D)
