Question 1Grade 6 • RP
Marcus bought 12 pencils for $4.80. What is the cost per pencil?
A. $57.60/pencil
B. $0.40/pencil
C. $0.04/pencil
D. $2.50/pencil
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost = $4.80, Number of pencils = 12
We need to find the cost for one pencil, which means dollars per pencil.
2
Unit Rate = Total Cost / Number of Pencils = $4.80 / 12
To find the cost per one item, divide the total cost by the number of items.
3
$4.80 ÷ 12 = $0.40
Dividing $4.80 by 12 gives us $0.40. So, each pencil costs $0.40.

Answer: $0.40/pencil (Option B)
