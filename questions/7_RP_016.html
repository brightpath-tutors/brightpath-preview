Question 1Grade 7 • RP
Emily put $500 into a savings account that earns 3% simple interest per year. How much interest will she earn in 2 years?

B. $15.00
C. $300.00
D. $30.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Principal (P) = $500 Annual Interest Rate (R) = 3% = 0.03 Time (T) = 2 years
Extract the principal amount, the annual interest rate, and the time period from the problem statement. Remember to convert the percentage rate to a decimal by dividing by 100.
2
I = 500 × 0.03 × 2 I = 15 × 2 I = 30
Multiply the principal by the decimal rate and the time in years to find the simple interest earned.
3
Interest (I) = $30.00
The total simple interest Emily will earn in 2 years is $30.00.

Answer: $30.00 (Option D)

Question 5Grade 7 • RP
A technology investor wants to earn $3,600 in simple interest from an investment of $9,000 at an 8% annual interest rate. How many years will it take to earn this interest?
A. 50 years
B. 0.5 years
C. 2.5 years
D. 5 years
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Interest (I) = $3,600 Principal (P) = $9,000 Annual Interest Rate (R) = 8% = 0.08
Extract the interest earned, the principal amount, and the annual interest rate. Convert the percentage rate to a decimal.
2
T = I / (P × R)
To find the time, divide the interest by the product of the principal and the rate.
3
T = 3600 / (9000 × 0.08) T = 3600 / 720 T = 5
Perform the multiplication in the denominator first, then divide the interest by that product.
4
Time (T) = 5 years
It will take 5 years to earn $3,600 in simple interest.

Answer: 5 years (Option D)

Question 10Grade 7 • RP
Maria bought a new refrigerator using a store credit plan. She paid a total of $1,440 after 18 months, which included a simple interest rate of 8% per year. What was the original price of the refrigerator (principal)? Round to the nearest cent.
A. $1,324.80
B. $1,285.71
C. $1,296.00
D. $1,200.00
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total Amount (A) = $1,440 Annual Interest Rate (R) = 8% = 0.08 Time (T) = 18 months = 18 / 12 years = 1.5 years
Extract the total amount paid, the annual interest rate (converted to decimal), and the time. Convert time from months to years.
2
P = A / (1 + RT)
Rearrange the formula to isolate the principal (P).
3
1 + RT = 1 + (0.08 × 1.5) 1 + RT = 1 + 0.12 1 + RT = 1.12
Multiply the rate by the time, then add 1 to the product. This accounts for both the principal and the interest factor.
4
P = 1440 / 1.12 P ≈ 1285.71428... P ≈ 1285.71
Divide the total amount by the calculated (1 + RT) value. Round the result to two decimal places for currency.
5
Original Price (P) = $1,285.71
The original price of the refrigerator was approximately $1,285.71.

Answer: $1,285.71 (Option B)
