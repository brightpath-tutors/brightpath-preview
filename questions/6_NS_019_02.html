A. 0.40 foot
B. 0.50 foot
C. 0.80 foot
D. 0.25 foot
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total length = 4 feet, Number of pieces = 8
Identify the total length of the dough and the number of pieces needed.
2
Length per piece = 4 feet / 8 pieces = 0.5 feet
Divide the total length by the number of pieces to find the length of each piece.
3
0.5 feet = 0.50 feet
Express the length as a decimal with two decimal places for easier plotting on intervals of 0.25. The length of each piece is 0.50 feet.

Answer: 0.50 foot (Option B)

Question 4Grade 6 • NS
A chef has 3/4 of a pound of spices. He wants to divide the spices equally among 6 different dishes. How much spice will each dish receive, expressed as a fraction in simplest form, and plot this amount on a number line that is divided into 1/8 pound intervals?
A. 3/24 pound
B. 1/6 pound
C. 1/8 pound
D. 1/4 pound
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total spices = 3/4 pound, Number of dishes = 6
Identify the total amount of spices and the number of dishes.
2
Spices per dish = (3/4) / 6 = (3/4) * (1/6)
To divide a fraction by a whole number, multiply the fraction by the reciprocal of the whole number.
3
(3/4) * (1/6) = 3/24
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
4
3/24 = 1/8
Simplify the fraction by dividing both the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD), which is 3. Each dish will receive 1/8 pound of spice.

Answer: 1/8 pound (Option C)

Question 8Grade 6 • NS
A construction worker needs to cut a metal beam that is 17 1/2 feet long into 5 equal sections. What will be the length of each section, expressed as a mixed number in simplest form, and plot this length on a number line that is divided into 1/4 foot intervals?
A. 3 1/2 feet
B. 35/10 feet
C. 17 1/10 feet
D. 8 3/4 feet
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total length = 17 1/2 feet, Number of sections = 5
Identify the total length of the beam and the number of sections.
2
Convert mixed number to improper fraction: 17 1/2 = (17 * 2 + 1) / 2 = 35/2 feet
Convert the mixed number to an improper fraction for easier division.
3
Length per section = (35/2) / 5 = (35/2) * (1/5)
To divide a fraction by a whole number, multiply the fraction by the reciprocal of the whole number.
4
(35/2) * (1/5) = 35/10
Multiply the numerators and the denominators.
5
Simplify the fraction: 35/10 = 7/2
Simplify the fraction by dividing both the numerator and denominator by their GCD, which is 5.
6
Convert improper fraction to mixed number: 7/2 = 3 1/2
Convert the improper fraction back to a mixed number. Each section will be 3 1/2 feet long.

Answer: 3 1/2 feet (Option A)
