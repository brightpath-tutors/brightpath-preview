A. 3/2 inches
B. 1/2 inch
C. 3/6 inches
D. 1 1/2 inches
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Length of one piece = 1/2 inch
Identify the length of the ribbon pieces in question.
2
Number of pieces = 3
Count the number of 'X' marks above the 1/2 inch mark on the line plot.
3
Total length = 1/2 * 3
Multiply the length of one piece by the number of pieces.
4
Total length = 3/2 inches
Perform the multiplication.
5
Total length = 1 1/2 inches
Convert the improper fraction to a mixed number.

Answer: 1 1/2 inches (Option D)
