D. 3/8
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1

To find the total distance completed, we need to add the two fractions.
2

1

It's often easier to add the whole number parts of mixed numbers first.
3

E. None of the above

Step-by-step solution
1
Wood for first shelf = 2 3/4 feet Wood for second shelf = 1 7/8 feet
To find the total amount of wood used, we add the two mixed numbers.
2
2 + 1 = 3
Start by adding the whole number parts.
3
Fractions are 3/4 and 7/8. The least common multiple of 4 and 8 is 8. Convert 3/4 to an equivalent fraction with denominator 8: (3 2) / (4 2) = 6/8.
The fractions need a common denominator before adding. We convert 3/4 to 6/8.
4
6/8 + 7/8 = (6 + 7)/8 = 13/8

Question 8Grade 6 • NS

A. -2.3 degrees Celsius
B. -4.7 degrees Celsius
C. 4.7 degrees Celsius
D. 2.3 degrees Celsius
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Drop in temperature = -3.5 degrees Celsius Rise in temperature = +1.2 degrees Celsius
A drop indicates a negative change, and a rise indicates a positive change.
2
-3.5 + 1.2
To find the net change, we add the two temperature changes.
3
When adding numbers with different signs, subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value and keep the sign of the number with the larger absolute value. Absolute value of -3.5 is 3.5. Absolute value of 1.2 is 1.2. 3.5 - 1.2 = 2.3. Since 3.5 has a larger absolute value and is negative, the result is negative. Result = -2.3
Align the decimal points, subtract, and apply the correct sign.

Answer: -2.3 degrees Celsius (Option A)
