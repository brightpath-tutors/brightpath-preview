Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A cashier counted the following bills: 5 one-dollar bills and 3 five-dollar bills. What is the ratio of one-dollar bills to five-dollar bills, in simplest form?
A. 5 : 8
B. 8 : 5
C. 5 : 3
D. 3 : 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
One-dollar bills = 5, Five-dollar bills = 3
Identify the number of each type of bill.
2
Ratio of one-dollar bills to five-dollar bills = 5 : 3
Write the ratio in the requested order (part-to-part).
3
GCD(5, 3) = 1
Determine the greatest common divisor (GCD) to check for simplification. Since the GCD is 1, the ratio is already in simplest form.

Answer: 5 : 3 (Option C)

Question 2Grade 6 • RP
A local soccer team played 10 games, winning 6 of them and losing the rest. What is the ratio of games won to the total number of games played, in simplest form?
A. 6 : 10
B. 6 : 4
C. 5 : 3
D. 3 : 5
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Games won = 6, Total games played = 10
Identify the number of games won and the total number of games played.
2
Ratio of games won to total games = 6 : 10
Write the ratio in the requested order (part-to-whole).
3
GCD(6, 10) = 2
Determine the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 6 and 10 to simplify the ratio.
4
Simplified ratio = (6 ÷ 2) : (10 ÷ 2) = 3 : 5
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCD to express it in simplest form.

Answer: 3 : 5 (Option D)

Question 8Grade 6 • RP
A student tracks their daily activities for 24 hours: 8 hours for sleeping, 6 hours for school, 4 hours for studying, and the rest for leisure. What is the ratio of leisure time to total time, in simplest form?
A. 1 : 3
B. 1 : 4
C. 6 : 24
D. 4 : 1
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total time = 24 hours
Identify the total time in a day.
2
Time for sleeping = 8 hours, Time for school = 6 hours, Time for studying = 4 hours
Identify the time spent on specific activities.
3
Time spent on other activities = 8 + 6 + 4 = 18 hours
Sum the time spent on known activities.
4
Leisure time = Total time - Time spent on other activities = 24 - 18 = 6 hours
Calculate the leisure time by subtracting the sum of other activities from the total time.
5
Ratio of leisure time to total time = 6 : 24
Write the initial ratio in the requested order (part-to-whole).
6
GCD(6, 24) = 6
Determine the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 6 and 24 to simplify the ratio.
7
Simplified ratio = (6 ÷ 6) : (24 ÷ 6) = 1 : 4
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCD to express it in simplest form.

Answer: 1 : 6 (Option B)

Question 9Grade 6 • RP
In a small town, there are 1,200 adults, 800 children, and 500 seniors. What is the ratio of children to the total population of the town, in simplest form?
A. 8 : 17
B. 8 : 25
C. 800 : 2,500
D. 25 : 8
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Adults = 1,200, Children = 800, Seniors = 500
Identify the number of individuals in each demographic group.
2
Total population = 1,200 + 800 + 500 = 2,500
Calculate the total population of the town by summing all demographic groups.
3
Children = 800
Identify the number of children in the town.
4
Ratio of children to total population = 800 : 2,500
Write the initial ratio in the requested order (part-to-whole).
5
GCD(800, 2,500) = 100
Determine the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 800 and 2,500 to simplify the ratio. Recognizing that both numbers end in two zeros indicates a GCD of at least 100.
6
Simplified ratio = (800 ÷ 100) : (2,500 ÷ 100) = 8 : 25
Divide both parts of the ratio by their GCD to express it in simplest form.

Answer: 8 : 25 (Option B)
