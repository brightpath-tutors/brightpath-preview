Question 1Grade 4 • NF
Liam bought a comic book for $3/4 of a dollar. He also bought a sticker for $1/4 of a dollar. What operation would you use to find the total amount of money Liam spent?
A. Subtraction
B. Multiplication
C. Division
D. Addition
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Comic book cost = $3/4, Sticker cost = $1/4
Identify the costs of the two items Liam bought.
2
Total amount = Comic book cost + Sticker cost
To find the total amount spent, we need to combine the costs of the items. This means using addition.
3
Operation: Addition
The operation needed to combine the amounts is addition.

Answer: Addition (Option D)

Question 7Grade 4 • NF
A city's population grew by 1/5 in the last decade, and then decreased by 1/10 in the current decade. To find the net change in population, what operations would you use?
A. Subtraction only
B. Addition and Subtraction
C. Multiplication and Division
D. Addition only
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Growth = 1/5, Decrease = 1/10
Identify the change in population for each decade.
2
Net change = Growth - Decrease (or Growth + (-Decrease))
To find the net change, we need to consider both the increase and decrease. This means adding the positive change and subtracting the negative change (or vice versa).
3
Operations: Addition and Subtraction
To find the net effect of growth and decrease, both addition and subtraction are needed.

Answer: Addition and Subtraction (Option B)
