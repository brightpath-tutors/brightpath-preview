A. 5.2 km
B. 6.2 km
C. 6.12 km
D. 5.12 km
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Segment 1: 3.4 km Segment 2: 2.8 km
To find the total distance, we add the lengths of the two segments.
2
3.4 + 2.8 ------ 6.2
Align the decimal points vertically. Add the tenths column (4+8=12), write down 2 and carry over 1 to the ones column. Then add the ones column (3+2+1=6).
3
Total distance = 6.2 kilometers
The sum of 3.4 km and 2.8 km is 6.2 km.

Answer: 6.2 kilometers (Option B)

Question 3Grade 8 • NS
A recipe calls for 1/4 cup of sugar and 1/2 cup of brown sugar. What is the total amount of sugar needed, in simplest form?
A. 1/3
B. 2/6
C. 1/2
D. 3/4
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Fractions: 1/4 and 1/2
We need to find the total amount of sugar, so we add the two fractional amounts.
2
Denominators are 4 and 2. The least common multiple (LCM) of 4 and 2 is 4.
To add fractions, they must have the same denominator. The LCM is the smallest common denominator.
3
1/4 remains 1/4. 1/2 = (1 × 2) / (2 × 2) = 2/4
Multiply the numerator and denominator of 1/2 by 2 to get an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 4.
4
1/4 + 2/4 = (1 + 2) / 4 = 3/4
Now that the denominators are the same, add the numerators and keep the common denominator.
5
3/4 is already in simplest form.
The numerator (3) and denominator (4) share no common factors other than 1, so the fraction is simplified.

Answer: 3/4 cup (Option D)

1
Math homework: 2 1/3 hours Science homework: 1 1/2 hours
To find the total time, we need to add the time spent on each subject's homework.

2 + 1 = 3

3

Question 8Grade 8 • NS
A train journey took 3 5/6 hours, and the connecting bus ride took 1 1/4 hours. What was the total travel time, in simplest form?
A. 4 13/12 hours
B. 4 6/10 hours
C. 5 1/12 hours
D. 5 13/12 hours
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Train journey: 3 5/6 hours Bus ride: 1 1/4 hours
To find the total travel time, we need to add the duration of the train journey and the bus ride.
2
3 + 1 = 4
First, add the integer parts of the mixed numbers.
3
Fractions: 5/6 + 1/4 Find a common denominator for 6 and 4. The LCM is 12. Convert fractions: 5/6 = (5 × 2) / (6 × 2) = 10/12 and 1/4 = (1 × 3) / (4 × 3) = 3/12. Add: 10/12 + 3/12 = 13/12
Find a common denominator for the fractions, convert them to equivalent fractions, and then add their numerators. Keep the common denominator.
4
13/12 = 12/12 + 1/12 = 1 1/12
Since the numerator is greater than the denominator, the fraction is improper and represents more than one whole. Convert it to a mixed number.
5
Combined sum = 4 (from whole numbers) + 1 1/12 (from fractions) = 5 1/12 hours
Add the whole number part from the sum of fractions to the initial sum of whole numbers. The fraction 1/12 is in simplest form.
6
Total travel time = 5 1/12 hours
The total travel time is 5 1/12 hours.

Answer: 5 1/12 hours (Option C)

Question 9Grade 8 • NS
The population of a small town increased by 1/10 in January and then by another 3/40 in February. What was the total fractional increase in population over these two months, in simplest form?
A. 4/50
B. 13/40
C. 1/4
D. 7/40
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
January increase: 1/10 February increase: 3/40
To find the total increase, we add the fractional increases from January and February.
2
Denominators are 10 and 40. The least common multiple (LCM) of 10 and 40 is 40.
To add fractions, they must have the same denominator. The LCM is the smallest common denominator.
3
1/10 = (1 × 4) / (10 × 4) = 4/40 3/40 remains 3/40
Multiply the numerator and denominator of 1/10 by 4 to get an equivalent fraction with a denominator of 40.
4
4/40 + 3/40 = (4 + 3) / 40 = 7/40
Now that the denominators are the same, add the numerators and keep the common denominator.
5
7/40 is already in simplest form.
The numerator (7) is a prime number, and 40 is not a multiple of 7, so the fraction is simplified.

Answer: 7/40 (Option D)
