B. $45 + 15 = m$
C. $15 + m = 45$
D. $45 - 15 = m$
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Cost of game = $45
Identify the total cost of the video game.
2
Money Marcus has = $15
Identify the amount of money Marcus currently possesses.
3
Amount needed = m
Identify the unknown variable representing the money Marcus still needs.
4
$15 + m = 45

Question 10Grade 6 • EE
A marathon runner completed a race in $3$ hours and $45$ minutes. If they ran 'r' miles per hour for the first $2$ hours, and $8$ miles per hour for the remaining $1$ hour and $45$ minutes, write an equation to find the total distance of the race in miles.
A. $2r + 8(45) = \text{Total Distance}$
B. $2r + 8(1.75) = \text{Total Distance}$
C. $2r + 8(0.45) = \text{Total Distance}$
D. \(r + 8 = \text{Total Distance}\)
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Time for first part = $2$ hours
Identify the time spent running the first part of the race.
2
Speed for first part = \(r\) mph
Identify the unknown speed for the first part of the race.
3
Remaining time = $3$ hours $45$ minutes - $2$ hours = $1$ hour $45$ minutes
Calculate the remaining time of the race.
4
$1$ hour $45$ minutes = $1 + \frac{45}{60}$ hours = $1.75$ hours
Convert the remaining time to hours.
5
Speed for remaining part = $8$ mph
Identify the speed for the remaining part of the race.
6
Distance for first part = $2r$
Calculate the distance covered in the first part (distance = speed x time).
7
Distance for remaining part = $8 \times 1.75$
Calculate the distance covered in the remaining part.
8
$2r + 8(1.75) = \text{Total Distance}$
Formulate the equation by adding the distances from both parts to get the total distance.

Answer: $2r + 8(1.75) = ext{Total Distance}$ (Option B)
