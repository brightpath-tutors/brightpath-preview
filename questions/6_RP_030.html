Question 1Grade 6 • RP
A 12-ounce bag of pretzels costs $3.60. What is the unit price of the pretzels in dollars per ounce?
A. $3.33 per ounce
B. $0.30 per ounce
C. $0.03 per ounce
D. $4.32 per ounce
E. None of the above
🔒 Backend review only — not visible to students
Step-by-step solution
1
Total cost = $3.60 Total quantity = 12 ounces
To find the unit price, we need to know the total cost and the total quantity.
2
Unit price = Total cost / Total quantity Unit price = $3.60 / 12
The unit price is found by dividing the total cost by the total quantity.
3
$3.60 ÷ 12 = $0.30
Dividing 3.60 by 12 gives 0.30.
4
$0.30 per ounce
The unit price is $0.30 for each ounce of pretzels.

Answer: $0.30 per ounce (Option B)
